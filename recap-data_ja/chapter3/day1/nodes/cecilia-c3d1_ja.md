Immerkind awakens from his slumber and is beset by temporary deafness. The events of Libestal in the past few weeks left him feeling ill. He rushes over to Sister Shiori, where they temporarily [confide in each other away from the other Shining Stars](https://www.youtube.com/live/rjNDjL6R3-s?si=yPP7SM2Bpe7qLNTu&t=1715). The deafness eventually lifted after this conversation.

The Shining Stars were teleported by Fia to the new Libestal, where Knight Immerkind was being recruited by a mysterious Nix. Taken aback by the forwardness of this stranger, he politely declines as [his heart stays true to Fia](https://www.youtube.com/live/rjNDjL6R3-s?si=2-Tgs5fp2S-THkOQ&t=2488).

Immerkind explores the familiar but different city when he encounters Tam Gandr, The Hot Pink One, and Princess Bijou. He greets his fellow Shining Stars, who begin to question why they stay loyal to Fia. Immerkind takes upon his horse and gallops away in dismay.

Gonathon calls Immerkind (see [Cecilia-Gigi](#embed:cecilia-gigi)) who tells him that he has found a treasure that he might like. There, they meet the [ISexy Prophet](https://www.youtube.com/live/rjNDjL6R3-s?si=AfpiGMslXndmPuy3&t=3471), which leaves a critical puzzle for Immerkind that he looks forward to resolving.

Immerkind mused that there can be no way the supplier's job will be the same as before. After all, would it not make sense to choose a path he is familiar with in an unfamiliar world? He accepted the supplier position.

He ventured forth to the fields to complete his jobs and ran into devious stains. His search for lapis lazuli allowed for him to run into many others toiling away in the mines. He learns from Gonathan that the year is 2011, which triggers Immerkind to remember a critical fact.

Immerkind chaperones Bijou back to the kingdom. There, they converse about the future. Despite going on two different paths, the princess and knight continue to hold each other with high regard (see [Bijou-Cecilia: Two Sides of the Same Coin](#embed:bijou-cecilia)).

Upon returning to the city, Immerkind finds moments of fun and merriment with Tam Gandr and Gonathon, where [Immerkind excitedly explains](https://www.youtube.com/live/rjNDjL6R3-s?si=aAcYSQ8wNrPgAgFg&t=6429) the meaning behind his name. The banter continues and ultimately leads to a game of chance where Immerkind wins the cursed hat from Tam.

As Immerkind continues to toil with his work, sister Shiori called and requests his services. They return to the Cliffs, where Shiori must slay several slimes. The sister offers a house for Immerkind to stay in. Immerkind mulls how the [housing situation is crazy](https://www.youtube.com/live/rjNDjL6R3-s?si=wKLSL_fUkZLmLBtY&t=8618) with most houses being held by Mococo and Shiori. Despite being in opposing parties, they agree to stay together in a house, much to Gonathon's dismay, but that won't stop their friendship (see [Shiori-Cecilia: My Roommate is in love with a Marketable Plush]).

Immerkind departs his new dwelling and sets forth to the castle for his [audience with Fia](https://www.youtube.com/live/rjNDjL6R3-s?si=5MgMovwebSk6uiPu&t=9331). Immerkind takes a knee and confides his concerns about the people they are fighting. Fia assuages his concerns, but he still has some reservations due to the people in the castle that he calls impostors.

Immerkind travels to Terminus, where he encounters Tam and Gonathon. They mull upon the architecture and the denizens of the prison, pondering upon its music and the words "[six-seven](https://www.youtube.com/live/rjNDjL6R3-s?si=C5xsjMBB__ld1MD_&t=11077)" muttered by one in a prison cell. Upon [realizing the pain](https://www.youtube.com/live/rjNDjL6R3-s?si=dq2GxEUO9-qmU8fr&t=11391) of Tam Gandr in the letter, Immerkind returns the Stain Plush hat to Tam to help ease their pain, which caused Gonathon much sadness as Tam's memories were erased.

The whiplash of emotional events in the new kingdom left Immerkind exhausted. He picks up his trusty fishing rod and departs to the nearest river, where [HeroIna saves him](https://www.youtube.com/live/rjNDjL6R3-s?si=s-RAuht6_g_T1kqS&t=14439). They discuss the events of the day and how they are adjusting to this new world they found themselves in (see [Cecilia-Ina](#embed:cecilia-ina)). Immerkind was inspired by the toils of the Knight and resolved himself to work harder to keep up.

Immerkind ends the day by continuing to write his thoughts in his diary and his desire to help Fia, but with some reservations.
