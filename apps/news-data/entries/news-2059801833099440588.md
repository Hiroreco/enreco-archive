Chapter 3 - DAY 3 

The heroes brave the dungeon of Cell 0414 and emerge with a Lens of Revealing.  Peering through, they are able to see the path the Outsider took through Terminus, culminating at a dimensional rift. This must be the entrance to Nullspace, but the heroes can’t get through.  How did the Outsider do it?

Faust says they can find out using the Song of Memories sung by the prisoner of Cell 1121.  But to get there, they’ll need an Amulet of Levitation from Star Speaker Maaz.  Some heroes negotiate with the unpleasant Star Speaker, while others sneak into her room to steal it,  but all eventually reach the entrance.
