Chapter3  - DAY1 -

Following a clue from Fia, the heroes track the Outsider back to Libestal. 

There, they are saved by a sly rebel named Nix, and some of the heroes decide to join her crew, despite Fia’s distrust. 

They learn that the Outsider was in Libestal, but left through a dimensional anomaly. 

After earning the approval of Libestal’s leaders, the Star Speakers, the heroes go through the anomaly and enter a vast prison world known as Terminus. 

A prisoner named Faust says he saw the Outsider enter Cell 0502, but the heroes must present a secret to the guards in order to enter. After learning some secrets, the guards let them through.
