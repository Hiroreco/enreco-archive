## Chapter 3 is here!

_May 01, 2026_

### Announcement 🎉

Chapter 3’s schedule has officially been announced! Once again, the ENreco Archive will be updated daily as the event unfolds. After each day ends, recaps will be available on the site at **2 AM JST / 10 AM PDT.**

So keep an eye out for each update! In addition, we’ve spent the past few months working hard on new features—and they’re finally ready.

### New Features

#### BINGO!

So many unexpected things happen in this story, so why not turn it into a game?

You can now create your own ENreco-themed bingo card in the **Bingo tab.** Write your predictions, mark off events as they happen, make a new card each day, or use the preset options we’ve prepared. You can also export and share your card with friends or on social media.

#### Fanart Carousel

You can now view fanart directly at the end of each recap card through the **Fanart Carousel.** More artwork will continue to be added over time, especially throughout Chapter 3!

#### Broken Bonds

Chapter 1, Chapter 2, and now—Chapter 3: Broken Bonds. You can now select Chapter 3 from the navigation bar, where charts and recaps will be updated daily during the event. For now, a placeholder card with the update schedule is available.

#### UI Overhaul

On desktop, the Chapter Recap reader has been updated to a double-spread layout, resembling a book for a more immersive reading experience. The site has also had many other UI improvements.

More features will be released once the event begins, so stay tuned for those as well.
