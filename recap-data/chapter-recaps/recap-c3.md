## Chapter 3: Broken Bonds

### The Present, Opposing Ideals, and a Prisoner

Although the previous journey ended on a grim note and the heroes failed to prevent the Outsider's creation, they did manage to save the Libestal of the past from destruction. But what would await them if they returned to the present day? Was the fantastical kingdom still standing?

To their surprise, it was.

More importantly, the present-day Libestal was where they could finally locate and stop the Outsider.

With a new objective before them, Fia once again leaped through time, bringing the heroes to the kingdom's present era.

Yet the moment they arrived, they were confronted by a mysterious figure named Nix, who presented them with an ultimatum. They could continue placing their trust in Fia and support the side known as the **Shining Stars**, or they could turn their backs on her and join Nix and the rebels fighting under the banner of the **Rebellion**.

![Nix and the two choices](/images-opt/chrecap-2-faction-opt.webp)

According to the people of Libestal, beings from other worlds such as the heroes were known as **Strangers**. They also revealed that another Stranger had appeared in the kingdom before them—possibly the Outsider himself.

Unfortunately, that clue alone was not enough to track him down. To uncover the truth, the heroes would need the help of someone who knew far more about what was happening behind the scenes.

Nix was not the only important figure they encountered. Deep within the kingdom lay a rift that connected Libestal to a realm known as the **Prison at the End of Infinity: Terminus**. There, the heroes met a prisoner named Faust, a man who seemed to possess valuable information that could lead them to the Outsider.

Faust agreed to help them, but only under one condition: once everything was over and the score had been settled, they would fulfill a favor for him in return.

![The heroes meet Faust](/images-opt/chrecap-2-faust-opt.webp)

With the stage now set and new allies and enemies emerging from the shadows, the heroes were ready to begin the next chapter of their adventure.

### Decisions and Advent

The heroes' objectives throughout each day remained largely the same as in their previous adventures: complete the necessary prerequisites, explore and conquer dungeons, recover lost memories, and uncover the mysteries surrounding Libestal.

This time, however, there was a new twist.

At key moments during their journey, the heroes were forced to make important **decisions**. Depending on the choices they made, the path forward could change dramatically. Whether it was deciding whom to trust, which route to take, or how to obtain a crucial item, different choices often led to entirely different experiences and outcomes.

Four dungeons stood in their way, each themed around the memories hidden within:

- **Labyrinth of Knowledge** — revealed the memories of Shiori Novella.
- **Labyrinth of Emotion** — revealed the memories of Koseki Bijou.
- **Labyrinth of Song** — revealed the memories of Nerissa Ravencroft.
- **Labyrinth of Duality** — revealed the memories of Fuwawa Abyssguard and Mococo Abyssguard.

![The heroes tackling the Labyrinth of Song](/images-opt/chrecap-2-dungeon-opt.webp)

The trials awaiting them were more demanding than ever, requiring teamwork, trust, and determination to overcome. Yet no challenge was too great for the heroes. Armed with the bonds they had forged throughout their journey—or, in some cases, the bonds they had broken—they pushed forward regardless.

After all, with their friends by their side, they could overcome anything.

...Probably.

### A Phoenix's Passing, A Mark for What Came After

The heroes certainly did not hold back when it came to the chaos they found themselves involved in. If anything, the shenanigans were amplified tenfold compared to their previous adventures.

It all began with the most shocking event of them all: the murder of Shakira, bringing the orange phoenix's journey to an abrupt end only half a day into the story.

And from that single spark, everything else seemed to spiral outward.

Like a stone cast into still water, the event sent ripples through the lives of everyone around it. Some were directly connected to the tragedy, while others unfolded entirely on their own, yet together they created a chain reaction that would define the days to come.

No one was affected more than the phoenix's wife, Roa. As the days passed, she uncovered secrets and truths she was never meant to learn. With every revelation, her mental state deteriorated further, burdened both by the magnitude of her discoveries and the grief of losing someone she loved.

Her investigations eventually led her to uncover a mysterious cult known as the **Illumination**. Convinced that the world around her was not what it seemed, she desperately tried to warn others about the reality they were living in. Yet most dismissed her words as the ramblings of someone consumed by grief.

![The Illumination](/images-opt/chrecap-2-illumination-opt.webp)

Then there was the phoenix's sister, who sought justice for a murder that many seemed far too willing to move on from. What began as a noble pursuit, however, gradually took a darker turn, culminating in her constructing a literal portal to Hell and demanding sacrifices for a cryptic ritual whose true purpose remained unknown.

And, naturally, there were the ever-complicated relationships between the kingdom's inhabitants.

Thankfully, with the absence of a certain chaotic pink figure, the web of connections was somewhat easier to follow this time. Many relationships simply continued from where their previous stories had left off.

There was a knight desperately trying to reconnect with the queen who no longer remembered him. Another knight swore their blade to a witch, only to find themselves manipulated and used for her own ends. Day by day, the witch's influence grew stronger, while the knight's condition worsened, becoming increasingly frail with each passing sunrise.

As the days went on, the stories intertwined, the secrets deepened, and the people involved became just a little more unhinged.

Or perhaps far more than a little.

![The funeral of Shakira](/images-opt/chrecap-2-shakira-opt.webp)

### A Shorter Adventure, With More Yet to Come

On the final day, the heroes finally learned why some had chosen opposing sides in the first place. Several of their companions had been afflicted by a mysterious **Curse**, one that slowly fractured their bonds and drove wedges between those who once stood together.

Those cursed were the five members of Advent: Nerissa, Shiori, Bijou, Fuwawa, and Mococo.

Determined to restore what had been lost, the heroes gathered for the **Rite of Purification**. The ritual proved successful, lifting the Curse from the five and reuniting them with their friends. With their bonds restored, the heroes set out for their final destination: a realm known as **Nullspace**.

![Rite of Purification](/images-opt/chrecap-2-tag-opt.webp)

Venturing through the strange expanse, they overcame every challenge placed before them and eventually reached the very heart of the realm, where Faust awaited.

There, the truth was finally revealed. Faust was the Outsider.

The mysterious figure explained that everything which had happened thus far had been part of a larger plan. He sought to cultivate the power of **Revelation** through the heroes, allowing him to wield an artifact known as the **Egg of Creation**. With its power, he intended to create an entirely new multiverse—one free from what he viewed as the cruel design of the Origin.

But before a new reality could be born, the current one would have to be erased. Every world. Every history. Every friendship, memory, and life. All would be wiped away so that something entirely new could take its place.

With his intentions laid bare, the Outsider presented the heroes with one final choice:

_Would they help him bring about this new beginning, or reject his vision entirely?_

The heroes each gave their answers. Yet before the Outsider could respond, the confrontation was interrupted by one final twist, as the Curse had survived. Once again it emerged from the shadows, abruptly cutting the conversation short. As the Outsider and the foul being prepared to clash, Fia urgently warned the heroes just as the Looking-Glass Shard activated, whisking them away from Nullspace and into parts unknown.

![The final choice for this journey](/images-opt/chrecap-2-final-opt.webp)

And so ended the third enigmatic journey.

Admittedly, it was a shorter adventure than the two that came before it. Admittedly, its conclusion was far more abrupt as well. Yet despite the unresolved mysteries, the lingering questions, and the countless threads left hanging, it remained a journey filled with chaos, laughter, triumph, heartbreak, and everything in between.

Many stories remained unfinished.

Many answers remained undiscovered.

But perhaps those answers would come in the next journey.

Perhaps the loose threads would finally be tied together.

Because perhaps the next journey... would be the last.
