Nix was the first major citizen the heroes encountered within this new Libestal.

She claimed herself to be a rebel, and it was she who presented the heroes with their first true dilemma in this unfamiliar world: either join her in the "Rebellion," or remain loyal to Fia as part of the "Shining Stars."

Yet Nix was far from an ordinary citizen.

She possessed the ability to rewind time itself, using it to save the heroes from being captured by the kingdom’s guards.

[Nix the Rebel](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2rnclFeQ5EM&t=2685)

Nix explained the existence of "anomalies," openings to other worlds that let beings or objects into this one. When people emerged from anomalies, the locals referred to them as "Strangers," a term spoken with fear and disgust alike.

According to her, the kingdom’s rulers, the Star Speakers, had begun relentlessly hunting down and arresting anyone suspected of being a Stranger. All of it stemmed from the appearance of a single shadow-cloaked Stranger who had somehow entered the kingdom before disappearing into the anomaly above the plaza fountain.

To Fia, the description sounded like the Outsider.

After explaining the state of the world, Nix guided the heroes on what they needed to do next, setting them upon the path that would mark the true beginning of their new journey.
