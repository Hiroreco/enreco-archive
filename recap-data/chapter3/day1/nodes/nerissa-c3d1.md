#### Make Haste, There is No Time to Waste

Nerissa Juliet woke up in the White Room, unsurprised by the strange phenomenon about to unfold yet again. It felt less like an accident now and more like something she had long prepared herself for.

With her memories intact, she recalled briefly her adventures from the past—her [pact](https://youtu.be/To_P9pUvMRA?t=591) with the other heroes, Shiori of the Nyavellas, Biboo, The Fuzzy One, and the Fluffy One, in particular—or as they fondly called it, The Rebellion.

Among them, Nerissa stood out as the conniving one. As if she already had everything planned out.

#### The Promised Illusion

And so it turned out, she was right. [Fia couldn't be trusted at all](https://youtu.be/To_P9pUvMRA?t=992). She weaponized the heroes' ignorance, their kindness, and their empathy to gratify her selfish desires. The destruction of the Old Libestal, the growing chasm between their friendships, all of these were her fault.

Nerissa decided from the very beginning that Fia must be stopped. It didn't matter if she would do it right this time. Her attempt to "save" the world must come to an end at once!

And so as the heroes gathered in the library, she remained observant. Watching everyone's actions and talking with them, to know their hopes and aspirations for this new world.

Then she saw her knight.

Of course! She had to [test](https://youtu.be/To_P9pUvMRA?t=1446) her.

Would she be the loyal knight who swore fealty to her? Or would she eat her words and betray her? There was only one way to know... (see [Liz-Nerissa](#edge:liz-nerissa)).

---

To begin her plan, she must first be familiar with this new realm. The architecture, the ways of the land, the people—all of it seemed familiar. And yet, different.

She took the same job she had in the Ancient World, completed quests, and unraveled secrets that may be the key to stopping Fia and the heroes who believed her lie.

She explored and explored, traveling across the kingdom with haste, thanks to the rebirthed steeds.

As Nerissa gathered more information about this modern Libestal, she stumbled upon Heka, who gave hints on one of the many peculiarities of this world, the anomaly.

Without hesitation, the curious maiden sought to investigate. Her journey led her to a massive prison cell called the Terminus.

She went around, examining each cell in the hopes of finding the mysterious person they were looking for. She ran into other heroes and inquired what they had learned so far. Heeding Shiori's advice, she called Gonathon and asked for a secret.
