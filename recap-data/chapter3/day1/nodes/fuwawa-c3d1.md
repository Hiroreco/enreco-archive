"Bau bau!"

The soft call rang out, filled with wonder and confusion alike. A voice so gentle that simply listening to it felt like being wrapped in a warm, fluffy blanket. Fittingly enough, it belonged to fluff incarnate herself. Fuwawa was once again setting out on a brand new journey.

She [greeted](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YY5EJ_TX-iM&t=351) those around her with the same sweet, gentle demeanor as always, which felt almost unexpected. At the end of the previous journey, she and the other members of “Advent”—the rebels, the ones who stood against the kingdom—had been, for lack of a better word, rather menacing toward everyone else.

But here?

Here she was simply Fuwawa again: the hardworking owner of a small, fluffy [cafe.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YY5EJ_TX-iM&t=430)

Though the rebellious side of her still lingered beneath all the fluff.

When Nix asked which side she intended to join in this new kingdom, Fuwawa immediately chose the [rebels,](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YY5EJ_TX-iM&t=2693) hoping to stand alongside the people she held closest to her heart—Shiori, Mococo, and the others. Last time she had worked as a chef, but this journey called for something [different.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YY5EJ_TX-iM&t=3088)

Perhaps a Smith?

Faction chosen, job decided, and heart overflowing with excitement and curiosity, the fluffy one prepared to venture forth!

Or at least, that was the plan before her steed somehow slipped from her grasp entirely.

[Bau bau! Horsey! Come back!](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YY5EJ_TX-iM&t=3467)

Not exactly the greatest start, but Fuwawa was not deterred. Walking was good cardio, after all.

This new kingdom felt similar to Libestal from her first journey, yet at the same time, it was filled with unfamiliar sights. New quests, new jobs, new regions to explore—Fuwawa approached all of it with endless wonder and glee.

Though one place stood out from the rest.

The prison at the End of Infinity: [Terminus.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YY5EJ_TX-iM&t=6883)

Needless to say, Fuwawa found the place deeply unsettling. Her fluffy demeanor was never suited for somewhere designed to keep others locked behind bars, stripped of freedom. The prison felt impossibly vast, yet suffocatingly small for those trapped within it.

But perhaps the discomfort came from something deeper.

As if somewhere in the back of her mind, she could almost remember what it felt like to be imprisoned herself.

[I don't like this place...](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YY5EJ_TX-iM&t=7368)

After enduring the tasks required there—alongside many [screams](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YY5EJ_TX-iM&t=9545) and a healthy amount of trauma—Fuwawa escaped the prison as quickly as she could, returning instead to the more familiar fun and calm [shenanigans](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YY5EJ_TX-iM&t=10247) the kingdom had to offer.

The new Smith continued tackling task after task, getting [overwhelmed](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YY5EJ_TX-iM&t=11170) many, many, times along the way. It was difficult, especially since she handled most of the work alone, but Fuwawa refused to give up. She simply kept working hard, one step at a time.

Because alongside her determination, there was another thing warming her heart.

A shared [roof,](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YY5EJ_TX-iM&t=12736) kindly offered by Shiori.

And so, after a day that swung wildly between rebellion, confusion, terror, and fluffy calmness, Fuwawa finally called it a night.

From the intimidating rebel seen at the end of the previous chapter, to the soft and comforting fluffy girl everyone knew so well, she remained unmistakably herself in the end.
