Faust introduced himself as a prisoner who had managed to escape his cell within the Prison at the End of Infinity: Terminus, though he still remained trapped within the prison "world" itself.

[Faust the "prisoner"](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2rnclFeQ5EM&t=11420)

He quickly noticed something unusual about the heroes.

Unlike him, they were capable of freely entering and leaving this prison realm, much like the mysterious shadowy figure wandering within it—the very same figure Fia believed to be the Outsider.

Seeing opportunity in this, Faust proposed a deal.

He would act as the heroes’ guide throughout the prison realm and help them search for the shadow-cloaked figure, but in return, they would owe him a favor to be fulfilled sometime in the future.

Neither Fia nor Nix, both speaking through the FiaPhone, trusted him in the slightest. Something about Faust felt suspicious, almost unnervingly so.

Still, the choice was left to the heroes themselves.

Would they accept Faust’s offer, or refuse his deal entirely?

But it seemed this offer was merely a test of the heroes' resolve, and Faust decided to provide his advice anyway, revealing that the mysterious figure had gone to Cell 0502, and explaining how the heroes could gain access as well.
