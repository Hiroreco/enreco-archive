As the curtain rose on another chapter of adventure, Gonathon G miraculously found himself in full possession of his memories from the past chapter, although not all of them were pleasant. The humble knight was not alone, however, he was quickly joined by companions with whom he traded a few words, including Peasant Bae and Tam Gandr. The latter generated some concern for Gonathon, as the azure royal had seemingly forgotten parts of her identity. But Gonathon had little time to dwell on this, as the heroes were soon thrust into a new, but oddly familiar setting.

[A Stranger in a not-so-strange land.](#embed:https://youtu.be/ZSkPr-qXud0?t=2148)

Gonathon was shocked to find himself back in what seemed to be the Libestal of the present, 1000 years after the kingdom he had become accustomed to in his previous adventure. He did not have much time to take in the sights, as he was soon greeted by a strange purple-haired figure. Calling herself Nix, she denounced the ruling Star Speakers and offered Gonathon a place at her side as a member of the Rebellion. Mistrusting of one who seemed to be opposed to what he saw as the side of Justice, Gonathon [flatly refused](https://youtu.be/ZSkPr-qXud0?t=2303).

Alas, as he would soon discover that many of his former companions did not see things the same way as him, including those he called his brother (see [Bae-Gonathon](#edge:bae-gigi)) and even his fellow knight, Lady Bloodflame.

For now though, Gonathon busied himself with the preparations for his new role in the kingdom. Adopting the role of a Smith once more, he was tasked with crafting a number of upgrade stones as his first task.

Upon entering the questing fields, he happened upon his fellow wolfpack member Immerkind. The two were [overjoyed to be reunited](https://youtu.be/ZSkPr-qXud0?t=3410) and talked for a short time. It was then that Gonathon made a surprising discovery: [it was currently the year 2011!](https://youtu.be/ZSkPr-qXud0?t=3623) Little did Gonathon know, this fact would be instrumental in the quests of many others, as throughout the day many people would contact him asking to be told a secret, and each time he would reply with the knowledge of the date.

[The year is 2011…HBO has just debuted Game of Thrones!](#embed:https://youtu.be/ZSkPr-qXud0?t=10023)

Coming upon a group of fellow adventurers, Gonathon was reunited with Mordon Ramsay, his therapist. Following a [cordial chat](https://youtu.be/ZSkPr-qXud0?t=4689) in which the two discussed their favorite eras, Gonathon once again encountered the elusive Tam. It was here that Gonathon learned the true extent of the queen’s trauma: Tam had obtained a stain hat that had seemingly corrupted her memories. (see [Gonathon-Tam](#edge:gigi-kronii)).

Thanks to support from Immerkind, the humble knight was able to wrench the blighted headgear from Tam. Alas, this did not seem to alleviate the azure noble’s woes, and only after Tam agreed to check in with Gonathon did the humble knight feel reassured enough to return to his questing.

[A battle of dice rolls](#embed:https://youtu.be/ZSkPr-qXud0?t=5807)

Having fallen behind in the main quest due to his need to provide support to others, Gonathon hastily made his way to [speak to the Star Speakers](https://youtu.be/ZSkPr-qXud0?t=7554). Despite their rude and condescending attitude towards the knight, he remained committed to assisting Fia in finding the Outsider. It was here that Gonathon was contacted by Nino Ina, and the two reminisced about their days at the academy. The knight further encountered Princess Bijou, and after [a rather strange encounter](https://youtu.be/ZSkPr-qXud0?t=8047), Gonathon promised he would not give up on her.

Finally, Gonathon arrived at the Terminus quest. Seemingly unable to escape the pull of drama, Gonathon was left speechless as Immerkind gave Tam back [the stain hat](https://youtu.be/ZSkPr-qXud0?t=11394), the queen’s memories once more leaving her. Immerkind, realizing her mistake, promised Gonathon that they would find a way to remove the corrupting hat once and for all, and suggested that a new pet would perhaps be the key to it all.

The humble knight’s day ended with a quaint interaction with Roa, one that took a turn for the disturbing as the clumsy feline [killed](https://youtu.be/ZSkPr-qXud0?t=13119) one of the horses they had summoned by accident. Fleeing back to the kingdom’s center, Gonathon retired for the day. While the knight’s first day in this strangely familiar land was not the most eventful, only time will tell what weird and exciting adventures lie in store.
