She knew she couldn't trust that damn cat!!

After the rise of The Outlander, tensions were at an all-time high—and Mococo was no exception to such an atmosphere. Fia's speech was one that would fall on the fuzzy one's deaf ears as she paraded around the library, making sure her [lack of faith in the plushie](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PkPzzhx2hrc&t=1170) was heard loud and clear by all of those around her as they made their journey across the stars... and who better to hear her worries than her beloved sister and protector? (see [Elizabeth-Mococo](#edge:liz-mococo))

Although, despite all her distrust, she still played along with the little memory game that was proposed, focusing on memories that really mattered to her... [the riches... her jewelry...!!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PkPzzhx2hrc&t=2204)

Whether her reminiscence contributed to the success of this maneuver or not, Mococo would soon find herself in yet another new version of the Kingdom of Libestal, much like everybody else. After allying herself with a new face called Nix for the time being, Mococo was told she had to get a job, much like last time. And, well... she really likes jewels! Her answer was clear as day. But before she ventured out to work, there was still a dire problem to be resolved... [HOW COULD SHE BE A LITTLE WITCH WITHOUT A GOOD HAT?!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PkPzzhx2hrc&t=3696)

Phew! With her new hat atop her head, Mococo headed out to take care of business—mining a lot of ore, squabbling with others, crafting some gems, getting herself lost... that was, until she found herself in a street filled with empty houses. A brilliant idea came to mind... she could become a homeowner!! All it takes is changing the words on a couple of signs, apparently, which means she was now the proud owner of not one, not two, but THREE different houses!

[Three Houses... quite emblematic, don't you think?](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PkPzzhx2hrc&t=6499)

It wasn't long after her acquisitions that chaos would quickly unfold, as Mococo heard simply terrible news... Shakira had been [KILLED!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PkPzzhx2hrc&t=6851) She'd hear wails of grief and accusations being thrown all around her... even if Mococo herself fairly quickly moved back to her quests and escapades. She arranged proper housing for her trusted ones before having a meeting with the Star Speakers, where she... technically tells them the truth about herself! [YIPPEE, LOOPHOLES!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PkPzzhx2hrc&t=8472)

Departing from the castle after getting lost in it again, Mococo would take some time exploring the rebellion's hideout, being very interested in their supply gathering quests. She had found her calling!! For TREASURE!! From then on, it wouldn't take long for her to discover the prison of Terminus. She was... pretty casual about this too, honestly. She happily spoke with the locals and unlocked access to the Labyrinth of Knowledge... [even if she wasn't supposed to be there yet.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PkPzzhx2hrc&t=10750) Oops!

With others around her settling down for the night, Libestal's little witch decided to follow suit inside her personal comfy abode. There was a lot on her mind, but as she gazed at the shining light of the sunset beaming in from outside the window... she hoped to see more of that light when she woke up.

And if she didn't, she could make her own! She was still both the only Jeweler _and_ Real Estate Agent in the kingdom, after all.
