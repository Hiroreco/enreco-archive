There was a lot of distrust in the party of heroes as they flew forth toward a new Libestal. Some were still shaken by what had happened just prior; some didn't trust Fia and her words of guidance; and some distrusted the others among them, trying to get ahold of themselves in the madness.

And then, standing by herself in the corner of the Library, there was Mordon Ramsay—[someone who just wanted to cook.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jWrom0fcdQk&t=2381)

Of course, she wasn't going to abandon the heroics; there were still worlds to save, after all. But she'd be lying if she said she's all that invested in the drama and discourse flying around. If someone's gonna be able to make a difference, it's with some good flippin' food. After picking up her previous job as a Chef where it had left off, Mordon quickly got to work gathering ingredients for her to make her meals, already planning to rebuild her restaurant yet again once it was possible.

Before that idea could come to fruition, though, Chef Ramsay was approached by her good friends, Tanakisha Karia—more widely known as Shakira—and Roa Pandora, and they had a special request for her: the couple wished for a ["fertility cake"](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jWrom0fcdQk&t=2080) to be baked in honor of their recent marriage, as they'd both wanted Shakira to bear a child, much like how the Hot Pink One had one recently. Mordon smiled—she was happy to hear it! Both for the lovely couple, and for the fact that her work was being appreciated.

She got to work right away, and making a chocolate cake really was no cinch at all. The trickiest part was adding the "fertility" aspect under the limited resources, but [her friends of the Wolfpack](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jWrom0fcdQk&t=5440) helped guarantee she had just the right spices to make this work. Once the cake was finished, she'd placed it in a safe chest and told Shakira of its whereabouts. Her first dish of the new world had been created, and she couldn't wait to see how the seeds of her effort would bear fruit.

...those seeds sprouted faster than she could have ever expected.

[_Talk about a fall from grace..._](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jWrom0fcdQk&t=7142)

Shakira was dead...?! _Dead_ dead?! How?! Complete and utter disbelief engulfed Mordon's expression as Roa's wails of agony filled the air. Her mind was racing. Shakira was one of the most resilient around—did she have a chili allergy or something?!

Whatever it was, one of her closest friends throughout this journey was now _gone,_ and she was left to deal with the ensuing thunderstorm.

“The cake was poisoned”, they claimed! She thought this was likely too, but the madness only escalated when others started pointing fingers at the most likely suspect... Mordon Ramsay herself. She tried to defend herself, but she couldn't get even a single word out. Caught in the middle of far too much for her to handle at once, Mordon Ramsay fled the scene, [screaming her anguish to the skies.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jWrom0fcdQk&t=7522)

Residing in the calmer atmosphere of the wilds, Mordon attempted to push through her [broken will](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jWrom0fcdQk&t=8222), retracing her steps and vowing to find the true culprit of Shakira's murder—no matter what it may take. It wouldn't be easy, as for the rest of the day, almost everyone she came across pushed her away out of fear, anger, [or whatever the hell Tam Gandr was on about](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jWrom0fcdQk&t=11356), but she wouldn't stop until she could avenge her fallen friend.

Though she wouldn't be completely alone in this quest. Both NinoIna and Gonathon promised to aid in clearing her name, as well as [the widowed Roa](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jWrom0fcdQk&t=14498), who was willing to hear out the other side of the story, now that she was able to think more rationally.

After splitting off from her, Mordon begins to finally build her restaurant before calling it a night. The road she made is paved with good intentions, and while she can't say she's all too excited to find out where her journey's going to end up leading her... she hopes to be able to help more people than she's harmed once it's all said and done.

["Good food will save the world... even if it's not my good food."](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jWrom0fcdQk&t=10268)
