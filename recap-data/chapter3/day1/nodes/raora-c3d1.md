Roa. Just Roa now. No Pandora, no Panthera, no fancy last name, no nothing. Simple, clean, easy to remember. That was all she needed as the big pink cat pounced her way into her third enigmatic journey.

It was only the first day, yet she could already tell it was going to be a fun one. Just look at all the people around her—familiar faces, cute and pretty ones, and especially the ones she had grown very fond of.

Miss Tim, or Tom, or Tem, or whatever, was here once again for her to endlessly tease (see [Tam-Roa](#edge:kronii-raora)). Then there was her hot pink master, who always petted and fed her sensually whenever they met (see [Hot Pink One-Roa](#edge:irys-raora)). And of course, there were all the others around her who found her adorable and loved her dearly.

She was a hard [worker](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XD-K3AirOdA&t=3630), choosing to become a Smith and even joining the good guys, the [Shining Stars](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XD-K3AirOdA&t=2730). Well... before she eventually decided to [switch sides](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XD-K3AirOdA&t=9065) later on just because.

She took on quests, grinded endlessly, explored the kingdom, and spent her days getting into all sorts of fun with the other heroes. It was shaping up to be a wonderful time for Roa.

[Horses! Shenanigans! Fun!](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XD-K3AirOdA&t=13290)

But above all else, the highlight of her days was always the love of her life, the one who had pulled her from the depths of despair and helped shape her into the loving and lively cat she was now. Shakira, her wife.

Shakira cared for her. Shakira loved her. Shakira gave her what no one else could.

And Roa loved Shakira in return.

Everything felt perfect. This new kingdom, this new life—it all felt like pure bliss.

...

...

Until it all crumbled away when she least expected it (see [Shakira-Roa](#edge:kiara-raora))
