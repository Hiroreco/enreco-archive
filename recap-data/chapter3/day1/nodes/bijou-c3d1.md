Everyone else besides her fellow members of Advent had become her enemies. And Fia was no longer to be trusted. When asked to [choose her name](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VuwvH2nOWxI&t=680), Bijou accepted the stain within her heart. She was now Bijou the Stained.

Returning to the library, Bijou began reminiscing with the others about their last time in Libestal. There, she met once more with her Wooden Shovel Sister, NinoIna, their relationship becoming shaky once more. (See: [Bijou-Ina](#edge:bijou-ina))

In order to find Libestal in the present, the group needed to focus on their fond memories of Libestal in the past. Bijou finds, however, that she does not have any such memories. All she remembers is [chaos, pain, and darkness](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VuwvH2nOWxI&t=2083).

Once rescued from the guards by Nix, Bijou's attention had immediately been caught. When Nix asks if Bijou wants to join their crew, Bijou immediately decides to...

[Join the Rebellion!](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VuwvH2nOWxI&t=2515)

Being guided to get a job once more, Bijou decides she will not become a jeweler as she had before. She felt unappreciated for all the work she did for others, so instead she chose to work only for herself, as a smith. Bijou also then chooses to [accept her destiny](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VuwvH2nOWxI&t=3150) by claiming her Stain hat once more.

Needing some iron to begin her smithing work, Bijou heads out to the fields alongside Mordon Ramsay. While traversing, Bijou hears from Mordon about the fertility cake she is making for Shakira and Roa. Upon her return from the field, Bijou begins looking for this cake herself. Finding the cake in the fridge Mordon left it in, Bijou [stains the cake](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VuwvH2nOWxI&t=5975), swearing not to speak of this to anyone.

Present when Shakira consumes the cake, Bijou internally goads Shakira to eat it, while still feigning concern as Shakira perishes. Following the accusations of Mordon for having baked the cake, Bijou is pleased to note that her plan to make the others perish is working.

["...They will all fall by my hand."](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VuwvH2nOWxI&t=6993)

When asked by the Star Seekers what it is she wants, Bijou "lies", stating she wants power in order to kill the others. Expectedly, they do not believe her, for this is a preposterous claim. They do still recognize her as a Starborn regardless.

Despite not going to the Guild as the Star Seekers instructed, Bijou still goes to investigate the Anomaly, finding Terminus on the other side. For some reason, she finds herself uncomfortable with the mention of a [prison](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VuwvH2nOWxI&t=9170). Regardless, she presses onwards, meeting with Faust and accepting his deal, for he can be of use to her.

Finding she can progress no further, Bijou decides that it is time to work on further increasing her power, as well as find a place to take up residence. Luckily, Shiori comes along to aid with both, with the two going on an adventure to gather materials to strengthen themselves, and with Shiori inviting Bijou to live with her and Immerkind. Bijou is hesitant at first, believing she does not need anyone else to live with, but when she is unable to acquire a house of her own, she accepts Shiori's offer.

Upon [discovering Mordon Ramsay's restaurant](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VuwvH2nOWxI&t=14730), Bijou realizes that getting Mordon on her side may be beneficial. Bijou thus offers to help Mordon clear her name by taking on the Dungeon together the next day, to which Mordon accepts. Knowing that Mordon is also investigating the true cause of Shakira's death, Bijou also hints to Mordon that the cake could have possibly been poisoned when left in the fridge, knowing that is exactly what she did to it.
