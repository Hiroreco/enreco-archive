Once again finding herself inside the white box, NinoIna found herself riddled with emotion: intrigued, confused, and possibly most daring of all: [hungry.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cLbDjefl2-w&t=143s)

It was surely fate then that the first face Ina saw was that of her own Consort, the Hot Pink One. (see [Ina-Hot Pink One](#edge:ina-irys))

After a “brief” visit to Fia—involving the usual shenanigans—the heroes were thrust back into Libestal, before immediately getting into trouble with some guards—typical.

But before she could get apprehended, Ina was quickly saved by a pink-haired Libestan. The mysterious fellow revealed herself to be Nix, a member of a so-called "Rebellion," who informed Ina that they were open to welcoming her into their ranks. Despite the tempting offer, and after a lengthy consideration through the voices in her head, Ina believed Fia's words true and honest over Nix, deciding to stay alongside her as a [shining star.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cLbDjefl2-w&t=2475s)

Nix let Ina go off on her own way, with Ina turning her attention towards the job selection that Nix mentioned. With her previous occupation as a jeweler, Ina considered something novel. Each job listing had its own intrigues, its own...

[Did I just see "fishing"?](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cLbDjefl2-w&t=2835s)

With her mind promptly made up, Ina chose the path of the supplier and began her tasks dutifully. During this, Ina bonded with Gonathon over their shared experience at the academy. Their bond was strong, even surprising Elizabeth, who seemed to... [misunderstand their pleasantries.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cLbDjefl2-w&t=4450s)

After the lengthy process of obtaining an iron pickaxe, Ina set her sights on the castle that housed those whom Nix called the "star speakers". These four folk were intrigued by Ina's story and attire, claiming that the monacre of the 'warrior of the stars' had not been heard of in a _thousand years_, and dubbing Ninom- I mean, NinoIna, a ["starborn"](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cLbDjefl2-w&t=5796s), tasked her with investigating a rift by the fountain.

Despite the title, status, and quest, NinoIna seemed to be leading towards a peaceful life this time around. One devoid of gachas and drama alike. One where she could breeze through the day without much more than the thought of worry; the other heroes, unfortunately, [didn't get the memo.](www.youtube.com/watch?v=cLbDjefl2-w&t=6335s)

Nevertheless, Ina found her way through the day’s trials and ended up at a door that... wouldn't open. 

[That's it?](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cLbDjefl2-w&t=8382s)

It seemed that, for now, her quest of the day had come to a close, and she had some time to kill. Her leisure began with the goal of obtaining a home of her own. There were plenty of houses around anyway; there's no chance they're all bought out, [right?](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cLbDjefl2-w&t=8908s)

Well, that doesn't matter; a lack of available housing didn't stop her in the past, and it wasn't about to stop her now. And so, she gathered up her materials and got to work, making a humble little shack by a lake. It wasn’t much, but as the hero herself said, "I don't need three houses to myself."

Onto her next task: fishing. She understood the basics just enough: grab some bait, cast your rod, and the fish will bite eventually. Plain and simple, as Ina liked it. Unfortunately for Ina, a special challenge reared its ugly head: _the ’river prince’_.

It began with passerbys inquiring Ina about this fish, leading her to try to find it. It wasn't until she met up with Immerkind that she was informed of its elusiveness: not _only_ was the river prince exclusively caught at night, but you also had to use a specific type of bait, or else you were out of luck.

It was a challenge, and after many sleepless nights and _forty_ whole minutes of fishing...

[The river prince was real!](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cLbDjefl2-w&t=17426s)
