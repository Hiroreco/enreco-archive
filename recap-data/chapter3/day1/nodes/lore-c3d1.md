#### Main Story

**Settings**

After failing to stop the Outsider from coming into existence, Fia was at a loss for what to do. Then, she found a clue in the book of poetry the Outsider gave her: he could be found "back where it all began."

With the heroes' help, she manages to navigate the Library back to present-day Libestal, which somehow escaped its fated destruction and is now drifting in the far reaches of the multiverse.

**Factions**

When the heroes enter Libestal, they find that a lot has changed. The kingdom is threatened by dimensional rifts known as anomalies, and dangerous otherworldly visitors known as Strangers.

Suspected of being Strangers themselves, they're accosted by guards, but rescued by a local rebel named Nix, who seems to have unusual abilities.

Nix then gives them a choice: either join her gang, the Rebellion, or stay with Fia and the Shining Stars.

#### Discoveries

They learn that a mysterious figure resembling the Outsider was sighted in Libestal, but entered one of the dimensional anomalies and disappeared.

After completing some quests to earn the trust of Libestal's rulers, the heroes investigate the anomaly and are able to enter it using their power of Revelation.
The anomaly takes them to the Prison at the End of Infinity: **Terminus.**

They meet a peculiar prisoner named Faust, who offers to guide them. Faust has also seen someone resembling the Outsider and says they entered Cell 0502 to speak with the **Prisoner of Knowledge,** who may be able to give them information on their next step.
