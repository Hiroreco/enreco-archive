## Day 1: The Shining Stars and The Rebellions

We’ve been to a fabricated Libestal, journeyed through the ancient real Libestal, and even managed to save that ancient kingdom, though the ending was far from kind to the people involved. Still, the kingdom was technically saved nonetheless. So what would that kingdom look like now, after returning to the present? That is the setting for this third enigmatic journey.

Libestal still stands, alive and thriving, but the very threat everyone fought so desperately to stop in the previous journey now roams free: the Outsider. Somehow, he too has reached the present-day Libestal. And so, Fia’s new objective became clear: find the Outsider here, and once and for all prevent the destruction of the universe with the heroes’ help.

But conflict had already begun to take root. By now, some of the heroes had started questioning Fia’s judgement, and several chose to turn their backs on her entirely. Those who remained loyal became known as the “Shining Stars,” while those who abandoned her became the “Rebellion.”

Who is right? Who is wrong? No one truly knows. But one thing everyone _does_ know is that chaos on the very first day is practically inevitable at this point.

![The heroes choosing who to side](/images-opt/factions-opt.webp)

Interestingly enough, everyone seemed to retain their memories from the previous journey this time around. No sudden “hit on the head and wake up with amnesia” nonsense anymore. Because of that, things had changed. Some relationships had healed, while others remained just as bitter and hateful as before. Yet even then, things were far from predictable. New conflicts emerged from bonds once thought unbreakable, and unexpected choices were made by the very people no one imagined would make them. No one could truly tell how any of this would end.

And guess what? Someone’s pregnant. Yes, again.

Oh, that doesn’t impress you yet? Fine, let me make it better.

The baby is dead.

Still not impressed? Seen this already with the pink woman who somehow gave birth to gas in the last chapter?

Well... this time, the mother is dead too! Gone. Poof. Murdered. Vanished forever alongside the goo goo ga ga.

Now _that’s_ drama.

![There wasn't even a birth party this time](/images-opt/kiara-dead-opt.webp)

They still have the exact same job system as before, but who caaaaares about any of that when there’s fresh tea and absurdly spicy drama waiting to be devoured instead?
