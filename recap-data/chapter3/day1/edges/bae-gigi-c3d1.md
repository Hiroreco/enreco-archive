In another universe, Gonathan would have been happy to see his brother find love. To see her move on from Immerkind to a happier and healthier relationship would have been great.

[But, not to _Nix_.](#embed:https://youtu.be/ZwqLwWTa98E?t=3246)

Dishonorable, trickster.

Gonathan fears that Nix would only corrupt The Peasant, turning her away from the righteous path.

However, to Bae...

Nix is one who offers an escape from the painful reality she lives in. From being a Hero. From the Heartbreak.

But despite this disagreement, the two are deeply bonded. Not even romance can break their Bromance.

Gonathan believes in the bond he and The Peasant Bae have. That Bae can be the bigger person who may inspire Immerkind to follow her.
