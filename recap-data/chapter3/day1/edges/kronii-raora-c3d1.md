She was Tam. Just Tam. She wanted her name to stay that way this [time](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sT4tuLP6UJQ&t=360)—simple, elegant. No more confusion, no more people making fun of the previous title she once carried. Just three letters, short and concise.

And yet somehow, somehow, she still ended up at the center of a joke that had persisted for what felt like an entire decade.

[ITS TAMMMMM!!!](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sT4tuLP6UJQ&t=3493)

Tam never thought a cat—a pink one, at that—could get on her nerves this much. She removed her last name *specifically* so this woman would stop butchering it into whatever cursed collection of vowels and syllables she could come up with. Tim. Tom. Tum. Tem. Might as well get all of them out of the way at this point. It was literally [spelled out](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sT4tuLP6UJQ&t=3690) character by character, for god’s sake!

Was this some kind of cruel cosmic joke? Like beating a dead horse over and over again just so the universe could squeeze out one pathetic chuckle?

You know what? Fine. Tam could play that game too.

If you’re gonna do it to her, she can do it right back.

[Ahh, Rora. Rao? Ro.](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sT4tuLP6UJQ&t=3779)

As Roa repeatedly asked Tam to say her name properly, Tam simply ignored her. Never before had the phrase "a taste of one’s own medicine" felt so fitting, because the pink cat was slowly [losing her mind](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sT4tuLP6UJQ&t=3894) over it as well. It was sweet sweet [payback.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sT4tuLP6UJQ&t=4370)

However, for reasons unknown, Tam seemed to attract pink beings into her life like a curse. Just like the previous journey, first there was the pink "one", and now there was a pink "cat" making themselves a blight upon her day.

The three of them even managed to "reunite" later that same day, though "reunion" was a generous term. It was mostly just yelling.

[Surrounded by pinks](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sT4tuLP6UJQ&t=7623)

They told Tam to "reminisce" (force her to relive the past), tried to “mend the damage” (manipulate her into becoming their plaything again), and generally made the entire ordeal exhausting. Did anything actually get resolved in the end?

Mmm... meh.

Still, Tam and Roa—or rather, "Ro," as Tam insisted on calling her—did seem *somewhat* closer afterward. Closer in the sense that they could finally say each other’s names without immediately turning it into a joke.

Their [past](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sT4tuLP6UJQ&t=9766) had certainly been full of turmoil, but perhaps... perhaps things might change this time.

The two explored the kingdom together afterward. Roa even asked Tam to [adopt](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sT4tuLP6UJQ&t=11603) her for some reason, seemingly trying to replace Tam’s actual pet—the Stain perched atop her head—to which Tam politely declined.

Still, things were slowly beginning to look up.

After all, Roa was the one who stayed behind to see Tam off at the very end of the day.
