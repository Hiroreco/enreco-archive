Shiori Novella and Cecilia Immerkind may not have had the tightest bonds from prior chapters, but it doesn't stop them from inquiring about each [other's health.](https://youtu.be/ZzXAmkDHgTY?t=1815)

Knight Immerkind was worried for a moment that Shiori was evil, but was relieved to see that the curse was also truly lifted from Shiori.

Later on, Shiori would call upon Immerkind's help to slay stains from outside the castle walls. Immerkind, of course, agreed to help.

Friendship blossomed through battle, and soon enough, Shiori invited [Immerkind to move with her.](https://youtu.be/ZzXAmkDHgTY?t=8959) Though there was a difference in philosophy as Immerkind would always be faithful to her Fia, and Shiori had chosen to rebel, but Immerkind states that she doesn't think Shiori is evil and that as long as Shiori herself doesn't hate Fia and will not seek to harm her, then there's no reason for bad blood between them.
