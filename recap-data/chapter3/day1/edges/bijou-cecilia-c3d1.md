As she traverses the city, she encounters the Princess Bijou who exchanges pleasant greetings. But this pleasantry devolves into dismay as Princess Bijou tells The Knight that [Fia has been lying](https://www.youtube.com/live/rjNDjL6R3-s?t=3090) to her as she is attuned to emotions.

This is one of many attempts by Bijou to convince Immerkind to disavow her loyalty to Fia.

Despite this, the two maintain the bonds of Princess and Knight. Immerkind [guides Bijou](https://www.youtube.com/live/rjNDjL6R3-s?t=5685) back to the kingdom and converse about the future. While they disagree with where the path to the future is, and Bijou questions Immerkind's loyalty to Fia out of concern, they both realize they are two sides of the same coin.

But that does not stop Bijou from continually reminding Immerkind to move on from Fia, much to Immerkind's dismay.
