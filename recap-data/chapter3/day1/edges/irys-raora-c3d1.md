Pink was already a really, _really_ vibrant color, so combining a BEEG pink with a HOT pink only resulted in... an even bigger, hotter pink or something along those lines.

The point was that these two had shared an incredibly close relationship for the longest time. The questionable kind, where one was practically treated like a pet cat that got fed mouth-to-mouth by her master.

And it seemed nothing had changed this time around.

[Meow meow meow](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=M1lDsivuVb8&t=1146)

It was honestly difficult to imagine anything breaking the bond between them. The hot pink had chosen to become a chef again solely so she could [feed](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=M1lDsivuVb8&t=3516) her beloved cat. She helped Roa mourn the death of her wife with all the care she could possibly offer, tending to her emotional needs at every opportunity.

What more could anyone even ask for?

[Who dare hurt my cat](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=M1lDsivuVb8&t=7815)

And Roa returned that devotion in her own way. Aside from emotionally _appealing_ to the pink one in all the positive ways possible, she also eagerly assisted her master in emotionally _attacking_ others in the most negative ways possible.

Which was... probably a good thing, yeah?
