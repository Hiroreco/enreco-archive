Though times change, bonds between wolves do not.

Gonathon called Immerkind, who excitedly said that he had found a treasure for her. There, they meet the [ISexy Prophet](https://www.youtube.com/live/rjNDjL6R3-s?si=AfpiGMslXndmPuy3&t=3471), which leaves a critical puzzle for Immerkind. Gonathon is aware of how much Immerkind enjoys riddles, so his immediate thought was to call his trusted friend.

As the heroes toiled in the mines (again), Gonathon discovered that they were actually in 2011 and excitedly told Immerkind. Upon hearing this, Immerkind realizes that this is the year in which the hit series Game of Thrones premiered on HBO!

[2011: The Time HBO premiered Game of Thrones!](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/rjNDjL6R3-s?si=SuY2XwGOfFzgcEvQ&t=4948)

Despite the new world and craziness of everything occurring, Immerkind and Gonathon find joy in playfully bantering with Tam. Gonathon explains the mystical d20 rolls where [Tam consented to wagering her stain hat](https://www.youtube.com/live/rjNDjL6R3-s?si=iZ7TDUjou79MOONv&t=6604) for a game of chance. Taking a chance, Immerkind hits an 18 to Tam's 7 and wins the cursed hat.

Bonds between wolves are difficult to break, even as Immerkind moves in with SisterShiori, Gonathon does not fear, for Immerkind has eyes upon Ophenia.

However, bonds were tested in the prison of Terminus. Immerkind was moved by Tam's letter, which described a pain and desperation his heart could not handle. Gonathon was left speechless as Immerkind gave Tam's hat back, and the queen’s memories immediately dissipated. Seeing this dismay in Gonathon's eyes, Immerkind apologizes for his actions, as they were in the best interest of alleviating Tam Gandr's pain. They promised each other to slay the beast that resides upon Tam's head.

[The Stain Hat](#embed:https://youtu.be/ZSkPr-qXud0?t=11394)

This is the bond that cannot be broken. But it may bend from time to time, but that is okay. True friendships will defy the outside forces of chaos.
