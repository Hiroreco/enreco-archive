["You feeling okay, sister?"](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2rnclFeQ5EM&t=1779)

Despite how overwhelmed Elizabeth was with the situation at hand, she knew she had to be strong for the people she wanted to protect. Weaving through the sensory overload surrounding the cosmic library at that point, she eventually found her beloved sister from Libestal's past—and was quick to comfort her when she did. Yay, headpats!!! :D

The two began to talk, beginning with their thoughts on Fia. Mococo very much didn't trust her, warning her sister not to be deceived by her words. Lady Bloodflame nodded in understanding—she was still hesitant in the back of her mind about a lot of things, but with her sister and Nerissa now both distrusting their plushie caretaker, it was hard to even imagine turning a blind eye.

Yet, despite all this uncertainty... she couldn't help but smile. Mococo was a little bundle of sunshine that wouldn't sit still, and her antics always resulted in [a giggle or two](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PkPzzhx2hrc&t=1803) escaping her lips. And to the fuzzy one, her sister was a sense of safety in this cruel world... someone to share her food and riches with, someone to rely on. They were true found family... when they spoke, the stars felt so close, even when they were so far. Even when memories risked fading, when worlds keep changing, when more and more things and people get left behind... together, they could recollect what mattered, all while treasuring the present.

For as long as she drew breath, Lady Bloodflame swore by her sword that she would always protect Little Witch Mococo.

["...Little... Witch...?"](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2rnclFeQ5EM&t=2165s)

She... had heard about a coven's creation, but to think that her own little sister would be part of such a group? That was... shocking to hear, to say the least. Nonetheless, she put her trust in Mococo. She'd just have to keep an eye out to ensure she didn't get hurt.

The sisters would cross paths a couple more times throughout the day. They discussed their jobs and allegiances together with Nerissa, where Lady Bloodflame's hesitance to trust Fia due to her faith in her companions was clear, and then they may have also [peer pressured](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2rnclFeQ5EM&t=3559s) her into becoming a supplier instead of a smith, like she'd have liked to be. Oops.

After that, the little witch and her knight would cross paths again shortly after the tragedy of Shakira, with the latter being interested in the supposed land her sister had acquired. Mococo was more than happy to [tell her all about it](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PkPzzhx2hrc&t=7184), and even handed the deed to one of her houses to her! They were fairly empty on the inside at the moment, but as siblings and now neighbours, it was nothing they couldn't furnish eventually. Lady Bloodflame was also the one to help Mococo acquire a secret for the Constant by being the one to point her towards Gonathon before they both headed to rest for the day.

There was no doubt in Elizabeth's mind that the days ahead would be incredibly hectic, but there was nothing to worry about. As long as she was there to make sure her little sister was happy and safe... it would mean it was all worth it.
