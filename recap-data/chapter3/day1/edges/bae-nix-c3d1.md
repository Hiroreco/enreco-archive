Beautiful purple hair, a chunni outfit that screams "I'm edgy", and a reckless, rogue-ish attitude towards the world.

Though Bae's heart still hurts from Immerkind's rejection, another woman stole her eye. Sweeping in like a hero from legends, Nix saved Bae from the suspicious Libestal guards. Giving the Peasant her number while she was at it.

But more than that, Nix had given Bae an [opportunity to escape.](https://youtu.be/ZwqLwWTa98E?t=2670)

Escape from the consequences of being a hero.

Escape the pain of a rejected love.

Her brother, Gonathon, worries it might be a rebound, but for now, The Peasant Bae is too enraptured by her purple beauty to agree.
