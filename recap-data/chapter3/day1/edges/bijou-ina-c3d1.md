Their relationship started out rocky—haha, get it? Rock.

These two were, and still are, sisters. They had already gone through a major conflict during the first journey, where one had to save the other from corruption. Now, that same scenario had presented itself once again. With the gem princess's heart now consumed by darkness, the two spoke about themselves, about the present, and about what might occur in the future.

[Sister... The darkness](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VuwvH2nOWxI&t=1023)

Once more, they stood on opposing sides with clashing ideals. The one shrouded in darkness declared that she would not hesitate, and that she would win should they ever face each other again. Meanwhile, the other swore that she would bring her dear sister back to the light—and she truly did try whenever she got the chance, even going as far as offering to share a roof together.

Despite all of that, Bijou declined. She needed no company from anyone. She was fine alone. By herself.

[It's fine, sister.](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VuwvH2nOWxI&t=10845)

But despite everything, she still cared for her sister. Their ideals simply stood on opposite sides. Perhaps, even if she would not stay, she would still visit her sister's house every now and then.
