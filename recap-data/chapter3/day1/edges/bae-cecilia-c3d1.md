One can never forget her first love.

This was not a lesson the Peasant learned from Mother but it was one she cannot help but grasp as she gazed from afar, eyes watching Cecilia Immerkind with [painful longing.](https://youtu.be/ZwqLwWTa98E?t=1836)

Despite her words prior, swearing from all matters of the heart, The Peasant can't help but feel these painful heartaches once again. From what was once fond friendship between a peasant and knight only turned to tense air between what might as well be strangers.

Whenever she tries, the words get stuck in her throat and [Bae quickly runs away.](https://youtu.be/ZwqLwWTa98E?t=1353)

Will Bae be ever to converse with Immerkind again? For now we don't know. Only that while Bae looks upon Cecilia with wistful regret, another has caught her eye.
