Do you believe in the red string of fate? That no matter what, you always have a special connection to someone else?

Despite Shiori's missing memories of Gonathan, only missing to pay the price for his memories of his dearly loved Pearl, a connection seemed to stay between the two of them.

Rather, it seems that Gonathan is intent on keeping Shiori within his sights.

How much he worried about Sister Shiori's intention to stay with the roguish rebels and her signing a contract to stay with Nix. How he offered to break that contract for her. 

How he revealed a painful [secret](https://youtu.be/ZzXAmkDHgTY?t=5506) that, due to an unfortunate twist of events, Shiori will never truly understand.

That he, who once swore to be righteous, had hurt someone he loves, and how Shiori will never remember what he did. 

Shiori, of course, didn't understand his pain, only confused and confounded that Gonathan would let someone be homeless without doing anything.

Gonathan's turmoil will only increase when he witnesses his beloved [friend move in with Shiori,](https://youtu.be/ZzXAmkDHgTY?t=10864) questioning whether Shiori knew of Immerkind's deep devotion to Fia.

Perhaps these are shackles of a broken bond weighing them down.
