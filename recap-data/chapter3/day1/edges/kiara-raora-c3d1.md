Shakira and Roa had been more than happy to see each other again. The cat and the phoenix walked hand in hand, two devoted lovers finally reunited. It had felt like forever since they had last touched each other, even if their separation had only been brief.

That feeling had only been [amplified](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XD-K3AirOdA&t=4460) by the fact that they were in the middle of their honeymoon. Having gotten married only a few days prior, the two sought to continue their lives together in peace. This was especially true for Shakira, who desperately wanted to start a family with Roa. Their previous attempts at having Roa bear a child had failed, but Shakira knew better than to give up.

And, for once, her determination seemed to be rewarded.

Offers of counseling and a cake that supposedly guaranteed childbirth?

Perhaps this really could have worked out.

Slowly, the two became accustomed to their new life together. Roa had even been inspired to become a smith just like her wife. They rode their horses freely through the wind, took on their first quests together, and embraced every little moment they could share.

Everything seemed well.

Everything _had_ been going well.

[I love you](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XD-K3AirOdA&t=6120)

...

She met Shiori and Mordan Ramsay, who offered their services regarding a delicacy known as the [**Fertility Cake**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XD-K3AirOdA&t=1520)—a chocolate cake said to guarantee the future birth of a child.

The married couple opened the box and carefully took the cake out. There appeared to be something else inside besides the cake itself, though Roa had been far too excited by the moment to notice what it was.

Roa suggested that a cake like this deserved to be eaten somewhere special. Together, they decided that the perfect place would be atop the fountain of Libestal.

As they climbed onto the fountain, several people gathered below to witness the occasion. Roa watched lovingly as her wife took a bite of the Fertility Cake.

Then, within a single fleeting moment, Shakira collapsed.

[My... wife?](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XD-K3AirOdA&t=6590)

Roa frantically rushed forward and tried to catch her, but she failed. The mighty phoenix fell straight into the freezing water at the bottom of the fountain.

Panicking, Roa immediately jumped in after her and desperately tried to pull her wife back out. She did everything she could to save her, even begging the people nearby for help, but no one could do anything except watch as the fire in Shakira's eyes slowly faded away.

Roa cried until her eyes burned. Roa stepped away for a moment to gather herself. The beeg cat felt so unbearably small as she stumbled into a nearby alleyway. Her hands trembled violently, and she could barely even stand anymore.

People from all around town came to check on her. Immerkind eventually found Roa and gently asked how she was doing.

She tried to console Roa, reminding her of how she brought color and life to the people around her.

Roa paused for only a moment before slowly standing upright once more.

Then she told her the very same thing she would later tell everyone else.

She would avenge her wife.

[_For justice._](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XD-K3AirOdA&t=13850)
