Mordon returned to Libestal a little behind on account of having to be absent for a check-up at the plague doctor and all, which meant she had a lot of things to catch up on.

It didn't help much that Immerkind had a situation she was going through that Mordon just couldn't ignore (see: [Immerkind-Mordon](#edge:calli-cecilia)). Then there was Nerissa with her usual song and dance, scaring the bejeezus out of her every chance she got—seriously, what is [her problem](www.youtube.com/watch?v=UBbTSf5A9bc&t=3428s)?

Then there was Maaz. _Oh boy, was there Maaz._ All Mordon needed from her was an Amulet of Levitation; it should be easy, right? But the demands were relentless: Lapis, upgrade stones, charms, soup, wait, no scratch the soup—wait SCRATCH THE SOUP?! And to boot, calling her an oaf? Mordon was having none of it.

["That's what you get for making me do all that BULLSHIT!"](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UBbTSf5A9bc&t=8556s)

With the amulet finally in hand, Mordon managed to enter the Labyrinth of Song with Bijou, NinoIna, and Immerkind, where her musical abilities were tested. More specifically, her memory was tested, but she quickly solved the issue with some quick snaps of her phone—no use having one if you aren't going to use it, [right](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UBbTSf5A9bc&t=11222s)?

With these puzzles conquered, they got through the Labyrinth and successfully retrieved the memories of the Demon of Sound, [Nerissa Ravencroft](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UBbTSf5A9bc&t=12135s). Not only that, but Mordon had to perform a song nostalgic to her in order to open the way ahead inside Terminus. It was finally time to use that one trusty tool that's been sitting in her back pocket.

[doooot doot doot doot](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/UBbTSf5A9bc?si=Ff3bsK45D849GVN9&t=12336)

After that, it was time for... _another_ dungeon. That's right—because our chef had missed a day, there were not one but _two_ dungeons, and this second one was on a whole other level. Labeled as the Labyrinth of Duality, the party of NinoIna, Immerkind, and Mordon herself found themselves sharing health and potion effects for the duration of the labyrinth, something that proved to be simple enough at times, but just as challenging as she thought it would be. Also, these puzzles were just hard. [Her brain hurts.](https://www.youtube.com/live/UBbTSf5A9bc?si=TijXmLN-N0r_1e9Y&t=18522)

It would take a lot of perseverance, but eventually, they'd find their way through with the power of their bonds. After a short stop at a cozy little house, there was one more set of memories to be recovered today: those of the twin demon dogs, [Fuwawa Abyssgard and Mococo Abyssgard](https://www.youtube.com/live/UBbTSf5A9bc?si=JMqpBbrJXLiHbPjY&t=19638).

Who would've thought those two were _actually_ twins, huh? With another adventure under her belt, Mordon left Terminus to speak with Fia. She'd come this far on the side of good, and she wasn't interested in abandoning it at this rate. It was time to restock her shops and head to bed—

[!!!](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/UBbTSf5A9bc?si=Qth2rf7Z9v4ygpLi&t=20419)

Alright, there's a trend. What the hell's going on? Why's everyone suffocating in walls? Her shinigami senses were tingling, and she wanted to know about this in more detail, even if she had a bad feeling about it. After being told to come to the church, she overheard Princess Bijou scheming against her, and she had to stand her grOOOOOOOUND-?!

[Ever get a feeling of déjà vu?](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/UBbTSf5A9bc?si=QV3B2I40qETcZVIz&t=20525)

The next couple of minutes were full of panic: from Mordon; from the conspirators; from Knight Immerkind, who'd found her way here too somehow(?). Mordon ended up discovering the identity of Shakira's killer using her sleight of hand, but she would end up dropping down to the concealed zone built under the church, together with Immerkind. At that point, no matter how much she struggled, she was powerless to stop what was about to happen (see [Sister Shiori](#node:shiori)), and the last couple of minutes were erased into a blur inside her mind.

[...but the chicken did not forget.](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/UBbTSf5A9bc?si=vJ_CiaSHV72da4Gf&t=20816)
