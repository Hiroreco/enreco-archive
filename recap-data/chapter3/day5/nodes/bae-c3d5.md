The sun once again set as the peasant readied herself for the future. The time draws near, and the final day will be upon them once again.

As in the days before, Peasant mined the land and fished the lakes, building up her riches until she was finally the **richest of all the land!**

A goal very much within her sights with [the gold](https://youtu.be/Ap9GUELHGSg?t=293) she already had in hand.

And haste she must since the day can only last so long and there are peasant duties to be done. Perhaps in an Isekai world.

Not to waste time, the peasant fought once again in the fields where the strongest of the stains can be found.

And of course to [fish.](https://youtu.be/Ap9GUELHGSg?t=1098)

But alas, the Lake King eluded Bae so far. Perhaps there is something that Peasant can learn to improve her already impressive piscatorial skills.

And, as it turned out, fishing with enchanted bait will also enchant your own fishing rod.

But it will all be for naught if the royalty of the lakes were to elude her once more.

With that in mind, Bae prepared for the hardest battle of her life yet.

A little rod comparison with [Ina the Hero](https://youtu.be/Ap9GUELHGSg?t=2052), which the peasant barely lost. For now, Ina the Hero has the strongest rod in the Kingdom.

So, Bae toiled once again, looking for the premium shaded spot to find her adversary. It was a long and arduous battle, but finally...

[The Lake King.](#embed:https://youtu.be/Ap9GUELHGSg?t=5150)

And of course, hard work is not without its rewards. Bae had accumulated far too many things throughout her days, and her stash can only hold so much.

Though hypocritical, the peasant had set up her own shop.

[The Peasant Pop-Up!](#embed:https://youtu.be/Ap9GUELHGSg?t=6191)

And already her first customer! Along with her [first complaint](https://youtu.be/Ap9GUELHGSg?t=6248).

The peasant had truly undergone a change to price her stock like that. Nonetheless, after a successful bargaining from Nerissa Juliet, the Lake King was sold for 6,700 gold.

What would Charlie think, Bae?

With her true goal accomplished, Peasant sought to conquer the next Labyrinth along with Tam and Roa. The three-lettered group.

The Labyrinth of Duality, as a trio, was an experience for Bae. For a moment, it seemed like it might be for naught, for she lacked a partner to traverse; [the gods spoke and showed her the way](https://youtu.be/Ap9GUELHGSg?t=7186).

It tested their coordination, their parkour skills, their trust in each other, and their ability to create potions.

And [their ability to coordinate while parkouring.](https://youtu.be/Ap9GUELHGSg?t=8798)

It was a struggle. One of the hardest challenges Bae has ever faced throughout her days in Libestal, past and present.

Still, Bae was nothing if not a persistent peasant. Nothing would come between her and her goals. And so, after [much hardship.](https://youtu.be/Ap9GUELHGSg?t=9599)

[Until finally it was over.](#embed:https://youtu.be/Ap9GUELHGSg?t=9690)

The Cerberus was put down soon enough.

Finally, the peasant can leave.
