Another night of rest. Good rest too, or at least, it felt good.

That mood was immediately crashed by seeing so many open doors, making Lady Bloodflame groan in despair as she walked around closing doors. Did _anyone_ believe in having a sense of privacy around here!?

In the midst of this adventure, she ran into Mordon Ramsay. How long had it been since she had seen the chef? To the disappointment of Mordon, she had no store to sell stones. As a knight, she didn't need money!

With some minor conversations completed, Lady Bloodflame made her way through her tasks and into the castle. After going Above and Below in the castle, she gasped for air as she made it all the way to the top. Talking through a strained voice, she groaned at knowing she would need to return with a rose.

Turning back to the hall, she leaned over the railing and exhaled.

[And jumped.](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8J7aGAe42EE&t=1719)

Death was something she never took as a shortcut before, but without a second thought she had done it.

Going to her place after purchasing what she would need, the Jade Knight was shocked to see a little [Viridus statue](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8J7aGAe42EE&t=2188) next to her bed, making her light up with a smile. Leaving the house, she dropped the spare rose she had purchased into Nerissa's mailbox without a word.

And painfully climbed the stairs again. Quest completed, she waited for the dungeon for the day, heading down to the arena.

Apparently her sister, Mococo, needed to learn to [spar](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8J7aGAe42EE&t=2784). The excitement of the arena had the knight grinning as she quickly signed up for a match, picking up her wooden sword and shield. Even though the match ended in a tie, it made her happy to be having fun again with something that wasn't life or death.

Upon the time of arrival for the dungeon, Liz turned to Nerissa where she was given directions before they split ways (see [Liz-Nerissa](#edge:liz-nerissa)).

Running around, Lady Bloodflame fought to get her weapon upgraded to the highest level. Somehow she had fallen behind despite being so sure she should have been prepared. A sting of guilt hit her as the others waited on her.

Shiori gave her a call and Liz picked up instantly, needing to talk to her partner (see [Liz-Shiori](#edge:liz-shiori)).

With Thorn properly upgraded well enough, it was time for the dungeon. Liz, Shiori, Fuwawa, and Mococo set off into a dungeon that would require teamwork. Pain was shared and so was death it seemed.

The entire [potion creation](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8J7aGAe42EE&t=6282) was giving her a headache as she tried to understand how it worked. Through the phone, Mococo and Shiori tried to explain before everyone got on the same page. They just needed to prepare two of each potion and then drink them if the room matched.

With that out of the way, they made their way through the rest of the dungeon to find themselves fighting a [dog with multiple heads](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8J7aGAe42EE&t=8618). It spoke twice, at the same time, in complete synchronization.

Stepping into the hall, Liz assured Mococo they would chat the next day so that the poor girl could rest. It couldn't be easy to get your memories back, to find out how many things weren't what you thought.

Liz thought on that herself. She seemed to be one of the last to get their memories back, after all.

Still, Liz imposed the question to Nerissa.

[What if we change?](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8J7aGAe42EE&t=9775)

Still, everything they had done was real. Physical. She could touch things, interact, see people change.

The thoughts were too tiring, so she retired for a short nap. The fog in her head had tired her out combined with the heat from the sun of Libestal beaming down upon them all.

And what wonders the nap had done for her. Upon getting up, she recalled the gifts from Nerissa, taking time to sit outside and open box after box. Most were disappointing, some disgusting, but every little statue of a guild master made her smile.

With her fun completed, Lady Bloodflame moved to their guild, checking what needed to be done. Grimacing, she stared at the Lake King fish necessary to progress. Worse even, she couldn't [catch it](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FzangfLML4E&t=1927). Not since swapping back into a smith earlier on.

Sighing, she ran into Nerissa at the quest board for another quick chat. With more stain souls provided, she could once again feed one of her two addictions: gacha.

Her day started to wind down, having a rare chat with [Tam](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FzangfLML4E&t=2238) before speaking to Star Speaker Rigel.

As the sun set, she made her way to Nerissa where the two would have a private moment.

Finally, she helped work on her garden, putting down some hedge walls before calling it once again.

What would await them on their final day?
