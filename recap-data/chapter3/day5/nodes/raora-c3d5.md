The founder of the Illumination began her morning in her safe space: [outside the borders](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rFmdOarYXuE&t=160). Woken up a bit earlier from her much-needed rest by a non-obese creature called "The Fat". Another day to fight for enlightenment, bound by a vow to never stop until everyone knows the truth. She starts it again with a dive into the water for death anew.

Throughout the morning, Roa held profound discussions with Nerissa and Bijou about the secrets of this world and promised to journey together outside the walls. She stumbled upon Sister Shiori afterwards and had another confrontation. But thankfully she called Immerkind right after. (See [Cecilia-Raora](#edge:cecilia-raora))

Right outside her house, Roa saw Mordan Ramsay and Lady Bloodflame.
She greeted them with some pleasantries as she takes care of business for people who needed her services as a smith. Also, the chef gave a brief mention about a [trial](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rFmdOarYXuE&t=1600) scheduled for tomorrow as Roa walks past them.

But something feels off as she opens her door.

A sickening transformation had overtaken her floor, replacing it with objects that have no rhyme or reason. Obsidian mass with red nerves like lesions, sickly bile-colored bright rocks, and ancient bricks of paradox.

And to top it off, an old wooden frame plastered in the basement wall.

With a mockery of her wife's corpse as a mere carcass covered in pale, putrid ooze. The deafening music outside certainly wasn't helping Roa, but she had a solution for that at least.

A quest required Roa's immediate attention, so off to the prison cell area she goes. She had a talk with a person called Prisoner Zero. This prisoner shared different views about freedom that the smith cat [resonates with](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rFmdOarYXuE&t=2250). Calling this individual a possible member of the Illumination and an Enlightened.

Prisoner Zero even reveals his true name as:

[AAAHHHH!](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/rFmdOarYXuE?t=2446)

After helping the Libestal citizens, Roa stumbled upon Lady Bloodflame.
They discussed each other's well-being and activities they have been up to since they last spoke. Both of them waved goodbye momentarily because they found each other again later with Mococo assisting them where to find [enigmatic stones](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rFmdOarYXuE&t=4165).

Roa teamed up with Tam and Peasant as the time to enter the Labyrinth of Duality came to be. Tam and Roa go on again with their silly banter about their names, then Roa hummed an adorable melody in honor of Tam's nicknames. They defeated the dungeon afterwards.

The biggest meeting of the Illumination took place in the [basement](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rFmdOarYXuE&t=10810) of Roa's house, with Nerissa and Bijou taking attendance. Roa updated the members of her secret society, officially recording Immerkind, Bijou the Stained, and Nerissa Juliet. She also educates both of them in the room about the recent Gap found near the castle walls, as instructed by Immerkind earlier. Although, the leader of the Illumination takes Nerissa first to see the "Fourth Wall".

Upon returning back to the city, Roa gathers Nerissa and Bijou to check the castle Gap. They all attempted to maneuver through the complex series of steps needed to reach the walls, but Sister Shiori arrives (See [Raora-Shiori](#edge:raora-shiori)) and later leaves Roa with a broken mind after a grueling encounter deep below the Church basement.

Roa materialized beneath the shadows of the castle gates like a [discarded husk](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rFmdOarYXuE&t=12970). Her broken form sprawled helplessly on top of the cold, rocky street. She stands up as a reflex to collect herself but abruptly falls to the ground again.

Crawling to reach her wife's gravestone, Roa breathes heavily to compose herself. A splitting headache breaking any kind of thought she wished to make sense of. Recollecting memories she still retained after a ceremony of blasphemous communion.

The tears that painted her face with unspeakable sorrow came from the swollen, scorched retinas that people may call a window to her soul.
