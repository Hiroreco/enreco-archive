The morning begins, and Bijou the Stained finds that her affliction still weighs on her. No matter. Even if her killing of Shakira was the cause, she would push ahead, for she has important things to do this day. She grows tired of the kingdom and its people, so they must be made to suffer as she does.

She begins by meeting with Roa, the two believing it will be a great day to learn about the gaps in the world. Roa also leads Bijou to show her a discovery she had made: a glass structure beyond the borders of the world. (See: [Bijou-Roa](#edge:bijou-raora))

Returning to the town, Bijou makes a call to Sister Shiori. She feels that she can no longer keep her sins to herself, and she finds Shiori to be the only one who truly cares and understands her. The two make plans to meet later in the day, to sow the seeds of rebellion across the kingdom. (See: [Bijou-Shiori](#edge:bijou-shiori))

Parting ways with the Sister, Bijou begins stocking up on Ultimate Potions of Invisibility for the day's hauntings, surprised that no one else has discovered them yet. In doing so, she feels her affliction continuing to weigh on her. She anticipates she will feel worse after continuing to defile Shakira's body, but she will bear that weight regardless, for the lands must burn. And besides, perhaps what Roa had shared about these lands not being real is true?

[Why should she feel bad for destroying what does not exist?](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FJNKv92ZWdM&t=1780)

Following her preparations, Bijou meets with her sister NinoIna, her knight Immerkind, and Mordon Ramsay to take on the Labyrinth of Song, which the four had been unable to challenge the day prior. With the many musical puzzles, Bijou finds herself recalling the lessons she had received at the Princess Academy she attended. Clearing the dungeon, they are awarded a glimpse at Nerissa's memories and the Music Box of Memories. To complete its melody and let it function, Bijou joins with NinoIna and Immerkind in singing the [nostalgic lyrics of Simple and Clean](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FJNKv92ZWdM&t=5700).

Discussing with Faust what the Music Box had shown her, Bijou decides to meet with Prisoner Zero to gain access to Cells 0201-0202. When asked to prove she is truly free, Bijou remarks that she had already done so, based on Prisoner Zero's examples, but she decides she will still play along. She [calls Gonathon](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FJNKv92ZWdM&t=6280), declaring them friends as an act of befriending an enemy. She notes, however, that the true freedom in this action is that she does not really mean it.

Wanting to make sure that she has time after completing the dungeons to enact her plans before her affliction takes her, Bijou meets with Nerissa and Gonathon to tackle the Labyrinth of Duality. Upon the Labyrinth's completion and the glimpse at Fuwawa and Mococo's memories, Bijou decides that she does not want to team up with those who have not abandoned Fia and will stay alongside Nix instead.

Before fully beginning with her schemes, Bijou takes some time to meet with others. She starts with Roa, the two discussing how Nerissa had joined the Illumination and the gap in town that Immerkind found. She also finds Fuwawa's music mashup and gifts her the Rock In disc found in the Labyrinth of Emotion. However, [when Fuwawa starts playing the song](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FJNKv92ZWdM&t=11025), a shot of piercing pain runs through Bijou's mind, leading her to yell at Fuwawa to make it stop and refusing to take back the disc despite wanting it back beforehand.

Bijou then makes contact with Shiori, the two reaffirming their plans to work together soon. Shiori also inquires about what Bijou remembers now that her memories have returned and if she remembers those who imprisoned them. Just as Shiori asks this, Roa and Nerissa appear to take Bijou to the gap that was found in town. Shiori leaves to prepare some things, and Bijou goes with the other members of the Illumination.

As they were investigating the gap, [Shiori returns](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FJNKv92ZWdM&t=11550), taunting Roa. Bijou uses this opportunity to slip away and prepare her haunting equipment, wanting to obtain a Magic Feather of Flight. By the time she has managed to obtain one, she's missed her opportunity, however, as Roa has already left.

Trying to track Roa down, Bijou comes across Gonathon and Tam, taking the opportunity to haunt them as well. Being signaled by a call from Shiori, she returns to the church as Roa has been brought there as well. [Pretending to be Shakira's spirit](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FJNKv92ZWdM&t=12230), Bijou helps bring Roa to the space beneath the church. There, Shiori and Nerissa force Roa into completing the ritual before Bijou reveals herself. She informs Roa that it was by her own hand that Shakira perished in order to further break Roa before she is made to suffocate as a sacrifice.

Following Roa's sacrifice, Bijou returns to her hauntings. She haunts Fuwawa even as the poor pup is being brought down to the basement. The coven reveals itself to Fuwawa as Advent, bringing her into the group once more...before she is also made to suffocate, not being able to get out otherwise.

["We are Advent."](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FJNKv92ZWdM&t=13600)

Bijou finds that she is gaining a taste for sacrifice, wanting to sacrifice Mordon next. As luck would have it, the chef had just come by the church alongside Immerkind, so the three members of the coven brought them down to the basement. Bijou reveals herself as Shakira's killer once more, before Mordon and Immerkind are sacrificed. Not like they'll remember it anyway.

The members of Advent then [reunite one more time](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FJNKv92ZWdM&t=14450), congratulating each other for what they had accomplished that day. It seems they are also preparing something for the next day...
