After a good night's rest, Mococo was up and ready to do one thing... make some more MONEY!! She happily fed her little buddy WOOF and set out for the day in better spirits, especially because once again, SHE GOT SALES!! She didn't have a lot of the new important stones right now, though... so you know what time it is! It was time to explore, make charms, and talk to people around to see what she could get!

She went around checking people's stores, mildly disappointed that Tam Gandr [hadn't taken her business advice](https://www.youtube.com/live/K3N6S6rIgS0?si=anhllAV_aKXh0rJy&t=1231) from yesterday. Her shop's just not worth it... and she was also getting a little tired of seeing the Illumination posters being everywhere all the time. But it wasn't long before she found herself back in one of her old favorite spots–it was time for more Color Cannon, and you know what they say! Always bet on red! The first two rounds were disappointing, but it wasn't long before Mococo had a familiar reason to celebrate.

[GANBARIIIIIIII!!](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/K3N6S6rIgS0?si=EKJypjSEVZGT3QkJ&t=2301)

After that, she helped Roa set up her business and called her beloved sister Lady Bloodflame, just in time to hear her be run over by a horse. [Again](https://www.youtube.com/live/K3N6S6rIgS0?si=S--IUO1J1n0GPamn&t=2574). She reunites with her and discusses some plans for the upcoming dungeon, and Mococo mentions that she's uneasy thinking about it... especially when it comes to working with Fuwawa. The knight does her best to reassure the little witch, but it doesn't go as successfully as she'd hoped. Still, Mococo tries to force a smile and look forward. Her sister's being so brave... she has to be too, right?

For one last stop before she headed out, Mococo stopped by the memorial built to honor the departed Shakira. It had long been desecrated by now, but its original intent still shone through as a symbol of her glory immortalized.

Mococo didn't get why there was so much chaos over this.

[What was so great about her?](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/K3N6S6rIgS0?si=j-zmKYWYTe5zBzbB&t=3056)

She never really talked to her. She only heard that she had died. She was probably the least sad in all of Libestal about this tragedy.

And yet... she's left an impact on her life all the same. The departure of a loved one changes someone, and they'd go through heaven and earth to preserve their memory of the fallen. Just look at Roa and how much Shakira's death changed her, for example. Her current mental state isn't all because of that, but it was a big factor pushing it forward. Because she loves her wife so dearly, she's willing to risk it all over and over to push through for her sake.

One could say that a person is only gone once the memory of them has also faded away. And if a memory is recovered, that person returns to inhabit someone's heart...

She didn't understand it. Any of this. But there was something about this memorial that left a deep impact on her, even if she didn't want to admit it.

...at least it looks cool.

Alas, despite her worries, a dungeon is a dungeon, and it must be cleared for the sake of heroism. After doing some training with her party at the coliseum and doing some final preparatory quests, it was time to head out. Together with Lady Bloodflame, Sister Shiori and... Fuwawa...

[Mococo ventured into the Labyrinth of Duality.](https://www.youtube.com/live/K3N6S6rIgS0?si=-cs3526AN6jfBFJc&t=6392)

Souls linked together. Her beloved sister, Elizabeth.

It hurt. [It hurt so much.](https://www.youtube.com/live/K3N6S6rIgS0?si=Cdj_1r1zngdJVHfX&t=7478) And it only got worse the deeper in they went. 

...

Eventually. The deed was done.

At the end, there was a vision of the other side. [A home for flowers.](https://www.youtube.com/live/K3N6S6rIgS0?si=Au_nFbVke6oAzvDU&t=9614) 

It was... peaceful. Very peaceful... warm, even. Mococo smiled, giggling and having fun with her party for a moment. She wanted to stay here forever... maybe she'd find a way to come back later!

Her recent worries and pains washed away... lowering her guard completely.
