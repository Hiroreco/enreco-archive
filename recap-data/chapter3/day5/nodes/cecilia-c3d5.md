["What does this mean?"](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IpNXgvS3QjM&t=652)

Immerkind giggles to herself as she stares at the mysterious "Illumination posters" scattered throughout Libestal. Having spent the previous day in a deep slumber, the kind knight awakens utterly confused by the sudden changes around the kingdom. Still, no amount of mystery would stop her from fulfilling her mission: building Tam Gandr's castle!

...A task she immediately comes to regret.

The [long hours of toil](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IpNXgvS3QjM&t=2603) spent placing and removing blocks leave Immerkind utterly exhausted. She is a kind knight, yes—but manual labor was clearly not her calling. Yet kindness perseveres, and in her hour of need, the [kind Lady Juliet](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IpNXgvS3QjM&t=2687) arrives to lend a helping hand. Soon after, the kind Nerissa introduces her to the [power of gacha](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IpNXgvS3QjM&t=2740), prompting Immerkind to shower the lady with [kind kisses](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IpNXgvS3QjM&t=2905).

While continuing work on the castle, Immerkind stumbles across Sister Shiori and Nerissa engaged in a suspicious conversation. The [knight silently peers](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IpNXgvS3QjM&t=3425) through a hole in the castle wall she had accidentally made, eavesdropping just long enough to hear unsettling words involving Juliet and the powers of Lady Bloodflame. Before she can process any of it, [Nerissa catches her](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IpNXgvS3QjM&t=3634)... only to kindly gift her important building materials.

How could someone so kind possibly be evil?

Conflicted beyond belief, Immerkind runs frantically through Libestal. "I am so conflicted!" the knight cries as she sprints through the city. Her eyes suddenly fall upon a small window high along the castle walls. Through sheer determination and questionable physics, she somehow discovers [a way out.](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IpNXgvS3QjM&t=3970)

The voices in her head quickly [notify Roa](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IpNXgvS3QjM&t=4289) of this monumental discovery. Immerkind escorts Roa to the wall and proudly demonstrates the escape route. Then, in an act of pure kindness, she assists Roa by [pushing her directly off the wall](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IpNXgvS3QjM&t=4690).

Afterward, Immerkind joins forces with NinoIna, Gonathon, and Mordon Ramsay to gather resources so she can finally enter the dungeon she missed the previous day. Along the way, she secretly informs Gonathon about the suspicious conversation she overheard between Nerissa and Sister Shiori (see [Cecilia-Gigi](#edge:cecilia-gigi)).

The group relies on their understanding of [music](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IpNXgvS3QjM&t=7493) and rhythm to overcome the dungeon's many challenges, uncovering hidden secrets along the way. Immerkind even finds time to showcase her impressive musical [skills](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IpNXgvS3QjM&t=7547). But upon their victory, the memories of Juliet suddenly come flooding into the knight's [mind](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IpNXgvS3QjM&t=8890).

Even so, Immerkind continues to insist that Juliet is good.

Later, while searching for Gonathon within the castle, Immerkind stumbles upon Iphania's old room, now transformed into what appears to be a library. ["I miss her ..."](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IpNXgvS3QjM&t=10275) the knight whispers tearfully.

She returns to Tam's castle and resumes construction, proudly showing NinoIna the progress she has made. Unfortunately, the manual labor once again [fills her with anguish](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IpNXgvS3QjM&t=11908). But amidst the suffering comes a moment of joy—Immerkind pulls a Fia Headwear from one of the material [boxes!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IpNXgvS3QjM&t=11875)

Now Iphania can always remain by her side.

After this brief respite, the group sets off toward the [Labyrinth of Duality](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IpNXgvS3QjM&t=13090). Together, they endure countless trials and tribulations, steadily overcoming each challenge through teamwork and determination. Eventually, the trio encounters the [Prisoner of Duality](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IpNXgvS3QjM&t=16109), carefully preparing themselves with delicious soup and powerful brews before the battle ahead.

The team successfully conquers the dungeon and witnesses the haunting [memories](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IpNXgvS3QjM&t=16446) of the Little Witch Mococo and Fuwawa.

Not long after, Immerkind witnesses a horrifying sight as the [body of Shakira chases Mordon](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IpNXgvS3QjM&t=17247) through Libestal. Sister Shiori urgently instructs everyone to flee to the church and exorcise the body. Immerkind follows Mordon and the others into the church basement, where a terrifying revelation unfolds and the truth is finally exposed.

The killer is --!

[-Redacted-](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IpNXgvS3QjM&t=17505)

Moments later, Immerkind awakens beside the fountain in Libestal and reunites with Mordon, who now carries Shakira as a pet chicken. Gonathon soon calls Immerkind to ask what happened inside the church, but the knight discovers that her memories are incomplete. When the two meet again, they realize they both spoke to Sister Shiori... and both now suffer from missing [memories](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IpNXgvS3QjM&t=18001).

The pair eventually regroup with Tam, who shares alarming information she overheard. According to Tam, Advent intends to [kill Justice](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IpNXgvS3QjM&t=18834).

The group falls silent.

"...Who is Justice?" Immerkind asks.

Despite everything, the knight still refuses to believe Juliet could truly be evil. Attempting to lift the mood, Immerkind instead tells everyone a joke about [a green pea](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IpNXgvS3QjM&t=19109).

Soon after, the trio enacts a plan to infiltrate the castle while Gonathon distracts Nerissa. Tam, Tamda, and Immerkind sneak into the church and [search](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IpNXgvS3QjM&t=19518) thoroughly for anything suspicious hidden within. Finding nothing, Immerkind instead decides to proudly show Tam her castle.

The two share a heartfelt conversation about the strange misfortunes and unkindness that seem to follow whenever Immerkind spends time with Tam. Wanting to make things right, the knight promises to continue working hard on the castle—and even vows to build a magnificent gold castle for [Tamda](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IpNXgvS3QjM&t=20797) as well.

The day finally comes to an end where it all began: at Tam's castle.

There, Immerkind speaks quietly with Juliet one last time.

[There is no way she can be evil, right?](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IpNXgvS3QjM&t=21252)
