Gonathon's day immediately became hectic as he made his rounds. After pleading with Gonathon for assistance in acquiring materials needed for the next dungeon, Immerkind relayed some [intel](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=662s) she had gathered in a reconnaissance mission: Shiori and Nerissa were planning to kill someone! Combined with other strange occurrences, such as Shakira's body disappearing from the church and strange glitches occurring, the two concluded that the Curse of Advent was spreading quickly.

[The Shining Stars were not aligned](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=855s)

Returning to the farming fields to gather materials for the guild, Gonathon requested an enchanted pickaxe from Bae. Despite all he had done for the Peasant and their sworn oath as brothers, the [miserly rat](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=1801s) still demanded payment, which the generous knight was all too willing to provide.

Venturing deeper into the fields, Gonathon encountered Shiori, who was as always keen to converse with him (see [Gonathon-Shiori](#edge:gigi-shiori)). The two found themselves at a familiar spot: the waterfall where they had reconciled in the ancient past. This time around the witch was more interested in discussing [intrigue](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=2294s), including the dubious relationship between Nerissa and Lady Bloodflame. Leaving Gonathon with her pessimistic views of the world, Shiori departed as Nerissa arrived to collect the knight for the dungeon they were to embark upon together.

[Both of you are Shiori to me](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=2660s)

Before this could occur, however, another blue woman made her appearance: the taciturn Tam Gandr (see [Gonathon-Tam Gandr](#edge:gigi-kronii)). The queen would not stick around for long, as following a sour conversation between her and Nerissa, Tam departed. Gonathon's early mingling would only get stranger from here, as a call from Bijou resulted in the fussy gem proclaiming that she and Gonathon were now [friends](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=4514s) before quickly hanging up (see [Bijou-Gonathon](#edge:bijou-gigi)).

Returning to town to prepare for the dungeon, Gonathon met up with Immerkind once more. The automaton knight bequeathed a rose to Gonathon, a platonic symbol of their bond. Immerkind, for her part, spilled her guts about her fears concerning her relationship with Fia, but as always Gonathon remained optimistic things would turn out well.

[A rose at the top of the castle](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=4959s)

With the myriad of distractions finally subsided, Gonathon entered the Labyrinth of Duality alongside Nerissa and Bijou (see [Gonathon-Nerissa](#edge:gigi-nerissa)). This dungeon proved to be one of the most difficult yet, with Gonathon forced to cooperate closely with the untrustworthy members of Rebellion. Despite any misgivings, the trio emerged [victorious](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=7429s) and were rewarded with the final memories of Advent: those of the twin demon guard dogs Fuwawa and Mococo.

Upon his victorious return to Libestal, Gonathon was once more confronted by Shiori. The witch wistfully requested that he follow her, claiming that she wished for him to be ["her first."](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=8804s) With these ominous words she guided him to the basement of her church, whereupon a horrific scene unfolded. There in the depths of the earth, Gonathon gazed upon the [unholy altar](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=9000s) she had constructed. Shiori pleaded with Gonathon to escape with her, to be her chosen one. Gonathon flatly refused, and upon having his memories wiped, the knight found himself back in the city.

[A witch's farewell](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=9164s)

From here, he stumbled into Tam once more. But the two were not alone: the ghastly [apparition of Shakira](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=10382s) had taken to haunting them. Fleeing in a frenzy of terror, Gonathon desperately pleaded to be forgiven for his sins.

It was here that he once more found Tam, the queen having been stripped of her stain by Advent. Realizing she truly desired to be freed of her burdens, Gonathon recognized her as simply [Tam](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=12180s), his close friend and comrade.

[A bond between wolves](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=12325s)

The knight was unable to savor this cordial abdication, as he soon discovered that Immerkind had suffered a [similar fate](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=12712s) to him at Shiori's church. Realizing that something had to be done, Gonathon and Immerkind joined forces with Tam and NinoIna to form a new force dedicated to preserving justice.

[To the Fields of Justice!](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=13299s)

With Advent's power waxing every moment, the group committed to operating with subterfuge. Gonathon, for his part, [distracted](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=14294s) Nerissa long enough for Tam and Immerkind to scout out the malefic church. After failing to find the basement, they concluded that their best course of action was to wait until the next day and use Tam as [bait.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=15331s)

The die had been cast, and it was unclear whether justice would prevail. Just what did the final day hold in store? Only time would tell.
