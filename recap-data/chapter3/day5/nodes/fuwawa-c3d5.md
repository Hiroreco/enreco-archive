The fluffy one had another dungeon conquest ahead of her today, though this one already felt different from all the previous expeditions. Not only did it promise to uncover long-buried secrets, but she also had to travel alongside a particularly troublesome [someone](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Db4P0eDfOMY&t=1153s).

As she prepared for the journey, she noticed the kingdom had grown far rowdier than usual. Giant floating text loomed across the skies, posters of the "Illumination" covered the streets, and strange slogans echoed through the town. Fuwawa rarely involved herself in such matters, but even she could not help but grow curious.

["The border will fall"?](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/Db4P0eDfOMY?si=IVf_l27WEeApig-Y&t=1666)

Certainly mysterious, though she ultimately dismissed it as just another odd hobby people had picked up.

Speaking of hobbies, Fuwawa had recently discovered an intense love for vinyl records. Ever since finding one the day before, she had become determined to collect as many as possible, much to the probable suffering of her neighbors, thanks to the endless blasting of [music](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Db4P0eDfOMY&t=1878s) from her home.

Before the dungeon expedition began, she attended a [sparring lesson](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Db4P0eDfOMY&t=3915s). Afterwards, she finally joined Lady Bloodflame, Shiori, and Mococo to challenge the [Labyrinth of Duality](https://www.youtube.com/live/Db4P0eDfOMY?si=8ltE2Ks7A8_tgorL&t=6762).

The dungeon revolved entirely around pairs. Every puzzle, every obstacle, every battle demanded trust, coordination, and precise timing between partners. Compared to the previous dungeons, this one proved far more difficult than expected.

Yet despite the hardships, the group managed to overcome every trial placed before them.

And upon conquering the labyrinth, Fuwawa finally recovered the memories of who she truly was.

[The Twin Guard Dogs](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Db4P0eDfOMY&t=9723s)

She had once been a guard dog stationed at the Gates of Hell. One day, a lost dog wandered near her post, and Fuwawa chose to abandon her duty in order to guide it safely back to the mortal realm. The journey had been long and arduous, and through it she came to realize just how dull life in Hell truly was. In contrast, the mortal world was vibrant, joyful, and endlessly entertaining.

So she left Hell behind entirely, embracing her newfound "heaven."

But the revelation did not end there.

She had not been the only guard dog.

The very partner she had spent the entire day alongside, the one whom she protected, worried for, and endured the dungeon with, was someone far more important than she had ever realized. Mococo was not merely a companion, but the other half she had unknowingly been searching for all along (see [Fuwawa-Mococo](#edge:fuwawa-mococo)).

With the emotional revelations finally behind her, Fuwawa spent the remainder of the day happily restocking her store and expanding her ever-growing mountain of vinyl discs. She proudly enjoyed her newly acquired [collection](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Db4P0eDfOMY&t=11245s), even managing to completely [traumatize](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Db4P0eDfOMY&t=11469s) an unfortunate bystander with it.

She also obtained a variety of new pets, carefully giving each one a uniquely [meaningful](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Db4P0eDfOMY&t=13229s) name.

And with nothing else left to accomplish, the fluffy one happily called it a day.

...

Or at least, she _should_ have called it a day.

[B-bau bau?!?!](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/Db4P0eDfOMY?si=SXHMGELCh98FJGTW&t=13714)

Out of nowhere, Fuwawa found herself being chased through Libestal by the ghostly figure of Shakira. Panicked and utterly confused, the poor dog hastily fled through the darkened streets until she accidentally stumbled upon something she absolutely was never meant to see.
