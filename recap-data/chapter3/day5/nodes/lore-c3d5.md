#### Main Story

**Objective**

Armed with either a shortcut or a fireproof shield, the heroes make their way to Cells 0201-0202 and venture deep into the **Labyrinth of Duality.**

After defeating the Prisoner of Duality, they finally obtain the Looking-Glass Shard Faust had spoken of.

Not long after, Fia contacts them, saying everything is finally prepared for their journey into Nullspace and their pursuit of the Outsider. But before that can happen, the fractured heroes must reunite once more.

Nix quickly interrupts the call, insisting that the heroes should never place their trust in those who once turned against them.

**Discoveries**

Heroes who choose to speak with Fia learn that traces of the Curse may still linger within certain members of the group, preventing any true **reunion** from taking place.

Seeking answers, they consult Star Speaker Rigel, who promises to search for a Rite of Purification capable of cleansing the remaining corruption.

And so, with little else they can do, the heroes are left to await Rigel’s response while preparing themselves for the raid on Nullspace.
