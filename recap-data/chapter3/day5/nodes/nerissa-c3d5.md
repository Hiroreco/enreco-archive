**There's Something Happening**

It seemed all she truly needed was proper rest. When Nerissa awoke, she felt lighter—clearer somehow.

Today, she finally came to [peace with her identity](https://youtu.be/4anxJRoJo4s?t=321). Not as *The Witch*, nor as *Nerissa Juliet*, but simply Nerissa. How exactly she reconciled with herself remained unknown.

Perhaps she spoke with the many versions of herself in her dreams. Or maybe the voices in her head finally convinced her to stop fighting and accept everything at once.

---

**Broken Brokes**

With her identity crisis seemingly settled, it was time to face the true, never-ending battle.

Money.

For four straight days, Nerissa worked tirelessly, clearing every quest she could find just to scrape together enough coins to survive. She passed ten coins, then twenty, eventually even forty.

Surely that would be enough now, right?

[NOPE. SHE WAS STILL BROKE.](#embed:https://youtu.be/4anxJRoJo4s?t=976)

Along the way, she crossed paths with [Peasant The Bae](https://youtu.be/4anxJRoJo4s?t=936) and [Tam Gandr](https://youtu.be/4anxJRoJo4s?t=5984), only to discover they were somehow even poorer than she was. What a miserable peasant. What a tragic queen. How either of them continued living with pockets that were empty remained a mystery.

---

**HELL HOUNDS**

Of course, the day would not be complete without another venture into the Main Quest.

Together with her party—Bijou, Gonathon, and the others—Nerissa entered the [Labyrinth of Duality](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4anxJRoJo4s&t=8502). After conquering its trial, they recovered the lost [memories](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4anxJRoJo4s&t=9912) of Fuwawa Abyssguard and her twin sister, Mococo Abyssguard.

---

**I SEE THE LIGHT**

After the dungeon conquest, Nerissa accompanied Roa, who insisted on showing her something called ["The Gap"](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4anxJRoJo4s&t=11286).

Given Roa's increasingly unhinged behavior over the past few days, Nerissa understandably approached the matter with skepticism. Still, she followed along out of curiosity—not fully believing, yet not dismissing her either.

To her surprise, Roa's discovery appeared to be [genuine.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4anxJRoJo4s&t=11424)

At least, that was how it looked on the surface.

Nerissa needed Roa's trust. She needed it badly enough to slowly guide the pink maiden into a [false](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4anxJRoJo4s&t=12414) sense of security—just enough for Roa to unknowingly become one of Shiori's sacrifices for the project Nerissa had been assisting with these past few days.

And morbidly enough, it worked.

[Nice job, team!](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4anxJRoJo4s&t=12658)

**BENEATH THE MILKY TWILIGHT**

All's well that ends well, they say.

The dungeons had been cleared. Advent's memories had been restored. And somehow, against all odds, the countless shenanigans across Libestal resolved themselves for the time being.

Yet before closing the book on today's adventure, Nerissa still had one final task left unfinished.

[*"Let me slick my hair back."*](#embed:https://youtu.be/4anxJRoJo4s?t=18874)

At the elegant park she had built earlier, Nerissa stood alone beneath the fading sky, a rose in one hand and a ring in the other. She called Lady Bloodflame, inviting the knight to meet her there. The invitation was accepted, though the evening had yet to arrive.

So Nerissa waited.

Minutes passed beneath the relentless [sun,](https://youtu.be/4anxJRoJo4s?t=19329) hanging stubbornly overhead as though refusing to surrender the world to the moon. Even the voices in her head were beginning to lose patience.

Eventually, the gods took pity on her. The sunlight finally softened, the heavens dimmed, and one by one, the stars began to emerge.

Then, Lady Bloodflame arrived (see [Lady Bloodflame-Nerissa](#edge:liz-nerissa)).
