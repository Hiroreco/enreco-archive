With the final piece needed to enter the Nullspace finally recovered, the path toward the Outsider had at last revealed itself. The end of the journey was within reach.

Yet before anyone could press onward, Fia stopped the heroes and offered them one final proposal.

[The choice is up to you](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/phkMUVqF6aY?t=6604)

Fia insisted that if they truly wished to stand a chance against the Outsider, then everyone would need to cast aside their grudges and differences, at least for now, and move forward together as one.

But Nix disagreed.

Where Fia saw strength in bonds, Nix saw chains.

The two stood opposed not merely in opinion, but in belief itself, their convictions clashing like two paths that could never converge.

And so, with the journey seemingly approaching its final objective, the decision no longer rested with Fia or Nix.

It rested with the heroes.

Whose ideals would they follow? Which path would they choose before stepping into the unknown depths of Nullspace?
