The two founding members of the Illumination trusted each other, right? Roa seemed to think so, as Bijou was one of the only ones who believed her about the truth of the world. When the two met in the morning, Roa chose to continue to trust Bijou, showing Bijou another of the major discoveries she had made:

[A glass tower outside the world.](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rFmdOarYXuE&t=625)

Little did Roa know that Bijou was making plans behind her back. The jewel had gone to Sister Shiori, discussing their plans involving Roa. (See: [Bijou-Shiori](#edge:bijou-shiori))

The two went their separate ways until they met once more after taking on the dungeons. They began to question more than just the world, wondering about how they had even lost their memories, and even if the memories that returned were accurate, as some details still felt wrong. As Roa reflects on how few actually believe her, Bijou reassures her, [stating she believes in and supports her](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FJNKv92ZWdM&t=10650).

Roa also informed Bijou that Nerissa had joined the Illumination, and the three intend to investigate the gap that had been found in the city. However, after Shiori appears, the group splits. Roa does not see Bijou again until...

[The Betrayal](#embed:https://youtu.be/rFmdOarYXuE?t=12825)

Bijou reveals herself before Roa as she is about to be sacrificed to the Outer Gods, taunting her about how she had been working with her wife's killer. Awakening following the sacrifice, Roa finds [her memories fading, unsure if Bijou is a friend...](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rFmdOarYXuE&t=12970)
