Immerkind greeted Gonathon with a sorrowful wail, the burnt-out automaton recounting the various burdensome duties she had been forced to complete. Not wishing to wallow too much in her own troubles, Immerkind was also quick to inform Gonathon of what she had [observed](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=662s) while spying on Shiori and Nerissa: that the loathsome members of Advent were planning on killing someone! Despite the clear threat that their foes now posed, Immerkind also mentioned the items that Nerissa had gifted her and communicated her doubts as to whether the song demon was actually evil.

Immerkind and Gonathon spoke at length about [the Curse of Advent](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=1065s), an affliction that they had become aware of but still did not fully understand. Vowing to continue their efforts to thwart the evil that had befallen the kingdom, the two knights parted ways for the time being, with Immerkind descending into dungeons with NinoIna and Bae while Gonathon prepared for the day's trials in the Labyrinth of Duality.

Before Gonathon would enter the dungeon, however, Immerkind accompanied him on some chores in Libestal's markets. While purchasing items, Immerkind glimpsed the room that Princess Iphania had once dwelled in. The heavy-hearted automaton fell into a [depressive stupor](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=5090s) upon seeing where her beloved had been cut down, as it not only dredged up painful memories of that day, but also reminded her of her trepidations in the present. Immerkind opened up to Gonathon, expressing her frustrations and sadness over Fia's seeming disinterest in her. Gonathon reminded Immerkind of the cat's feelings and reassured his beloved friend that all would be well.

[A rose, from one knight to another](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/IpNXgvS3QjM?t=10271)

After completing the day's dungeon, Gonathon met up with Shiori, who invited him into her church. It was here that Gonathon was subjected to a most [unholy and despicable ritual](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=8804s) by the witch, but the knight soon awoke elsewhere with no memories of the event at all.

It soon became apparent that Gonathon was only the first of Shiori's victims. Soon after, Immerkind was also subjected to the ritual. In a panic she called Gonathon, but [her cries](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=12365s) for help were silenced. Rushing to her aid, Gonathon soon found Immerkind, seemingly no worse for wear. But most curious of all was that she had [no memory](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=12712s) of what had occurred, or even that she had called the knight for aid.

[Just what happened in that church...](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=12880s)

The two had little time to ponder this conundrum as they were interrupted by Tam: she was in danger! Fearing that it might be the work of Advent once more, Immerkind and Gonathon came to a realization: if Advent wiped their memories again, there would be no way of remembering the threat they faced. Obtaining some [paper to record their memories](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=13065s), the two sought out the former queen.

Finding Tam hiding from Advent, the three Shining Stars quickly sought out NinoIna, their only other ally present. With the forces of justice [gathered together](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=13299s), they shared what they knew about the Advent threat with one another. NinoIna revealed that Advent had not only been behind Shakira's death, but had been puppeteering her corpse around to sow fear. The Shining Stars had to act, but they needed to be careful. Gonathon would distract Nerissa while the rest scoped out Shiori's church.

[The most incredible joke ever told](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=13941s)

Alas, while Gonathon pulled off a more than stellar diversion, Immerkind was [unable](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=15003s) to locate the entrance to the secret lair of Advent. Reconvening with the Shining Stars, Gonathon learned that Immerkind had been unable to wrangle Tam into going along with her plans and earned the former queen's ire when she called her unstable to her face. This [jab](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=15730s) actually caused the ornery azure maiden to agree to be bait for the plan, and the forces of justice agreed that they would hatch their plan to infiltrate the church the next day.

While a shroud of darkness had descended upon the once vibrant kingdom, the unshakeable bond between Immerkind and Gonathon remained as strong as ever. Surely if anyone could save the kingdom from the foul machinations of Advent, it was the twin knights of Justice!
