As Lady Bloodflame moved down the roads to try and gather items, Nerissa on her heels, the two chatted.

The sound of [irritation](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8J7aGAe42EE&t=3245) made the Jade Knight turn her head back to her Princess. Hearing her in such tones felt wrong, as though she had slighted the other.

"I don't like not being able to see you on the map."

Immediately Lady Bloodflame explained they had the phones, not understanding the request. Why would she need her on the map when there were other ways of communication?

The silence in response was not comforting in the least.

---

**Forget Roommate**

It was sad to separate from Nerissa, from her Princess. But they were splitting up for the sake of helping others in the dungeons. Still, it pained Lady Bloodflame so to hear the words.

[Focus on your quest.](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8J7aGAe42EE&t=3303)

"Forget about me for a bit. Focus on your sister. Focus on your partner Shiori. And I will see you after."

A heavy feeling washed over the Jade Knight, her eyes fluttering for a bit as she mumbled out an agreement. Fog clouded everything for a moment and as it cleared, she listened to the woman before her speak.

"What a pretty lady!" The knight declared as she walked away, unsure of who she had just been talking to.

Why she had been in her house was beyond her, but it seemed like it was to offer assistance later to make more upgrade stones, which was fine by her books. They _were_ getting harder and harder to make after all.

In the process of doing such upgrades, she realized she was short a few stain souls. Frowning, she looked around her place, spotting the loft above her head.

Surely her [roommate](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8J7aGAe42EE&t=4046) would not mind if she borrowed some. They were roommates after all! They shared!

Stashing away her [pretty ring](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8J7aGAe42EE&t=3928), Lady Bloodflame ascended the ladder and gathered the stains she required, promising to return them later.

She would soon forget to do that too.

---

**Remember**

With the chaotic dungeon behind her and the memory of the apparent twin sisters unlocked, Lady Bloodflame stood outside the entrance, running over things in her head.

Had the heat really been so awful to make her forget something so important? If it was that important, how could she have forgotten it in the first place? Still, the taste was bitter on her tongue.

To distract herself, she addressed her 'sister.' Who apparently was really the sister to Fuwawa. Everyone knew they had similarities, but as of late, the two had been at each other's throats. Yet this memory seemed to soothe them both into knowing who they were.

After assuring Mococo they would talk tomorrow, her head turned to see none other than... than...

[Her Princess.](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8J7aGAe42EE&t=9425)

The wave of remembrance came crashing down on her like a tsunami, almost drowning her as everything floated by out of her control. Each wave was a new thought, a new recollection of events that had transpired. Unfortunately, it came with the bitter reminder of what she had said, how she had acted, and things she had done with Sister Shiori.

Shuddering, she approached Nerissa with a smile, trying to push down the bile at the reminder of being so disgustingly head over heels with Shiori.

Later, Nerissa would approach her again as she put the [ring](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8J7aGAe42EE&t=9425) back on once more. Her own [loyalty](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8J7aGAe42EE&t=9650) was questioned with what she had said. What she had done.

Grunting, Lady Bloodflame simply explained her mouth moved before her mind. That she had mostly felt like she forgot something important. _Very_ important.

---

**Beneath the Milky Twilight (cont.)**

The sun almost sucked the patience out of her. But no sun, not even the galaxy, could stand in the way of love.

Unrelenting, Nerissa waited until the sun gave up. The stars finally rose, one by one, like wingmen standing above her as witnesses of the "Greatest Confession of All Time."

Lady Bloodflame arrived, unsurprised. As if she already knew what Nerissa was about to do.

The maiden handed the rose and ring to her, and with confidence, declared:

["Lady Bloodflame, be mine"](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/4anxJRoJo4s?t=19610)

The Jade Knight did not need to think. It was as if she was waiting for this very moment—for the maiden to finally admit her feelings. That she did not only think of her as a "roommate" but someone far more special.

Unabashedly, Lady Bloodflame leaned in and excitedly smothered her in kisses. Nerissa felt the warmth rising in her chest, so much that she kissed back with her [tongue](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4anxJRoJo4s&t=19625).
