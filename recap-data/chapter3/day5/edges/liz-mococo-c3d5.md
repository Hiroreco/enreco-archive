"MY SISTER!!"
"Helloooo, my dearest Mococo~!"

[The most difficult days start much like any other.](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/K3N6S6rIgS0?t=2633)

The little witch happily greeted her sister, as usual. Lady Bloodflame needed some help with directions, and Mococo was happy to provide!

Once the topic turned to the upcoming dungeon, though, Mococo expressed her concerns to her sister, her worry with the fact that she has to do this with Fuwawa, and Elizabeth's response was... not as comforting as either would have hoped. She had thought the two doggos bickered as siblings or friends do, and that didn't land well, despite her good intentions. Her only sister was Lady Bloodflame after all, and she doesn't get along with Fuwawa at ALL. It didn't help that the woman of the hour just so happened to barge into this conversation, and Mococo wasn't nearly as willing as her sibling to talk to her right now.

["Speak of the devil."](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/K3N6S6rIgS0?t=2745)

Lady Bloodflame did get them all to be able to train together, though. It was honestly really fun! Her sister was a really good warrior, and sparring with her helped her feel stronger! Even if there was still an elephant in the room, she wasn't fully addressing... it was fine.

It wasn't too long until it was time for them to head out after, and things seemed... bright! The two siblings shared [the second cutest thing you'll see all week](https://www.youtube.com/live/K3N6S6rIgS0?t=5999), and Mococo was slowly getting over herself.

This cheeriness wouldn't last.

[She was burning. She was burning, and you were laughing, Lady Bloodflame.](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/K3N6S6rIgS0?t=7189)

This suffering wouldn't end. With Mococo and Elizabeth linked together, [they shared all damage taken](https://www.youtube.com/live/K3N6S6rIgS0?t=6677). You'd think this would mean she would do all she can to protect her little sister, but at every turn, Lady Bloodflame was giggling, cackling... empty laughs. Laughing out of nervousness, or perhaps at the absurdity of the situation itself. A blue husk. Mococo could feel it. She had noticed her appearance changing, but she didn't know it was this serious. It was hard to recognize her, it felt so, so wrong...

And as they died. And died. And died. They were slipping away from each other more and more.

Yet, they still loved each other. A familial bond like theirs isn't easy to break. They were siblings for as long as they could remember. They could make it through this too! And they did so, putting an end to the Prisoner of Duality and conquering the labyrinth together.

...

But what about the time from before they could remember...? As important memories were regained, Mococo started to realize... that Elizabeth was nowhere to be found in them.

[Sisters not by blood, but by heart](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/K3N6S6rIgS0?t=10108)

She panicked. She... she recognized she was being greedy. A lot of people don't even have one, but Mococo wanted two sisters...! With tears in her eyes, she ran away from the scene, needing to have space to herself.

Elizabeth's current state wasn't helping. She was relapsing over many things regarding Nerissa Juliet and Sister Shiori, but in her heart... she was still Mococo's sister. She could only hope the fuzzy one would still feel the same...

["...it does make me... no. This should make me happy."](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/8J7aGAe42EE?t=9477)
