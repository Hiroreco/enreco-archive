When it came to Shiori, Lady Bloodflame wasn't quite sure how to take her still. Despite three different Libestals visited and time well spent, there was always a sense of unease about the other. Something she couldn't quite place. Yet the only thing the other had ever done wrong was write ink on paper, assessing the knight as some monster willing to trap Nerissa.

But right before the dungeon, she felt that heavy fog on her head once again. Something... important felt forgotten, felt lost.

[My Partner.](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8J7aGAe42EE&t=3303)

Immediately she needed to get things done for her partner, Shiori. Upgrade stones had to be obtained and she was _behind_. Immediately she called the other to apologize, begging for [forgiveness](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8J7aGAe42EE&t=4233). The moment the other requested she stay on the ground, the knight dropped down like it was life or death.

Despite the other denying her for the rose, Lady Bloodflame passed it over in the trades with the stones.

[Who were you?](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8J7aGAe42EE&t=4424)

A sense of something wrong made Lady Bloodflame question who she was as Shiori stood before her, asking the knight who the dual-haired woman was. Everything was confusing.

Shiori sighed heavily at the sight, passing over handfuls of coins to help upgrade Lady Bloodflame's weapon. A curious question entered her mind as she asked what the most [beautiful place](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8J7aGAe42EE&t=5003) was around the area.

After a few moments of fumbling out nonsense, Lady Bloodflame responded with the arena. Turning to show the other, she knew a shortcut. Immediately she jumped into the water despite the commands from Sister Shiori, her partner, telling her to [stop](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8J7aGAe42EE&t=5050).

With shenanigans finished and weapons properly upgraded, the team took on the trial. Lady Bloodflame split from Shiori almost the entire time. Once successful and outside, distaste hit the tongue of the knight.

Had she seriously been all but licking the shoes of the one she had the ick about?
