Despite prior altercations, Bijou the Stained was set to join Gonathon and Nerissa in completing the Labyrinth of Duality.

During preparation, and after being told by Prisoner Zero in a quest requirement to "befriend a bitter enemy", Bijou contemplated only for a moment before deciding to call Gonathon.

[Cool. You're my friend now](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FJNKv92ZWdM&t=6289s)

The call lasted barely ten seconds. Perhaps he was slightly caught off guard by this, yet it was enough to get Gonathon to believe it to be true.

Satisfied, Bijou professed to the Prisoner that she had successfully befriended her enemy. But whether she meant it or not was another matter entirely. ["I didn't actually mean it,"](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FJNKv92ZWdM&t=6323s) she confessed, "I am free to not mean things that I don't mean."

Following the completion of today's dungeon, Gonathon crossed paths with Bijou before departing and asked if she needed anything. Bijou, uncertain of his inquiry, offered only that she may "perhaps" need something later. It was not entirely clear what she meant by this, though perhaps the vagueness was deliberate.

Later in the day, the two met briefly once more, though under unfortunate circumstances. Spotted ascending from the Church basement by none other than Gonathon and Tam, the scene shifted almost immediately. It became clear that Bijou had [different plans in mind](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FJNKv92ZWdM&t=12938s) for the knight all along.
