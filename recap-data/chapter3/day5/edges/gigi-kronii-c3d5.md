Gonathon G was no stranger to amnesia, having not only been afflicted by it twice, but also having suffered through the pain of having two of his loved ones forget him entirely. When Tam Gandr, his beloved queen, also seemed to succumb to the abyss of memory loss, it was too much for Gonathon to bear. But as their current adventure neared its end, the humble knight seemed no closer to saving his queen... and he began to wonder if she even wanted to be saved at all.

Upon [first meeting](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=2950s) for that day, the pressing matters of Advent overshadowed any plans the knight may have had to remove the stain hat from Tam. After relaying the intel he had received on the meeting of Nerissa and Shiori from Immerkind, Tam pointed out that [kindness](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=3513s) did not always mean someone was truly good. The two were soon interrupted by Nerissa, whose haughty attitude over Tam's lower level annoyed the queen, and after a shallow descent into frenzied ramblings, Tam left Gonathon to attend to her own duties.

Queen and knight would not reunite until much later, after which Gonathon had gone through Shiori's horrific ritual with seemingly no memories of what had happened. Tam, for her part, was very despondent, wondering if there was [any point](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=9958s) to contributing to upgrading her skills or the guild given the short time left in their current journey. This reflection caused Gonathon to become dejected as well, and he despaired that he hadn't done nearly enough to assist his queen. Tam was incredulous at this notion, arguing that Gonathon had always given his all for her sake. Most treasured of all was the trust he placed in Tam. In the past, the queen had constantly been beset on all sides by adversaries, and in those times, as now, Gonathon was always at her side.

[Trust between wolves](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=10160s)

While Gonathon once more attempted to broach the subject of removing the stain from Tam's head, the knight was interrupted by a most disturbing sight: the possessed corpse of Shakira! Fleeing the scene, Gonathon was closely followed by Tam, who [slapped](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=10514s) him out of his hysteria.

Following a strange exchange over the musculature of Fia's body, Tam once more brought Gonathon to Shiori's church, where the two witnessed the members of Advent [floating out](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=11122s) of the festering dungeon beneath. Shakira's ghost was also present, and the apparition chased Gonathon and Tam out of the church and into town. Gonathon once more spiraled into panic, screaming that he had sinned.

It was here that Tam reappeared, this time lacking her stain completely. [Somehow](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=11618s), Nerissa and Lady Bloodflame had convinced her to take it off and converse with Gonathon. Despite his queen possessing her memories once more, Gonathon could not ignore just how melancholic she had become. Realizing something had to change, Gonathon allowed Tam to renounce her claim to the throne. But in a tender moment, Tam reassured Gonathon that even if she was no longer his queen, she would always be [his friend](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=12199s). Now standing as equals, Gonathon proclaimed he would always be there for Tam, finally lifting the dark shroud that had engulfed the two.

[You were and always will be wonderful](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=12325s)

The two had little time to indulge in further pleasantries, as the true extent of Advent's evil soon became all too apparent. Upon [reuniting](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=13160s) with Tam, who had chosen to don the ushanka hat that Gonathon had given her, Gonathon brought together the other members of the Shining Stars. The group shared what they knew about the schemes Advent was up to and put together a plan of action: Gonathon would distract Nerissa while Tam and Immerkind infiltrated the church.

[The creation of the forces of Justice!](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=13587s)

Alas, Tam and Immerkind were unable to locate Shiori's eldritch chambers, in part because Immerkind was [unable](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=15397s) to wrangle the ornery azure maiden. Despite rejecting her royal status, Tam had in many ways become even more demanding, requesting a castle be constructed for her panda. The Shining Stars realized their best course of action was to infiltrate the church the next day, and with Immerkind and Gonathon already having been put through the ritual, the untouched Tam was deemed the ideal bait, given her status and antagonistic relationship with Shiori.

While Tam was quick to object, Gonathon [reminded](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w2BCfGaek4g&t=15675s) her that she was now their equal, and had to pull her weight just like the rest of them. And with that, the group parted ways. While Gonathon may no longer be the queen's knight, he had become something even greater: Tam Gandr's most trusted friend.
