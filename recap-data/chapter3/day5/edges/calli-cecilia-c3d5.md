Immerkind HAD to deliver the news. Once the knight had found Mordon Ramsay, word of Shakira's body disappearing from Sister Shiori's church quickly started to spread around. Neither of them was able to do their usual kingdom activities the previous day, so they were both unaware of the "hauntings" going on. To them, someone had taken her body away. Again! How despicable!

Now, she's not pointing fingers! But Immerkind begins to [share her suspicions](https://www.youtube.com/live/IpNXgvS3QjM?si=mQ37GWBNK1NoKfiw&t=1016) she has about Shakira's murder, noting to her accused confidant that she's noticed both Sister Shiori and Princess Bijou acting strange as of late. She remarks that she thinks it has something to do with the Curse of Advent that loomed over them a couple of days ago.

To Mordon, Immerkind's theory sounded a little bit over the top. But she couldn't deny that something was up with the town lately, and her logic seems sound enough. There must be some way to bring the truth to light, some way to prove her innocence... maybe they could bring it to court? Although she doesn't have a lawyer.

[But that was of no concern to Immerkind.](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/UBbTSf5A9bc?si=F9wLTZOQEkrPljQ9&t=4509)

The specific roles took a bit of discussing, but Immerkind was determined to hold this trial. She would prove her friend and fellow wolf innocent no matter what, with royalty as her witness! Mordon was relieved to have such a good friend by her side, and the two would cross paths many more times throughout the day. The two of them were in the same dungeon party twice, Mordon [lent Immerkind some money](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IpNXgvS3QjM&t=10879) when she needed it (though with an interest rate), and there was... something else they were together in down the line that neither of them could remember. Strange.

Oh well. What truly mattered was that they had a plan of action. Nothing could now stop the deliverance of swift justice! Or so Mordon hoped, anyway...
