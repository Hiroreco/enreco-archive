Early in the day, Bijou approached Shiori, needing to confess her sins. Feeling that she could only trust Shiori with this, Bijou informs Shiori about [how she was responsible for Shakira's death](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FJNKv92ZWdM&t=1100), and that she intends to further spread chaos across the lands. Bijou also finds that her grudge even extends to Shiori and the rest of Advent, after it seemed that they had replaced her with Lady Bloodflame. Regardless, Shiori assures Bijou that she will be there for her if she needs it.

The two then turn to [discussing their plans after the dungeons](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QB1ZSh0yKuc&t=1650). As Bijou wants to use Shakira's body to continue haunting, and Shiori wants to use it to help open the gate beneath the church, the two coordinate to work together later in the day.

With Shiori having her eyes on Roa for opening the gate, she questions Bijou about this, knowing she has been a confidante of Roa's. [Bijou admits that Roa has been confiding in her](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QB1ZSh0yKuc&t=1710), and that this betrayal would likely break her... and that Bijou would not feel bad about that at all, so she accepts the offer.

The two [meet again](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FJNKv92ZWdM&t=9845) after their completion of the Labyrinth of Duality. They both find that they will side with Nix, neither trusting in Fia. The plans set for Roa later in the day are also reconfirmed.

The two continue checking up on each other until Roa is brought to the church by Nerissa. There, the three bring her down to the basement, with Bijou acting as the spirit of Shakira. They make Roa complete the ritual set up there, with Bijou revealing herself to Roa, before making her suffocate in the wall.

[The Sacrifice](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QB1ZSh0yKuc&t=13135)

The three members of the coven continue this by bringing down three others to be suffocated in the wall. Fuwawa, who they inform of her place alongside them as Advent, as well as Mordon Ramsay and Knight Immerkind, who would not remember their experience.

After briefly splitting, Shiori and Bijou [reunite one last time with the rest of Advent](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FJNKv92ZWdM&t=14450). The coven discusses their accomplishments of the day, giving a rallying cry of "A-D-V-E-N-T!" It seems they also have plans for the next day...
