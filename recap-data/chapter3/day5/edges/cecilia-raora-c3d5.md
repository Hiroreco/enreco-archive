The day starts fresh between Roa and Immerkind. During the past days, grouping up to tackle a dungeon has been a grueling task. They never found the opportunity to do so, and perhaps they never would.

Not in this version of reality, at least.

Even so, Immerkind called Roa to inquire about [an activity that she is familiar with](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rFmdOarYXuE&t=940). Roa's expertise: The Gaps, the Barriers, and the Walls of this reality. And the countless ways she can break through any form of physical hindrance to truth and freedom.

Iphania's knight instructed the pink smith to use a magical feather to maneuver herself on top of the platform she created to reach the Gap. After falling dozens of times, Roa curses into the wind as she blames the gods for stopping her. Immerkind interjected that this is a ['skill issue'](https://www.youtube.com/live/rFmdOarYXuE?si=oIO1MXndQ7sDeEK8&t=1129) on the smith's part.

The green knight continued to hyperventilate with continuous failed attempts of the beeg cat. Before long, Roa lands inside the castle walls where the Gap is. Roa also felt a surge of vicarious pride when Immerkind found this area. The leader of the Illumination grants Immerkind a spot to [join her secret society](https://www.youtube.com/live/rFmdOarYXuE?si=q4a1H1i3RPZVmMn8&t=1179).

Lastly, both of them [banter again about](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rFmdOarYXuE&t=1350) the lost opportunities they had to go into any of the Labyrinths.
