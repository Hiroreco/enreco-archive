What little certainty Gonathon still held onto began crumbling apart.

The feelings he once tried so hard to preserve now felt weaker than ever, slowly fading toward complete collapse. Even [Immerkind](https://www.youtube.com/live/uLitDJpSaKQ?si=PcInvWbxOMdQHEOh&t=11720) recognized how unhealthy this attachment had become for him. Perhaps it was finally time to stop deluding himself.

Perhaps it was finally time to let go.
