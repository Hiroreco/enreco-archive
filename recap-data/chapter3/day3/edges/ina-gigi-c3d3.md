Throughout their journeys in the various Libestals Gonathon and NinoIna had crossed paths many times, never drawing too close but nonetheless providing aid to one another when times became tough. More often than not these tough times would be the result of the machinations of the vile Hot Pink One, but today circumstances would draw these two together in an unexpected way.

As had been the case the past two days, initially Gonathon and NinoIna exchanged minor pleasantries, usually consisting of reminiscing about their days at the Knight's Academy. While details about this supposed school were scarce, it seemed to be an important element of the history between the two. Aside from dredging up old memories, Gonathon busied himself with [providing upgrade stones](https://youtu.be/uLitDJpSaKQ?t=1044) and other materials for building up their faction base, as NinoIna had been one of the rare adventurers who had stayed loyal to the royal feline.

NinoIna provided Gonathon with some powerful charms, finally giving the knight some much needed defense against the vile Stains assailing the kingdom. Then, together with Bijou and Roa, the two Shining Stars embarked into the Labyrinth of Emotion. The dungeon proved simple enough to conquer, with NinoIna even treated to [a spirited exchange](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GzBAUkX8-r0&t=8110) between Gonathon and Bijou once the latter had reacquired her memories.

[No one can top NinoIna's charm](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uLitDJpSaKQ&t=4811s)

Destiny had much more in store for Gonathon and NinoIna however, as following their dungeon crawl they prepared to attend Sister Shiori's religious ceremony. Upon receiving the hymn Shiori passed out, NinoIna made a shocking discovery: the hymn would summon the baleful forces of [DMCA](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GzBAUkX8-r0&t=10857)! Racing to Gonathon and the other Shining Stars, Ina [implored](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uLitDJpSaKQ&t=10685s) them to not sing the hymn, lest they be corrupted and become heretics.

This quick-thinking proved vital, as during [the ceremony](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uLitDJpSaKQ&t=11178s) the initial suspicions casted on Shiori's intentions by Ina allowed Gonathon and the others to discover the full extent of the witch's machinations, and in turn saved Mordan Ramsay from being falsely convicted of murdering Shakira.

Despite her desire to live a simple life, NinoIna's heroism on the third day saved not only Gonathon, but the entire Shining Stars as a whole.
