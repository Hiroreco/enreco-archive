The day started seemingly maintaining the status quo, with Gonathon's attempts at conversing with Bijou instantly rebuffed with [huffs and indignation](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uLitDJpSaKQ&t=944s). Despite all of Gonathon's previous attempts at getting through to the stubborn princess, she remained steadfast in her utter opposition to reconciling with him. But as fate would have it, cracks would soon begin to form in her anatagonistic facade.

Early in the day Gonathon met with Bijou at her home. It was there that the knight met Bijou's pet, Lava Chicken. Breaking out into [song](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uLitDJpSaKQ&t=2251s), Gonathon was shocked to learn that Bijou also knew of the Lava Chicken hymn, an ancient tune passed down from Ancient Libestal. Recalling their time in the old kingdom, Gonathon once more implored Bijou to consider all they had gone through. The princess however would not be swayed, biting back that it was foolish to base one's principles and viewpoints in what had happened before, and that only the present should matter.

And in the present, all Bijou saw before her was an enemy that served one that the princess felt had scorned her. Unable to break the impasse, Gonathon departed.

[Why should the past matter?](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uLitDJpSaKQ&t=2328s)

As luck would have it though, Gonathon and Bijou would soon find themselves comrades-in-arms. Following the humble knight's schemes to set up Bae the Peasant on a one-on-one dungeon romp with Immerkind, Gonathon was left with Roa, NinoIna, and Bijou as his questing companions. Despite any apprehension Bijou might have held against Gonathon they proved to be capable partners as they braved the Labryinth of Emotion. Upon slaying the Prisoner of Emotion the party was subjected to a new set of [memories](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uLitDJpSaKQ&t=7450s), this time being none other than Bijou herself.

[Why'd she go through the doggy door?](#embed:https://youtu.be/uLitDJpSaKQ?t=7653)

The memories revealed that Bijou was no princess, but rather the Gem of Emotion, a catankerous girl who was sought after by others due to the way she affected their emotions. Tiring of what she saw as other people using her, Bijou preferred the company of her other rocks and had lived a solitary existence. Viewing these memories, Gonathon concluded that many of Bijou's problems, including her corruption were born from her loneliness. Perhaps there was still time to [save her?](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3iXshLxO3-E&t=11940)

The humble knight's [initial attempts](https://youtu.be/uLitDJpSaKQ?t=8164) at getting through to the emotional gem were met with abject failure, as despite reminding her of the past experiences they had had together and proclaiming that she did have [good in her heart](https://youtu.be/uLitDJpSaKQ?t=7950), Bijou was quick to reply that they just wished to use her, as everyone else in her past had used her. Refusing to give up, Gonathon was joined by Immerkind and the two pursued Bijou, but alas the gem made a quick getaway, leaving the knights befuddled as to how to get through.

All was not lost however, as after a bit of reflection Bijou decided to make a wager with Gonathon. If he could solve a riddle, then she would consider [becoming his friend](https://youtu.be/uLitDJpSaKQ?t=9995). Solving the riddle however would prove to be much easier said than done.

[What have I got in my pocket?](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3iXshLxO3-E&t=13780)

Despite Bijou's riddle proving to be insurmountable at a glance, Gonathon remained resolute that he would be able to succeed and win over the ornery gem girl. Perhaps with Immerkind at his side the rock's riddle could be solved, but time was of the essence as there remained only two days left before the final confrontation.
