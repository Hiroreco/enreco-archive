**Tension**

With the chaos of Shakira's funeral, Lady Bloodflame watched from the sidelines as Ramsay declared her innocence with Gonathon backing her up. Listening, she stepped forward to her fellow knight to ask some questions.

After all, they had a camaraderie in being knights together from the very start. How could she not trust Gonathon knowing that they had to abide by the rules of knighthood?

But when being told that her fellow knight has spoken about killing her faction, that moment of doubt enters Lady Bloodflame's head. A rush of thoughts hits her all at once as she processes that information. However, making judgment calls in a moment of high emotion isn't the right call.

After a brief pause, the red-haired knight turns to her comrade and pulls them aside to ask if this is true or not.

[Maybe he was just having a bad day](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/rg_rZ8reBVA?si=qei9FYAh1GMaor4L&t=11754)

With a small back-and-forth between her fellow knight and her princess, there is an initial wave of relief when Gonathon is thought to be right about Ramsay, but it's swept away by Nerissa's distrust. To quickly resolve their issues, Lady Bloodflame suggests they go for a drink to talk over their differences.

The entire conversation leaves her with a sense of unease. But with trust in her heart, she believes they can work out their differences.

---

**You look unwell**

Towards the end of the day, Lady Bloodflame found herself being called by Gonathon, following them along the paths through the city of Libestal to a small corner at the back wall. Above loomed a tree that she followed the other up, questioning why this was all necessary for a little chat. It seemed so excessive to go so far out of the way when a simple telecommunication device would have worked just as well.

But the idea of sitting up in a tree for the first time seemed charming enough that she climbed it, questioning the small child along the way before the two settled in on a large branch.

All of this for some fresh air with words of concern from Gonathon. Once again she was being told she didn't look like herself. That she looked... unusual.

["Homochromia? Heterophia? Homoph-"](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/rg_rZ8reBVA?si=2jRlzsVofqqO3fDB&t=12842)

Again, it was nothing more than a lot of traveling, work, and the loss of her close friend, Shakira.

And again, her eye was brought up. Sighing in exhaustion on this topic, she explained it away once again, waving her hand as she blew it off.

Then the ring was questioned. A simple little gift from Nerissa Juliet. Without hesitation, she handed it over when requested. It was nothing more than just a ring given to her. Quietly she watched as it was inspected before being handed back over.

Flipping the script, Lady Bloodflame asked if Gonathon was okay. Frowning, she listened to the worries of her friend. With so much going on, how was she to keep checking in on everyone to ensure they were okay? With all the travel on top of it, she knew that Libestal wasn't a place because of being a city or a kingdom.

It was Libestal because of the people around her.

[From all of you](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rg_rZ8reBVA&t=13144)

Gonathon paused briefly, looking away before explaining how much Lady Bloodflame meant to them. As a friend. As a fellow knight.

Smiling, Lady Bloodflame assured him that her path, though unusual with the Rebellion, was simply to keep a knightly promise to protect Lady Juliet and her sister Mococo. She was still a knight.

There was a sense of relief talking about things with a comrade. But with that came the honest truth: she was not herself in looks or actions.

Perhaps a night of rest was required.
