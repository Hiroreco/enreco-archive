As Immerkind went about her day, she encountered the young princess, Koseki Bijou, who was currently battling an illness. Concerned for the girl’s condition, the knight insisted that she drink ["strawberry" milk](https://www.youtube.com/live/gDiqP1BVNXY?t=2750s) so that she could grow strong and healthy once more.

Bijou, however, was no princess, and that revelation quickly shifted the mood. Having been exposed, she immediately grew defensive, her distrust toward Immerkind surfacing once again. Deep down, Bijou still carried the lingering sadness of the knight choosing Iphania over her. Though Immerkind tried to reassure the gem girl that she truly considered her a friend, the wounds of doubt ran too deep, eventually causing Bijou to flee instead.
