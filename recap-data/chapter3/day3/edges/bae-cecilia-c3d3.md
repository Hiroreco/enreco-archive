"Oh Bae." 

These words perhaps rang through countless watchers as we witnessed The Peasant Rat be smitten with Immerkind once again. 

The Peasant and the Kind Knight's relationship has certainly moved forward the past few days and culminated in... a loop back to the beginning. 

The two shared sweet moments of trust as [they fought side by side](https://youtu.be/JAibEhH7R1E?t=7588) in the dungeon, almost falling back into what they were before prior to Bae's confession during their time in the past.

However, due to Bae's delusion and Immerkind's honest but unfortunately vague descriptions of her own woes, a misunderstandidng was fated to happen:

[That Bae might have a chance against a cat plush.](#embed:https://youtu.be/JAibEhH7R1E?t=8645)


Undoubtedly, no matter what kind of relationship the two have, Immerkind cares about Bae as she warned her against trusting Nix and Advent and pleaded with her to change sides.

Bae was unable to give an answer to Immerkind's pleads, torn between the camaraderie of her fellow guildmates and her infatuation for the maiden before her.

Ultimately, Bae herself admitted she must move on from Immerkind... while accidentally confessing to her in the [process](https://youtu.be/JAibEhH7R1E?t=12832).
