[“Let’s make a shop!”](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-KfXS2FJl2o&t=249) Fuwawa excitedly declared at the start of her third day. Watching everyone around her run their own businesses made the fluffy doggo want one of her own too. It looked fun, after all—and more importantly, it made money!

Back in the previous journey, Fuwawa _had_ owned a shop already, a cozy little café. But things were different this time. She was no longer a cook, meaning she had to work with what her current profession specialized in instead.

Upgrade stones.

Now, setting up a proper business turned out to be a bit more difficult than she expected. Fuwawa, being Fuwawa, got distracted, confused, and sidetracked more than once along the way, but eventually, after much effort, her little shop finally came together.

[Roa was scary... but my shop is ready!](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-KfXS2FJl2o&t=856)

With the storefront complete, she immediately got to work preparing upgrade stones to sell. The process, however, was not exactly smooth. Between crafting attempts and magical mishaps, Fuwawa managed to [blow herself up](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-KfXS2FJl2o&t=3623) multiple times throughout the day.

A normal person might have been discouraged.

Fuwawa simply kept going.

Eventually, she regrouped with Calli, Bijou, Shiori, and Mococo to challenge the [Labyrinth of Emotion.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-KfXS2FJl2o&t=4386) After overcoming the dungeon together, the party successfully recovered the lost memories of [Koseki Bijou.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-KfXS2FJl2o&t=6615)

By the time everything was over, most of the day had already slipped away. Still, Fuwawa felt satisfied with everything she had accomplished. Her shop was running, she had spent time adventuring with her friends, and despite all the explosions, things had turned out pretty well in the end.

So, with a cheerful heart and a sense of fulfillment, Fuwawa happily decided to call it a day.

A short one, perhaps.

But a good one nonetheless.
