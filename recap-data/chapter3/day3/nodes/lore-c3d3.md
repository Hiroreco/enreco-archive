#### Main Story

**Objective**

With the glass compass in hand, the heroes are able to reach Cell 0414 and enter the **Labyrinth of Emotion,** at the end of which they battle the Prisoner of Emotion and obtain the _Lens of Revealing._

Peering through the lens is overwhelming, but it reveals where the Outsider went, as well as the location of the entrance to Nullspace.

**Discoveries**

However, the heroes are unable to pass through the entrance, and it's unclear how the Outsider managed to do so.

The lens also reveals that a cursed aura hangs over five of the heroes.

Consulting Faust again, he explains that the Song of Memories can reveal how the Outsider gained access to Nullspace. The prisoner of Cell 1121 knows this song, but a bottomless pit blocks the way.

The heroes will need an _amulet of levitation._

Some heroes negotiate with the exceedingly rude Star Speaker Maaz, who makes many unreasonable demands, but eventually gives them the amulet.

Others seek Nix's aid to sneak into Maaz's room and steal the amulet, though they must solve a difficult magical lock puzzle to open the chest.
