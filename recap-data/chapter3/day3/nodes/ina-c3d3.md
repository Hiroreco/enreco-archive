Despite enjoying the supplier lifestyle over the past two days, upon waking on the third day, NinoIna found herself wanting to return to her jeweler roots. That, and the looming presence of an inevitable monopoly, swayed Ina to change professions, becoming a jeweler once more. There was only one objection to this. (see [Ina-Mococo](#edge:ina-mococo))

["Oh yeah? Well watch this!"](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GzBAUkX8-r0&t=314s)

Starting a new job meant returning to square one and learning the basics. Luckily for Ina, this wasn't her first time as a Jeweler. She quickly caught herself up to speed, with the efficiency expected of a Knight such as herself. And just in time for the dungeon of the day: the Labyrinth of Emotion.

Alongside Gonathon, Bijou, and Roa, Ina made her way through the trials the dungeon presented: reassuring a depressed stain, painting pictures with ink, getting her anger out on some stains, and... [cookies?!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GzBAUkX8-r0&t=5910s)

After claiming victory against the Prisoner of Emotion and finding a secret room containing a [music disc](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GzBAUkX8-r0&t=7450s), Ina witnessed the memories of her sister, [Koseki Bijou](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GzBAUkX8-r0&t=7626s).

With this victory, Ina managed to claim the Lens of revealing and used it to seek answers. What she saw shocked her so much, she couldn't even find the [right words to say](www.youtube.com/watch?v=GzBAUkX8-r0&t=7825s). In fact, it shocked her so much that she blacked out.

But more shocking than that is what she witnessed when she came to. It seemed that the ideals of Gonathon and Bijou had clashed, and Ina and Roa had [front row seats](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GzBAUkX8-r0&t=8425s). (see [Bijou-Gigi](#embed:bijou-gigi))

Speaking to Faust, Ina learnt of the Amulet of Levitation, which would allow her to bypass a lava lake, gaining access to the next dungeon. The Amulet could be procured through Maaz of the star speakers, but Nix suggested they attempt to claim one through _stealing_ one from her instead. Ina quickly shut down Nix's blasphemous proposal and headed to Maaz to see whether she would be willing to give one to her.

Fortunately for Ina, it seemed Maaz was the person she was looking for; unfortunately, it seemed NinoIna was the  _last_ person Maaz was looking for. Maaz deemed NinoIna unworthy of her prized amulet and tasked Ina with bringing her a few things. First, it was lapis lazuli, then upgrade stones, then charms, becoming more and more insufferable as it went along. But fortunately, Ina had to put a pause on things for a second.

Because, while on her quest for upgrade stones, Ina was notified of a gathering at the church. It was when she got there, however, that Ina began to question the ordeal. Demanding payment for so-called "prayer guides", giving very little information on the proceedings, and worst of all...

[Blasphemous!](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GzBAUkX8-r0&t=10802s)

Fearful of the church's intentions, Ina decided to step back from the sermon and confided in Gonathon and Immerkind about her worries over the ordeal. (see [Gigi-Ina](#edge:gigi-ina))

Wanting to know more, Ina chose to attend the sermon in secret, using an invisibility potion she had fished up prior, and with the aid of her sister, who covered for her when her cover was close to being [blown](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GzBAUkX8-r0&t=11266s). But the potion ran out too quickly, and Ina had to dash. Returning with more potions, Ina learnt that the entire thing was an attempt to blame Mordon Ramsay for the death of Shakira. 

After a few [close calls](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GzBAUkX8-r0&t=11698s), Ina managed to sneak away and collect her thoughts before continuing on her quest for the Amulet of Levitation. She gave over the charms and was immediately met with demands for Gourmet Savoury Fish Soup. After handing the soup to Maaz—and avoiding any "spillage"—Ina finally acquired the amulet before submitting some charms to the community center as her last act of the day.
