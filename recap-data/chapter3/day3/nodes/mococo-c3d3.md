Something was wrong.

From the moment Mococo woke up for her third day as the best (and only) jeweler in Libestal, she was already a little frustrated. How come Sister Shiori and Nerissa seemed... [a little unsatisfied with her work?](https://www.youtube.com/live/AQ80hyGfzxI?si=g8h3CsYbwcXO0UTO&t=519) She doesn't understand; they can't get their charms from anywhere else, and she puts so much effort into them. What right do they have to complain? So what if they're still "Basic"?! She's giving them really good deals, y'know!!

But it's not like it'll be a problem, aha. The charm monopoly is all hers! She won't have to worry about silly things like market space.

...wait, what's that she's overhearing?

...

What do you mean, there's another one? (see: [NinoIna-Mococo](#edge:ina-mococo))

The squabble with NinoIna left her FURIOUS. She focused all her efforts from then on to crafting more and more charms, which she would put up for sale later! Their quality would be THE BEST!! Luckily for her, she was able to strike a deal with her sister Elizabeth where they would buy and trade each other's crafts. And, on her way to her next customer, her mood was quickly lifted as she obtained [a new canine friend!](https://www.youtube.com/live/AQ80hyGfzxI?si=HapvXJluOFOkywM0&t=1533)

With WOOF the Wolf now in tow, Mococo met up with Mordon and Bijou, wearing a smile on her face. She was quick to advertise to customers she's already provided services for. They know of the quality of her work already. What could go wrong? Well... the pink-haired lady revealed that, while she was interested in charms, she was a bit short on savings right now, so she planned to start a food truck near the entrance of Terminus to earn cash for the time being. Mococo had no problems with this and was eager to do business with her again once the time came!

And then Bijou revealed that she had also changed her job to that of a jeweler.

[**WHAT THE HEEEEECK!!!**](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/AQ80hyGfzxI?si=1H0lgpKbm0FxdF0k&t=1629)

Despite Bijou being one of Mococo's coven allies, she was NOT having this. REVELATION!!! GET OUT OF HERE!!! LOOK AT HER CHARMS, SHE'S A VERY HIGH LEVEL!!! GET HER CHARMS, NOT BIJOU'S!!! That was all she lashed out with as she, once again, fled the scene.

After that, it was time to gather resources for the dungeon to come and, more importantly, make more charms. While she did so, she had a [small conversation](https://www.youtube.com/live/AQ80hyGfzxI?si=6in9i3lX1Ep-4Zp2&t=2379) with Gonathon where she discussed her incredible magic abilities!! Although she was a little bit annoyed when Gonathon compared magicians and witches. Magicians are FRAUDS!!! And then the conversation turned to Fia, and eventually, a guy called Santa Claus? Mococo doesn't know who that is, but she wants gifts now. From Gonathon. Give her presents.

Once she split off from him, she met up with Knight Immerkind to mine together. The green one was DESPERATELY trying to get her to answer her phone call. She had been trying to do so for the last thirty minutes, unaware of her signature spell.

[It's very effective magic, you know.](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/AQ80hyGfzxI?si=bl1Y8Dnk7alMfdeZ&t=2932)

Finally, it was time for the little witch to brave the daily big adventure. But... forming a party this time around [wasn't super easy](https://www.youtube.com/live/AQ80hyGfzxI?si=uzav9sctDuldR1GL&t=4283). She had agreed to go with Sister Shiori, but she also wanted to go with her own sister! Unfortunately, the knight was already partying with Nerissa Juliet, and her current party had four people in it. She had to choose one or the other. Lady Bloodflame wasn't super keen on letting her around Mordon Ramsay, but... a promise was a promise.

The sisters went their own ways as Mococo joined Sister Shiori, Mordon Ramsay, and Fuwawa in the Labyrinth of Emotion. There were many puzzles along the way, but the highlights for her were definitely the many emotion-themed rooms along the way! Their party would work through the perils of the heart, and together, they recovered the memories of the Imprisoned Jewel, [Koseki Bijou.](https://www.youtube.com/live/AQ80hyGfzxI?si=ZjVBjvmjYvj-QZgf&t=6673)

After a quick encounter with the incomprehensible immediately afterward, Mococo collected her thoughts and marched forward. After doing some more quests, she set up her shop and called it a night in her home.

Tomorrow is another day! They would see the strength of her products in time... and, yes! 1000 coins for ONE charm IS cheap!!! TRUST!!!
