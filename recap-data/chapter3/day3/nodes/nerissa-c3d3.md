**PAINSTAKINGLY GRINDING**

The schedule was set to a T. Nerissa Juliet, as disciplined as she was, intended to follow her routine since the beginning: rise early, do some [side quests](https://youtu.be/LJoWhfyEV1Q?t=1425), complete the main quest, roam around, and explore more secrets hidden in this world.

From time to time, perhaps, mingle with other heroes, allies, and rivals alike.

It was without question to amass more riches. After all, the economy was hard these days; she couldn't even get past 10,000 coins. Man, am I [so broke!](https://youtu.be/LJoWhfyEV1Q?t=1734)

---

**The Labyrinth of ~~Emotions~~ Buttons**

The time to conquer today's [dungeon](https://youtu.be/LJoWhfyEV1Q?t=4483) had come. Nerissa was ruminating on whether to go with the Coven or with Lady Bloodflame alone. With the assistance of the voices in her head, she decided to go with her loyal knight alone.

And so the couple went on their way. They were greeted by fancy crystal puzzles with buttons, and a chandelier with more buttons. And then another crystal puzzle with... you guessed it right, more buttons.

Finally, they entered another room. A vast room with halls seemed carved entirely from marble. Doors of different colors decorated every wall, each leading to a chamber embodying different emotions. But what caught Nerissa Juliet's attention the most was the large [throne](https://youtu.be/LJoWhfyEV1Q?t=5145) at the center of it all.

She attempted to sit on it, but alas, she was too small to even reach the seat. Even so, she turned to her knight and inquired if she suited the throne. The knight, as expected, indulged her vanity. "Very suited," she said. As if the throne was made for her alone.

Ah, yes, the throne in Lady Bloodflame's heart... it now belonged to the Witch alone. (see [Liz-Nerissa](#edge:liz-nerissa))

---

**I SAW THE BODY! HIPS DON'T LIE!**

As there were no other pressing matters to deal with for the day, Nerissa Juliet continued exploring the kingdom. Eventually, she received an announcement about a memorial service for the late Shining Star, Shakira.

The Raven One made haste to the [church](https://youtu.be/LJoWhfyEV1Q?t=11064). A few of the heroes were already there, except for a very important person. Someone who was supposedly most dear to the deceased knight, her beloved, Roa Pandora.

Strange. Was it too traumatic for Roa that she couldn't even attend her own wife's funeral? Looking past the church, she noticed the widowed hero [hiding](https://youtu.be/LJoWhfyEV1Q?t=11185), quite reluctant to show up because of her aversion to sheep.

Nerissa Juliet entered the sacred hall, walked around, and to her surprise...

[THE BODY OF SHAKIRA LAID INSIDE A CHEST!](#embed:https://youtu.be/LJoWhfyEV1Q?t=11234)

Alarmed, she told Lady Bloodflame of her discovery and walked out sobbing in distress over what she had just witnessed. Roa asked if she was okay, and again disclosed the horrible revelation. The widow hurried back inside to investigate, but when she reached the altar, the body was nowhere to be found.

Was Nerissa seeing things? Did she truly see Shakira's body?

She nearly admitted it was probably just her imagination, but Lady Bloodflame defended her and confirmed it was there before somebody "stole" it for the second time.

Despite the commotion about such a sacrilegious act, they still proceeded with the service.

Boom Shakalah kalah, hips don't lie.

**You're going down, GONathon (Snow)**

A sense of disgust loomed whenever Nerissa saw Gonathon. Her blood boiled every time _that_ gremlin approached Lady Bloodflame and tried to convince her to follow the Code of Chivalry. He was a thorn in their relationship, a weed that needed to be pulled out.

And so, to carry out this plan, she pretended to be amicable to him, even inviting him to a ["friendly duel."](https://youtu.be/LJoWhfyEV1Q?t=13846)

Soon, he would fall into her trap.

**COLORED IN SHADES OF GREY**

Nerissa Juliet was rather pleased with today's turn of events, so it was only fitting to write about it.

The maiden entered the Library, took up her quill, and began writing in her [journal](https://youtu.be/LJoWhfyEV1Q?t=14582).

Taking a moment to recall all the most notable accomplishments for the day, she wrote about her advancement in her career, as well as her relationship with Lady Bloodflame, and how together, they were able to conquer the Labyrinth of Emotions with ease. Noting how "well-coordinated" they were and how the lady knight was apparently, ridiculously submissive to her.

Yet, despite the victory, something in the back of her mind [gnawed](https://youtu.be/LJoWhfyEV1Q?t=14892) at her.

Different emotions exude different colors, right? Anger flares up in red, melancholy swims in blue, disgust crawls in green, and joy leaps in yellow.

Conquering the labyrinth should have had an effect on her. Koseki Bijou is the Gem of Emotions, after all. She should be able to influence those around her. Yet to Nerissa, there was none.

Anger, melancholy, disgust, and joy—these various emotions, both beautiful and tender—they just stood still in monochrome. Desensitized.

Nerissa Juliet wondered. Was it because she had now become a Witch? Was this the cost of abandoning her humanity? Or perhaps...

"...I was never human to begin with."
