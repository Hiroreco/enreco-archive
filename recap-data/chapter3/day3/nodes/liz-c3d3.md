Lady Bloodflame stared at herself, her face pale, complexion weak, and one eye now changed to blue. She thought to herself—

[_Is it something I should worry about?_](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/rg_rZ8reBVA?t=597)

As she continued to gaze at herself, she realized it was no longer how she used to look. However, with how many strange turns the world had taken, she tried to find a [reason](https://www.youtube.com/live/rg_rZ8reBVA?t=616) for the change. Maybe her blue flame caused this? Maybe it was simply part of what she had become?

[Nerissa](https://www.youtube.com/live/rg_rZ8reBVA?t=690), also noticing the change, complimented Lady Bloodflame, saying that it suited her and was not something she should worry about. Once again listening to Nerissa’s words, Lady Bloodflame believed her. She also thought that, since she had no memories of her past, this strange phenomenon might simply be normal for her (see [Liz-Nerissa](#edge:liz-nerissa)).

Like clockwork, strange things always seemed to happen in Libestal. Lady Bloodflame finds a strange [letter](https://www.youtube.com/live/rg_rZ8reBVA?t=828) inside her home containing a mysterious message—

_The wall was repaired, the memory was not. The Illumination..._

Confused by the contents of the letter, Lady Bloodflame suspects that it was Nyavella who gave it to her as a prank.

With many thoughts swirling in her mind, Lady Bloodflame visits [Shakira's grave](https://www.youtube.com/live/rg_rZ8reBVA?t=903). She misses her deeply, unable to shake the feeling that the moment she needed her most, she was no longer there.

Still, she holds onto a quiet belief that Shakira will one day return, as she was a Mighty Phoenix. Lady Bloodflame continues to pray for her return, hoping that they will become friends again, as Shakira was the one who made her feel warmer.

The loss of Shakira has truly shaken Lady Bloodflame to the point that she [confronts](https://www.youtube.com/live/rg_rZ8reBVA?t=1783) Ramsay for naming her pet chick Shakira. Ramsay explains that she named it after her lost friend and that it is her business how she chooses to grieve that loss.

Lady Bloodflame, still unsettled, walks away and remarks that it makes sense, since Ramsay is no longer a knight.

After they separated, Lady Bloodflame [contemplates](https://www.youtube.com/live/rg_rZ8reBVA?t=3452) Immerkind in her mind. Despite what Nerissa had told her, she had felt comfortable around Immerkind from the beginning. She does not believe Immerkind is the type who would seek to conquer others.

After conquering the Labyrinth of Emotion and witnessing the Stained Princess’s memories, Faust requests that they retrieve an item. Lady Bloodflame is then faced with a [choice](https://www.youtube.com/live/rg_rZ8reBVA?t=7591), to do what is right by speaking to Maaz, or steal it instead.

Under Nerissa’s orders, and with some initial resistance, she ultimately chooses to steal, though with a heavy heart.

While mining diamonds with Nerissa, Lady Bloodflame receives a [call](https://www.youtube.com/live/rg_rZ8reBVA?t=8828) from Nyavella informing her that the upgrade she requested is ready, along with an invitation to the opening of the Church. Lady Bloodflame reluctantly agrees to attend and inform the others, even though Nyavella showed concern for her injuries earlier.

Lady Bloodflame attends the event Shiori organized, only to discover it was a [funeral](https://www.youtube.com/live/rg_rZ8reBVA?t=11123) for Shakira. To her horror, she opens the casket hanging from the wall and finds Shakira inside.

["I need to throw up..."](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rg_rZ8reBVA&t=11873)

Overcome with nausea, she runs outside and is forced to let all the rainbow out.

Despite the confusion, the ceremony continues. However, after they finish reading the Inkwell Prayer Guide, Nyavella reveals it was a way to expose the [suspect](https://www.youtube.com/live/rg_rZ8reBVA?t=11650) in Shakira’s murder—and claims it to be Ramsay.

Ramsay denies the accusation, while her wolf pack steps in to protect her. Lady Bloodflame asks Gonathon if he truly believes Ramsay is innocent, and Gonathon insists that he trusts her.

Lady Bloodflame tries to [convince](https://www.youtube.com/live/rg_rZ8reBVA?t=11728) Nerissa to trust Gonathon, but this leads to a heated exchange between them. Lady Bloodflame then suggests they settle the matter in the tavern, and both eventually agree.

Later that day, after all the commotion at the church, Gonathon [asks](https://www.youtube.com/live/rg_rZ8reBVA?t=12250) to speak with Lady Bloodflame and Immerkind. Lady Bloodflame agrees and suggests continuing the discussion at her home (see [Gigi-Elizabeth](#edge:gigi-liz)).

After their conversation, Lady Bloodflame returns home and shows Gonathon the strange letters she received. Worried about the unknown, she wants to stay up to keep [watch](https://www.youtube.com/live/rg_rZ8reBVA?t=13450), but Gonathon and Nerissa convince her to rest, noting that she still looks unwell.

Lady Bloodflame accepts their offer and decides to trust them both with her life.

Is Lady Bloodflame’s life in safe hands? Is the sudden change in her eyes only a minor thing, or does it mean something much deeper?

Lady Bloodflame, please remain who you are...
