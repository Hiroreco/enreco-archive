Bijou awakens from her slumber once more and—ugh. Her affliction persists, perhaps even stronger than the last day. She wonders, perhaps, if it was the burden of her sins, her memories, and the sheer weight of the world weakening her. Regardless, she will push ahead, doing her best for the day. She has a feeling that it was important for her to be present that day.

Beginning her tasks for the day, Bijou [stops by Shakira's memorial](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3iXshLxO3-E&t=660), discovering that Shakira's body is gone, being replaced by a note written in Morse code. Remembering how Roa stated she would be leaving coded messages around the kingdom, Bijou wonders if she was responsible for this one, as well as if Roa had done any other work for The Illumination (see Bijou-Roa day 2).

Interested in acquiring Intermediate Upgrades, Bijou heads to the Hideout to check on the Rebellion's progress. While there, she [happens upon Lady Bloodflame](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3iXshLxO3-E&t=1140). Lady Bloodflame notices Bijou's affliction, showing concern and worry. Reassuring her, the two make plans to help complete the Rebellion's Supply Quests, with Bijou deciding to become a jeweler once more. It seems it was no longer possible for her to be selfish in this way.

Completing the beginner jeweler quest, Bijou finds she needs more materials to supply the Rebellion. Heading out to collect them, she runs into Lady Bloodflame once more. She notices that [one of Lady Bloodflame's eyes had changed color](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3iXshLxO3-E&t=2640). Bijou attempted to point this out to Lady Bloodflame, though she brushed it off as nothing of concern.

As the others begin to awaken, Bijou greets those she comes across. Among these is Fuwawa, to whom Bijou passes on the Rebellion's smithing duties. She also calls Roa, asking about what Roa had accomplished the previous night. (see: [Bijou-Roa](#edge:bijou-raora))

Our Stain Princess also calls Gonathon after narrowly missing his call. The knight asks the princess if she would be willing to take on the Labyrinth of Emotion together, to which Bijou hesitantly accepts. (see: [Bijou-Gonathon: An Unexpected Journey](#edge:bijou-gigi)).

Upon gaining the ability to make Greater Charms, Bijou began offering her services to others. The intention here was not to make money or to be helpful to others, no. She simply wanted to [destroy the economy](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3iXshLxO3-E&t=6585), and Mococo's monopoly along with it.

Upon making a transaction with Fuwawa to trade charms for upgrade stones, the items intended to be traded [mysteriously disappeared](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3iXshLxO3-E&t=7200)! Items continued to disappear and reappear for a while longer, causing Bijou to question the cause.

Bijou then moved to take on the Labyrinth of Emotion with NinoIna, Gonathon, and Roa. As she entered, she could feel her familiarity with the place and the weight of her affliction growing. Still, she pushed forward with her group, conquering the rooms filled with different emotions and shattering the crystals within.

Upon defeating the Prisoner of Emotion, the group turned back, remembering the hint regarding the labyrinth's secret. There, they found what Bijou recalled to be [a Moai Statue](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3iXshLxO3-E&t=11040), as well as a familiar melody. What is this, "Biboo Biboo" within it? Bijou found she could not recall. Then, leaving the dungeon, the group obtained the Lens of Revealing, as well as a glimpse of Bijou's own memories.

["Oh, hey, cool, I'm Koseki Bijou"](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3iXshLxO3-E&t=11400)

Following this, however, Bijou found herself in a confrontation with Gonathon, causing her to flee Terminus. She returned to solve the Secrets of Terminus, but upon their completion, she was then confronted by both Gonathon and Immerkind, whom she fled from once more to properly give Fuwawa her charms.

Following her heist of Maaz's quarters for an Amulet of Levitation, Bijou arrived at Shiori's church for the event Shiori had invited her to. Ultimately, things got... quite chaotic, to put it lightly. In the end, [Bijou asked Shiori why she had done this](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3iXshLxO3-E&t=15400), and while Shiori's explanation was just to remind everyone of Shakira's murder, Bijou commended Shiori for the chaos she sowed.

With Shakira's body still up for sale in the church, Bijou decides that she would like to buy herself and [approaches Shiori about doing so](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3iXshLxO3-E&t=16490). Getting a special deal of only 1500 coins, Bijou decides she wants to use the body to cause as much madness as possible within the kingdom, and that she will work through the night while no guards are around to halt her plans. We shall just have to see what the result will be...
