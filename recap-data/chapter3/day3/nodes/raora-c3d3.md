The ocean breeze greeting Roa by the port felt lifeless.

The kingdom’s streets, buildings, even her own home, all looked foreign to her now. Dozens of impossibilities still lingered beyond her sight, waiting patiently to reveal themselves.

That is why Roa was determined to prove a point to the non-believers, the skeptics, and those who brushed her off as nothing more than a grieving widow.

The sheep is real.

The thought of that vile creature haunted her mind for a moment, yet she refused to flinch. Someone had [noticed her](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MGo9h3BNyj0&t=720). It was the woman of cloth herself, Sister Shiori.

Ignoring the pious woman’s protests, Roa [plunged herself](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MGo9h3BNyj0&t=780) into the freezing ocean waters, desperate to dismiss what Shiori believed were mere hallucinations. Afterwards, she called the Sister back to arrange a [ceremonial burial](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MGo9h3BNyj0&t=1280) for her wife.

Later, Roa takes a short break alongside Ninoina, Immerkind, and Nerissa to [slay stains](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MGo9h3BNyj0&t=1725). Though the task itself was simple enough, she soon found herself trapped within the complicated web of relationships surrounding Immerkind, the Peasant, and Gonathon. Their conversations were deeply [complicated](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MGo9h3BNyj0&t=3870), filled with tangled emotions and uncertain intentions.

Thankfully, despite the tension, the group eventually reached an agreement that satisfied everyone involved.

Before venturing into the next dungeon, Koseki Bijou privately approaches Roa regarding strange “glitches” she had begun experiencing (see [Bijou-Raora](#edge:bijou-raora)).

Soon after, Roa, Ninoina, Gonathon, and Bijou enter the enigma known as the [Labyrinth of Emotion](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MGo9h3BNyj0&t=5500).

But the deeper Roa wandered into the day, the louder the voices became.

A disturbing [message](https://www.youtube.com/live/MGo9h3BNyj0?si=8cBYe4AldnmslPaq&t=9585) from the voices inside her head claimed that Shakira’s death occurred because she escaped the simulations, and that Roa now had to uncover the truth if she ever wished to see her again. Rather than reject the message, Roa acknowledged it calmly before explaining the exhausting burden she carried every day pretending that everything was normal around others. For the sake of her own sanity, she wore a mask of conformity.

By afternoon, the church ceremony finally beckoned, prompting Roa to hurry toward the [event](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MGo9h3BNyj0&t=10885).

Yet before the ceremony could even begin, something immediately felt wrong.

Roa watched in horror as Lady Bloodflame and Nerissa reacted violently the moment they approached the altar. Lady Bloodflame’s face twisted into a jagged mess of bile and terror, as though fighting against the primal urge to purge her own senses. Nerissa did not simply flee; she scrambled away while bawling uncontrollably, her cries sounding like silk being violently torn apart.

Concerned, Roa rushed toward the church entrance.

And then she saw it.

A black sheep named “The Flock.”

A solitary mass of putrid black fiber stood motionless beside the oak doors. Its rectangular pupils remained perfectly parallel to the earth beneath its obsidian hooves. The sight alone sent Roa’s paranoia spiraling out of control. Almost immediately, she became convinced the church’s true purpose was to offer prayers to the sheep itself.

Roa desperately asks Sister Shiori to remove the creature, only for the Sister to respond with laughter. Eventually the sheep wandered away, but the suffocating feeling it left behind remained.

Finally entering the church, Roa approached the altar only to discover what appeared to be… an empty box.

_The box is empty now?_
_How is that possible?_

Her mind failed to process the impossible scene before her. Unable to comprehend it alone, she staggered outside alongside the other witness to the horrific sight.

[A Bird Box](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MGo9h3BNyj0&t=11160)

The red knight trembled as she described what she had seen inside the box: a blasphemous mockery of a body. Her wife had been crushed into a container barely large enough to hang from a crucifix. Her limbs were not merely broken, but stretched outward and twisted into grotesque angles. The once beautiful magenta eyes she cherished had become nothing more than empty husks of tissue and membrane. What remained of her face was frozen forever in terror and agony.

Roa’s entire body began shaking violently.

Then, from the corner of her eye, she saw the black sheep again.

A high-pitched scream escaped her throat as she began clawing [deep into the ground](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MGo9h3BNyj0&t=11375). She buried herself within the cold dirt, screaming over and over for the sheep to go away.

And when her sanity could no longer endure it, she fled while her pet desperately tried to console her.

Her stress threatened to tear her apart completely. In response, Roa constructed a small safe haven made entirely from pure white quartz. Within its pale walls, her breathing slowly steadied. The anxiety never fully vanished, but eventually became manageable enough for her to calm down.

Later, Roa confronts Sister Shiori regarding everything that transpired within the church (see [Raora-Shiori](#edge:raora-shiori)).

Still unable to let go of her obsession with the truth, Roa ventures out once again to inspect the Gap and break through its surrounding barriers. By now, traversing these impossible boundaries had become [second nature](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MGo9h3BNyj0&t=14365) to her.

But there, beyond the walls, she discovered another anomaly.

A solitary impossibility.

A non-euclidean cube of glass floating silently in the air.

[A cube that shouldn't exist.](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MGo9h3BNyj0&t=14460)

Roa carefully inspects the object before ultimately deciding to return to town [voluntarily](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MGo9h3BNyj0&t=14630), fearing that lingering too long might attract the suspicions of the powers lurking beyond reality itself.

And so, as the next day slowly approaches, Roa continues onward, burdened by truths no one else wishes to see.
