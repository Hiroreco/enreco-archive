The day begins for our Stain Princess with a case of the sniffles. It appears that overnight, she had gained some sort of affliction that would linger over her for the day. No matter. She pushes forward, greeting her fellow members of the Rebellion with a good morning, in particular including one Roa. (See: [Bijou-Roa](#edge:bijou-raora))

The Stain Princess continued about her day until it was time for her to receive her pet. Having learned from the Peasant that the pets are gacha, Bijou finds herself quite excited to receive hers. Opening her surprise box, she is met with a chicken, wondering if it is meant to be [a reminder](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uZ0IMLL2Vxc&t=3080) of her actions the previous day. Regardless, she grants this new reminder the name "Lava Chicken."

Clearing the Labyrinth of Knowledge along with Roa, NinoIna, Mordon, and the Peasant, Bijou realizes that there are some secrets left to be found within. Those, however, can wait, as the Labyrinth also granted a glimpse at the memories of Bijou's fellow coven member, Shiori, as well as a Tome of Knowledge. Learning of the All-Seeing Madman from this tome, Bijou [approaches Faust](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uZ0IMLL2Vxc&t=8200) to inquire about the Madman, who informs her that he is in Cell 0414. For some reason, that number tugs on her mind...

Learning from Faust that a Glass Compass is needed to enter Cell 0414, Bijou heads to the Black Market to acquire one. As the merchant Sabik continually makes obtaining the compass difficult, Bijou resorts to using increasingly extreme threats in order to get him to comply.

[I do not think that I should repeat these.](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uZ0IMLL2Vxc&t=9370)

In the end, Bijou takes the compass without paying and makes her way to the gateway of the Labyrinth of Emotion. Once again, she finds a sense of familiarity, this time with the [shape of the gateway](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uZ0IMLL2Vxc&t=9935).

With the Labyrinth of Emotion still closed, Bijou turns back with the intent of uncovering the Labyrinth of Knowledge's secrets. With Roa and NinoIna once more, and Tam and Fuwawa joining along, the five manage to discover one of the Labyrinth's secrets, yet find that there is still another to discover. Bijou swears that she will find this last secret, but with her affliction still weighing on her mind and body, that will have to wait until the next day. To recover from the affliction, Bijou decides to retire early, discovering that Tam had moved into the Shimmerstone house as well.
