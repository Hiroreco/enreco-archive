After the heroes conquered the Labyrinth of Knowledge and obtained the Tome of Knowledge, they returned it to Fia and reported everything they had uncovered. What followed was a mixture of revelation and growing concern.

[Nullspace](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?t=7990&v=7SzxeEiLGFs)

According to Fia, the tome spoke of a hidden world concealed deep within Terminus itself. Not merely a hidden chamber or forgotten ruin, but an entirely separate dimension known as "Nullspace," a place described as being even more secure than the prison realm of Terminus. It was a realm where the most dangerous secrets in the multiverse were sealed away, hidden from all existence.

And more importantly, likely the very place the Outsider was searching for.

The tome offered only one clue regarding its location:

_"The entrance to Nullspace is known only to the all-seeing madman."_

Unfortunately, even Fia herself had no idea who this mysterious figure could be. Whoever this "all-seeing madman" was, the answers they sought now rested with them, meaning the only path forward was to seek out someone else who might know more. (see [Faust](#node:faust))
