Sure, there was a dungeon opening today, one that might unlock more past memories of her comrades and uncover even more mysteries about the world itself. Sure, the [drama and news](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jdWk5-BLjCw&t=885s) surrounding Shakira's death were still spreading everywhere, with people continuing to speculate about the culprit while Mordon stubbornly maintained her innocence.

But all of that paled in comparison to the true terror inflicted upon this fluffy one that day.

What began with excitement and glee quickly spiraled into dread and horror.

A creature so terrifying, so incomprehensibly fearsome, shook the girl to the very depths of her soul.

What could only be described as... true fear.

[DON'T SCARE ME!](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jdWk5-BLjCw&t=3286s)

Rawr! The only predator known to hunt men, its bulky, chunky, *terrifying* frame nearly doubled the girl in size. Its teeth looked razor sharp, its claws absurdly long, and there was absolutely no way she was getting anywhere near it. The fluffy one screamed, cried, and ran for her life while the beast relentlessly chased her down, refusing to let her escape.

...

Okay, maybe that was *slightly* overdramatic, but at the start of the third day, Fuwawa and the others opened a special "gacha" present to receive their very own little companion. Well... *some* got little companions. As much as Fuwawa had wished for a cute little chicken, something everyone already knew she absolutely adored, she instead received the largest counterpart imaginable. White fur and all, who in their right mind thought a *POLAR BEAR* would make a [good pet?](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jdWk5-BLjCw&t=3450s)

Still, whether she liked it or not, she was basically obligated to take care of him now.

With that terrifying ordeal out of the way, the fluffy one went around finishing her required tasks before eventually regrouping with the others at the **Labyrinth of Knowledge.** After conquering it, though not without many [falls](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jdWk5-BLjCw&t=5275s) along the journey, they managed to recover the memories of [Shiori Novella.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jdWk5-BLjCw&t=7841s)

Once the dungeon was cleared, the fluffy one continued her day with even more questing around town. Some moments even became [violent](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jdWk5-BLjCw&t=8603s), though she was the one doing the violence this time, and she spent plenty of time hanging around the townsfolk afterward. At one point, she even managed to make someone tear up simply by correctly remembering their name.

[You're Tam right?](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jdWk5-BLjCw&t=11029s)

With little else left to accomplish, and being the good doggo that she was, the fluffy one stayed behind to help those who still hadn't completed the day's dungeon before finally calling it a day herself.
