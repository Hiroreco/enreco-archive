#### Main Story

**Objective**

After some investigation, the heroes are able to enter Cell 0502, where they're pulled into a strange labyrinth known as the **Labyrinth of Knowledge,** and must battle the **Prisoner of Knowledge** to obtain its forbidden knowledge in the form of a tome.

**Discoveries**

They show the tome of forbidden knowledge to Fia, who deduces that the Outsider was searching for a hidden dimension within Terminus known as **Nullspace,** which holds the most dangerous secrets in the multiverse.

The tome also states that the entrance to Nullspace is known only to the "all-seeing madman."

The heroes consult Faust, who explains that this madman must be the prisoner of Cell 0414. However, the heroes will need an artifact known as a _glass compass_ in order to navigate the twisted hallway leading there.

Some heroes decide to obtain it from Star Speaker Hamal, a merchant who challenges them to demonstrate their business skills.

Others decide to obtain it on the black market from a shady dealer who tries to scam them multiple times, but eventually folds when the heroes threaten him.
