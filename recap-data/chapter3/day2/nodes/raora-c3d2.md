**_The world isn't what it seems._**

After a bewildering discovery [the day before](https://www.youtube.com/live/XD-K3AirOdA?t=19434), Roa became convinced that the world itself was a simulation. Compared to the truth lurking beyond Libestal's walls, the problems surrounding the Fia, the Outsider, and the Stains suddenly felt insignificant.

People deserved to know the truth. They deserved to understand the reality they were trapped in.

But for that, Roa needed allies. People willing to believe her discoveries instead of dismissing them as madness.

The first person she tried to recruit was Fuwawa. Roa believed the fluffy one was kind-hearted and open-minded enough to understand her. Unfortunately, Fuwawa could barely comprehend what Roa was talking about and instead brushed it off as a [coping mechanism](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uSz1tR2SU28&t=860) for the death of her wife.

The rejection left a bitter taste in Roa's mouth. Hurt and frustrated, she ran off while Fuwawa helplessly tried to console her.

[She doesn't see the REALITY! She is BLINDED...](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uSz1tR2SU28&t=1145)

As Roa wandered through the city in despair, she suddenly noticed flowers gathered near the entrance. There stood a [memorial statue](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uSz1tR2SU28&t=1300) dedicated to her beloved. The grieving widow paid her respects, and in that moment, the resolve she had nearly lost returned to her once again.

Not long after, Bijou approached Roa and insisted on learning the "secret" she had overheard earlier. Roa hesitated. Revealing something this dangerous in public was already risky enough, and she still harbored suspicions about Bijou's possible involvement in the poisoning of the Fertility Cake. But then Roa noticed a sheep nearby and suddenly burst into manic laughter.

[The sheep is REAL!](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uSz1tR2SU28&t=2295)

The sight only reinforced her convictions. Still, Roa held back and promised Bijou she would explain everything later.

Despite her growing obsession, Roa still found time to continue exploring the Labyrinthe of Knowledge. Somewhere along the way, she also adopted a peculiar little pet she named [Sniffa Sniffa](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uSz1tR2SU28&t=2920). While some people, particularly [Fuwawa](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uSz1tR2SU28&t=2940) and [Tam](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uSz1tR2SU28&t=14735), seemed disturbed by the creature, Roa adored her dearly.

Later, Bijou confronted Roa once more about the promised secret. Eventually, Roa relented despite her suspicions and finally revealed her intentions of forming an underground organization devoted to uncovering the truth. (see [Bijou-Raora](#edge:bijou-raora))

With one ally secured, Roa continued searching for more believers. She attempted to recruit Tam next, but Tam's [reaction](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uSz1tR2SU28&t=16360) was no different from Fuwawa's earlier response.

Once again, Roa walked away disappointed, yet no less determined.

This time, she wanted undeniable proof. Something tangible she could show others.

The walls surrounding Libestal's fields had been heavily fortified since yesterday, but Roa refused to believe the truth could be hidden forever. Somewhere beyond those barriers, she knew the evidence still existed.

[And she found a way through.](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uSz1tR2SU28&t=18370)

She was certain the sheep would be waiting there.

[And there it was.](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/uSz1tR2SU28?t=18492)

Roa stared at the proof she had been searching for, but before she could fully process it, the powers beyond abruptly tore her away and sent her back to the village.

Frustration consumed her, accompanied by a splitting headache, yet beneath the pain was something else entirely:

The feeling that she was getting closer.

Closer to uncovering the true nature of this world.
