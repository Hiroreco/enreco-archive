_"Where do your loyalties lie, Lady Bloodflame?"_

_That is you as Lady Bloodflame, but who were you before Lady Bloodflame?_

A new day had risen for Lady Bloodflame, and what better way to start it than by fishing? As a member of the Rebellion, there were duties and responsibilities she had to fulfill in order for it to grow. While she was occupied, an unlikely guest appeared before Lady Bloodflame, and it was none other than Nyavella.

Despite their troubled past, [Nyavella approached Lady Bloodflame without hesitation to ask for assistance](https://www.youtube.com/live/7SzxeEiLGFs?si=0xLO-hXg9J93z33U), even believing that Lady Bloodflame still harbored hatred toward her. [Lady Bloodflame admitted that because Nerissa held no hatred toward Nyavella](https://www.youtube.com/live/7SzxeEiLGFs?si=VFR8mRBtyvK_smFa&t=697), she too chose not to hate her, which greatly relieved Nyavella. It seems their past animosity is finally behind them, at least for now.

While trying to gather the materials needed for fishing, [Lady Bloodflame discovered a letter from Nerissa](https://www.youtube.com/live/7SzxeEiLGFs?si=UBlEZyS0QwCJaV6S&t=880). In it, Nerissa explained that she had left food for her and had decided to make herself at home in Lady Bloodflame’s house. [At first, Lady Bloodflame felt frustrated, but in the end, she accepted it and believed it was a good idea](https://www.youtube.com/live/7SzxeEiLGFs?si=9SAbimYe-U8BgnLE&t=1027), for she trusted her princess as her knight. (See: [Liz-Nerissa](#edge:liz-nerissa))

Continuing the task at hand, [Lady Bloodflame enlisted her sister, Mococo, to protect her while she fished for the elusive River Prince fish](https://www.youtube.com/live/7SzxeEiLGFs?si=QODagI4yUJaXtOhb&t=1293). During a conversation with Immerkind regarding her pet, Moohternal, [Mococo remarked that killing it would be a kinder act](https://www.youtube.com/live/7SzxeEiLGFs?si=Ma9H-4UJ5-ACxqj3&t=1575), though Lady Bloodflame dismissed the comment as nothing more than hunger speaking. This caused Immerkind to question whether Lady Bloodflame had sworn herself to Mococo, to which Lady Bloodflame clarified that they were sisters. Immerkind accepted the answer and chose not to question it further. Despite what had happened with Mococo, [Lady Bloodflame admits that she feels comfortable when she was around Immerkind](https://www.youtube.com/live/7SzxeEiLGFs?si=U8SOEJqPZ_UVY7HU&t=1937).

With all said and done, things have changed. Lady Bloodflame had slowly become someone unrecognizable. [When Immerkind requested a trade for an enchanted item](https://www.youtube.com/live/7SzxeEiLGFs?si=CsOWpoai3CVrv23t&t=1990), Lady Bloodflame demanded an exorbitant price. This was not the Lady Bloodflame that she knew, and Immerkind called her out, saying she no longer recognized her. Realizing what she had done, Lady Bloodflame chased after Immerkind to correct her actions and assured her that she was still a knight. They made a satisfactory trade, but is Lady Bloodflame truly satisfied?

Back home, [she met with Nerissa and explored the changes to their home.](https://www.youtube.com/live/7SzxeEiLGFs?si=ifFba7qifUmLJUEe&t=3399) Lady Bloodflame felt pleased that Nerissa remembered parts of their past, and Nerissa mentioned that she still recalled most things about her. However, Nerissa noticed a change in Lady Bloodflame’s complexion and believed she might be ill. Lady Bloodflame dismissed the concern, saying it could be due to the passing of Shakira, an important comrade in arms—the one who had been her guiding force in the old world.

After joining Nerissa, Nyavella, Mococo, and Fuwawa in completing the Labyrinth, the group [successfully conquered it, and Shiori Nyavella regained some of her memories](https://www.youtube.com/live/7SzxeEiLGFs?si=eFPBf5I80J69EdQH&t=7734). As a post-labyrinth task, they were instructed to speak with the [Star Speaker Hamal to learn how negotiations work](https://www.youtube.com/live/7SzxeEiLGFs?si=9--G2e7ocbpz8fCx&t=8288). Without much thought, Lady Bloodflame agreed to whatever deal Nerissa offered her, without question, even though the terms seemed questionable.

After completing the Secrets of Terminus Puzzle Area, [Lady Bloodflame was given a choice of horse armor](https://www.youtube.com/live/7SzxeEiLGFs?si=VGVYwo4Lxb-1nPyL&t=10667): either the Shining Stars Binding, the Stars armor but weaker, or the Rebellion Barding, the Rebel's armor but tougher. Still undecided, Lady Bloodflame heard Nerissa subtly mention that there was no good reason to choose the other option. Before Nerissa could even ask what she wanted, Lady Bloodflame decided to take the Rebel's armor instead.

The Bloodflame household had once again expanded, as two new residents joined them. [As if fate had played its part, both Lady Bloodflame and Nerissa each obtained pandas as their first pets](https://www.youtube.com/live/7SzxeEiLGFs?si=ZnEcDwWdGIjpqrcj&t=11874). Lady Bloodflame named hers Jack, while Nerissa named hers Shiopanda. The Bloodflame household became merrier with the addition of the adorable creatures living there.

As Lady Bloodflame began sorting out a garden for Nerissa to grow her crops, as well as organizing their basement storage, [she received a call from Nyavella offering a newspaper](https://www.youtube.com/live/7SzxeEiLGFs?si=ir7_5-qY646ToejN&t=13830). Lady Bloodflame questioned its legitimacy. Nyavella insisted that it was real, and, giving her little choice, Nyavella visited Lady Bloodflame’s home and made her purchase the newspaper so she could build her church at a higher price than what she initially said.

[Lady Bloodflame questioned Nyavella about why she had mentioned that she was part of the Coven](https://www.youtube.com/live/7SzxeEiLGFs?si=kfcj9izo9c-kd_97&t=13951). Nyavella explained that Lady Bloodflame had been following Nerissa like a dog and abandoning her fellow knights for her. Lady Bloodflame dismissed what Nyavella had said and decided to take the newspaper with a grain of salt. They parted ways, leaving Lady Bloodflame with much to think about.

Some time later, [Nerissa returns to their home with Nyavella while Lady Bloodflame observes them](https://www.youtube.com/live/7SzxeEiLGFs?si=Y_3BnJmHvwLa1Fs_&t=14092). Nyavella attempts to sell the newspaper to Nerissa, but Lady Bloodflame goes between them and insists that they will be sharing the newspaper since they share the same home. Nyavella insists they still need to pay to help the church despite their protest. Lady Bloodflame, not wanting Nerissa to buy a newspaper from Nyavella, paid Nyavella the amount, which was again higher than the previous amount.

The day ended with the Bloodflame household now having a garden, a basement storage area, and three new residents. It had been quite an eventful day for Lady Bloodflame.

Lady Bloodflame, once a proud and noble knight who would fight anyone she believed to be evil, was slowly losing herself. Her once-strong complexion had started to fade. Were the choices she made incorrect? Did choosing to stay with her princess and sister cause her to lose her honor? Will living with Nerissa Juliet lead to a happy ending for Lady Bloodflame or to her fall...

May the Mighty Phoenix above guide her...
