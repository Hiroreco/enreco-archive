Bau bau...! Back to Libestal, a brand new day!!

The soft voice of a waking Mococo filled the empty walls of her big house as she gathered her thoughts, ready to head out! Though her excitement quickly turned to confusion once she saw that her front door was open. Not only that, but there was a [strange journal](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JCwz_K_r5M&t=365) left in her postbox with anonymous text inside asking if she wanted an older sister?? So weird... She already had Lady Bloodflame!! She didn't need _another_ older sister.

After disregarding the message, Mococo looked at her quest for the day... and instantly got confused. Luckily for her, Bijou was around! She helped her understand what she had to do, and from then on, it was off to clear it and make some MONEY!!

But it wasn't all pleasant. While on the way to help her sister with fishing, she talked with the Peasant. There, it was revealed that by an error from Bae's end, a lot of Mococo's hard work had seemingly vanished in an instant. The little witch was—understandably—pretty upset, at least the redhead apologized profusely and gave Mococo compensation coins. A couple of minutes later, though, things turned around! Bae reported that their charms were saved! She would just need her money back, that's all.

[That's okay. I can keep it.](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JCwz_K_r5M&t=1826)

After that, she had a couple more things to do before the day's main adventure. Mordon Ramsay [pretty much pleaded](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JCwz_K_r5M&t=2117) to commission the ONLY jeweler in the kingdom, providing both the resources and money for it, and the little witch was more than happy to aid her as her ego only grew. She also witnessed part of Lady Bloodflame's misadventure with the Peasant and defended her sister's overpriced fish purchase, and the last thing she did was bicker with the doggo who looked similar to her. They'd been close friends in times past, and there was always a sense of familiarity with her, but they just [could not get along today](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JCwz_K_r5M&t=2549).

Looking away from her frustrations, the little witch set up a small charm shop in front of the entrance to Terminus and waited for people to come around! Nerissa, her fellow coven member, stopped by for a moment. Mococo gloated about her ["LITTLE WITCH MOCO-POLY!!!"](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JCwz_K_r5M&t=3758) before they were reached by Sister Shiori with an invitation to join their dungeon-crawling party. Despite being annoyed at how she was treated by Fuwawa (whether it's justified or not depends on your point of view), she looked past it for now, joining up with the two, Nerissa and her sister, to tackle the **Labyrinth of Knowledge**.

The dungeon demanded a lot of physical and mental prowess of its adventurers. [Many screams were shared along the way](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JCwz_K_r5M&t=5419). But sooner or later, they'd made it through mostly unscathed and recovered the memories of the Forbidden Archiver, [Shiori Novella](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JCwz_K_r5M&t=7844).

Back in Libestal, she was a bit tired, so she decided to speak to Faust tomorrow. She had to rest up, because there were so many more charms to be sold tomorrow!! BAU BAUUU!!
