As a new day dawned upon Libestal there was but one thing on the mind of Gonathon G: how to recover his beloved queen's memories. Thankfully the humble knight did not have to wait long, as a [letter from Immerkind](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=281) laid out a plan to dispell the foul stain hat from Tam's head. The two would seek to replace the hat with an adorable pet, one that would win the queen's heart and resolve the issues plaguing her.

However as the two would soon discover, the day had other plans in store for them.

Doing his rounds throughout the kingdom, Gonathon bumped into many familiar faces. While most, such as [NinoIna](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=490), were friendly, one in particular bore a great deal of animosity towards him. Bijou, firmly in the clutches of Nix's honeyed words, spared Gonathon a few moments to hurl bile before trotting off in a huff, the diminutive princess' antipathy towards the knight seeming to only deepen by the day (see [Bijou-Gonathon](#edge:bijou-gigi)).

Gonathon himself had little tie to ponder her strange behavior, as he committed himself to upgrading his weapon. Following [a long-winded conversation](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=1121) with Nerissa about the specifics of the fabled Game of Thrones, Gonathon encountered Shiori who had seemingly not given up on entangling the reluctant knight in her schemes (see [Gonathon-Shiori](#edge:gigi-shiori)).

[It's okay, I can wait for you!](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=1646)

After failing to lure Gonathon into the Rebel Hideout, Shiori instead accompanied him to the farming fields. There Gonathon reached max level as he conversed with Shiori on a number of topics, including [their connection](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=1903) to one another that Shiori only had lingering memories of. While Shiori was unrelenting in her [attempts](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=1937) to coax Gonathon into accepting a place in her house, the real revelation was that the shiftless sister had seemed to remember some aspects of their time in the ancient kingdom.

Alas her memories remained incomplete, and she parted ways with Gonathon after expressing her longing for [the one she loved like a sibling](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=2734).

As Shiori departed, Gonathon was once again reunited with Immerkind. It was here that the two laid their feelings bare (see [Gonathon-Immerkind](#edge:cecilia-gigi)). Gonathon expressed [his desire](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=3074) to return his queen to what he saw as her true self, and that he longed to escape to a new land with her where they could be happy. Immerkind was unsure of this, and attempted to remind Gonathon of [his knightly vows](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=3122) to protect the commonfolk.

Immerkind for her part wished to live a happy, peaceful life with her beloved princess, to which Gonathon provided encouragement despite his fellow knight's apparent lack of standing. However, with the opening of the dungeon looming the two had little time to reminisce further, and simply [reaffirmed their vows](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=3193) to assist the other as much as they could.

[The vow of the queen's knights](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=3667)

Hastening to the gathering point, the two knights were shocked to find that they had been [abandoned](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=3843) by their fellow adventurers. Left with no possible companions to tackle the Labyrinth of Knowledge, Gonathon and Immerkind decided that they would have to do it on their own. Before departing, Immerkind assisted Gonathon in acquiring a **pet sheep**, which the humble knight christened as Geep. From there the duo set off to complete the challenges that awaited them.

While one would assume that a mere two adventurers would have difficulty accomplishing the task of completing the dungeon, Immerkind and Gonathon proved more than up to the task. Be it riddles, jumping puzzles, or being forced to watch an entire cutscene of their actions all over again, [the twin knights](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=6147) laid low all obstacles that stood before them. Upon defeating the Prisoner of Knowledge, Gonathon witnessed a new memory: that of Shiori.

[It takes two!](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=7761)

With the dungeon laid low, Gonathon and Immerkind returned to Libestal, only to be greeted by a startling sight: [Tam Gandr had returned!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=8960) Sadly, the amnesiac queen was just as delirious as ever, and her tardiness forced Gonathon to commit to assisting her with accomplishing her tasks for the day rather than hatching the plan he had concocted with Immerkind (see [Gonathon-Tam Gandr](#edge:gigi-kronii)).

Following the arduous task of guiding Tam through [a number of riddles](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=9182), Gonathon told his companions he would unfortunately need to retire early that day. However, his day would not end just yet. Returning to the Star Speakers, the clever knight was able to outwit [Speaker Hamal](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=11506) and accomplish the next portion of his quest. And with that, the second day of Gonathon G's adventure came to a close.
