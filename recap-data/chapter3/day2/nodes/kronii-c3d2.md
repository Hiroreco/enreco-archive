"You missed the whole show, buddy."

That was the first thing everyone told the maiden in blue as she woke up ridiculously late, arriving long after most people had already completed the day's major task: conquering the Labyrinth of Knowledge and solving the newly unlocked "puzzles." Still, better late than never. And honestly, how could the former queen, a woman infamous for loving quizzes and riddles, possibly pass up the opportunity to solve _puzzles_?

Now, loving puzzles and actually being _good_ at them were two very different things.

[Woman got nothing going on in that head](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Up0bZJINZx8&t=786)

Thankfully, plenty of people were still awake by the time she arrived, and knowing full well how hopelessly clueless Tam could become, several of them decided to help her out. Gonathon, Immerkind, and Nerissa all tagged along to assist the struggling blue maiden (see [Gonathon-Tam](#edge:gigi-kronii)). After [many](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Up0bZJINZx8&t=1309) painful attempts at forcing two neurons to connect, Tam somehow managed to complete the puzzle challenge before finally making her rounds to greet everyone in the kingdom for the first time that day.

She had a _lot_ to catch up on. People apparently had pets now, [strange ones at that,](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Up0bZJINZx8&t=1881) and others kept talking about various tech and mechanics that little old Tam was [failing](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Up0bZJINZx8&t=2097) spectacularly to understand. Thankfully, everyone was more than willing to help her through the day's dungeon regardless.

Alongside Ina, Roa, Fuwawa, and Bijou, The Azure Maiden ventured into the Labyrinth of Knowledge, where she once again showcased her incredible [puzzle-solving skills](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Up0bZJINZx8&t=2950), much to the amusement and praise of the others. Not that Tam took any of it seriously. Not during the dungeon, not even while recalling the [memories](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Up0bZJINZx8&t=4981) of her comrade. To put it simply, the blue maiden was absolutely zooted out of her mind this day.

[Munch, munch, munch](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Up0bZJINZx8&t=4596)

After conquering the dungeon, Tam later ran into Shiori, who generously offered the blue maiden a corner of her [house](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Up0bZJINZx8&t=5424) where she could build anything she wanted. Naturally, Tam immediately claimed a cozy little [spot](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Up0bZJINZx8&t=5559) by the window, proudly dubbing it her "thinking spot," a place where, with enough effort, she might finally be able to form a coherent thought for once.

By then, most people had already called it a night, so Tam spent the rest of her day running around completing quests and tasks, essentially trying to catch up with everyone else. Thankfully, a few people still remained awake to keep her company. [Roa](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Up0bZJINZx8&t=6142) kindly accompanied her here and there and, for once, refrained from making fun of her name or turning her into the kingdom's laughing stock again. Shiori also stayed around, though the latter spent more time saying increasingly [questionable](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Up0bZJINZx8&t=9656) things than being helpful.

Once everything was finally done, Tam ended her long day by writing in her [journal.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Up0bZJINZx8&t=10218)

As late as she had been to everything, The Azure Maiden still had a pretty good day nonetheless.
