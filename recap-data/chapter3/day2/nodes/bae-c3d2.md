Another day, another chance to catch the elusive River Prince. But so focused was The Peasant on her quests the day before that, to her shock, she missed [Shakira's death!](https://youtu.be/t0aLqfBeqK0?t=372) And that Mordon Ramsay was the perpetrator. And not Bae herself since she was fishing the day away.

So once again, Bae bought more lucky fishing bait before heading back to base to complete the quest. As a proud peasant, Bae didn't mind donating the money needed as well as the ores. 

All that is left was the River Prince fish from Sidero. There, Mordon Ramsay was in the fields.
"What happened to Shakira?" Bae asked. No, that was Mordon's pet.


Then, Immerkind. 

The Knight was fighting stains and Bae, despite their painful history, covered the Knight's back, shielding her from another [stain's attack.](https://youtu.be/t0aLqfBeqK0?t=970) 


Matters of the heart aside, a quest still bothered Bae. To her utter horror, she found that some of the quests for the guild were indeed group quests, contributions shared between each member, and Bae may have unknowingly messed things up! As compensation, Bae would give Mococo 500 gold as recompense for the troubles she caused...

[It Saves!](#embed:https://youtu.be/t0aLqfBeqK0?t=1431)

Thank the gods, the efforts for all that contributed was not lost and they may continue to venture on the quest for faithful companions.

All that was left now was the River Prince. But alas, in the midst of her stern preparations, [moonlight passed all too quickly](https://youtu.be/t0aLqfBeqK0?t=1747) and the sunlight would deter it.

But it would seem that one of her guildmates proved to be all too impatient. Bae came across Lady Bloodflame, listening to [NinoIna The Heroes's](https://youtu.be/t0aLqfBeqK0?t=2174) sales pitch.

800 gold for a fish? When bait is just 10 gold?! Bae's peasant sensibilities rang alarms in her head as she intervened with the Crimson Knight's purchase. For a moment, it seemed the Lady was convinced. They only need 1 more fish after all.

[But, Betrayal knows no (Immer)Kindness.](#embed:https://youtu.be/t0aLqfBeqK0?t=2210)

Just like that, The Peasant watched the once Proud Knight bow her head to the Hero and buy the fish. Though it stings, the guild may now go on and continue with new companions by their side.

[Polar The Bear the Polar Bear](#embed:https://youtu.be/t0aLqfBeqK0?t=2652)

If only Khaos and Chaoz could see their mother now. Though she may have questionably betrayed Fia for a woman she barely knew and who may possibly stab her in the back later on, Bae now has a pet! An adorable bear of snow white fur and great courage. No, Fuwawa, [polar bears are adorable.](https://youtu.be/t0aLqfBeqK0?t=2682)

But of course, things are never simple as Bae and her crew of Rebels still needed to fish for the River Prince for other quests.

That, unfortunately, had to take a back seat for now as The Labrynths beckoned our heroes again. But a [secret must first be paid](https://youtu.be/t0aLqfBeqK0?t=3462) before they can enter.

Through their perseverence, intellect, and a shiny newly levelled revelation (courtesy of Kouseki Bijou, of course), they conquered the Prisoner of Knowledge, a tragic being so lonely the Peasant couldn't help but emphatise with him.

Soon a new area was accessible which can only mean one thing... [New areas to fish!](https://www.youtube.com/live/t0aLqfBeqK0?t=8527) A familiar area too, with plenty of diamonds to mine.
