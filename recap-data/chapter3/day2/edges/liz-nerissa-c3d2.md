**A letter to us, a gift for you**

A single letter addressed to her from none other than her Juliet awaited her. After gently prying it open, Lady Bloodflame read it to herself. Gifts awaited her and-

[Nerissa is moving in!?](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7SzxeEiLGFs&t=874)

Taking a moment to process what she just learned, Lady Bloodflame realized it _actually_ made sense. It would be easier to watch her, to protect her. To... be close to her. It also meant she could avoid pranks such as that silly music from the Libestal of the past.

Moving in, the humble knight opened the new chest and found all the items Nerissa Juliet mentioned. But there was one particular item that caught her attention. A ring- a symbol of loyalty and commitment.

Lady Bloodflame was left gobsmacked, a strangled noise leaving her throat. Suddenly, a flood of memories rushed back. Their sparring matches that led to a stolen kiss, showers of affection in the form of flowers and sweet letters. Her face turned into a strawberry just by recalling those moments.

But she couldn't afford to waste time by reminiscing. So, she veered away from it and took note of the essentials—food. These were sufficient to energise her for the day.

She glanced back at the ring... _Nope. It shall remain in the chest for now._

---

**Under the ~~witch~~ weather**

A few days had passed. Like Nerissa, Lady Bloodflame toiled for their faction- completing meager quests, fighting the stains, dutifully fulfilling their respective occupation.

One day, she returned home, with Nerissa not far behind her. As the maiden set up her shop, they reminisced about the times when Shakira was still alive. After all, she was a fellow knight, but more than that, a dear friend, whom she had sought advice on all sorts of things.

It was slowly sinking into her that her mentor was now gone.

["Are you quite well, Lady Bloodflame?"](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7SzxeEiLGFs&t=3495)

Nerissa noticed the sudden change in her demeanor. She seemed rather... unwell. Lady Bloodflame admitted her passing was too much of a burden to carry.

Her Juliet consoled her, as any ~~friend~~ ~~lover~~ master would.

Eventually, Nerissa suggested that she switch back to becoming a smith. If that would mean she could honour her by retaining her old profession, she would be more than happy to allow her to do so.

Naturally, our Jade Knight immediately [succumbed to her](https://youtu.be/7SzxeEiLGFs?t=3535). No questions asked. As if entranced, she went on her way to go back to smithing.

---

**Foul is fair, and fair is foul**

Love was much like a game of thrones. A relentless power struggle, where only the scheming, the cunning, the calculating ever truly prevailed.

Nerissa Juliet learned that the hard way. To understand it, she had to lose herself, or perhaps discover who she truly was from the beginning.

The Witch, as she now called herself, no longer hesitated to scheme. Every interaction with Lady Bloodflame became an opportunity to move up the board— to dominate her. Sweet words carefully chosen, innocent smiles crafted with purpose. Simple requests carried hidden intent. All of it served her revenge. And it was only the beginning.

Seeing the once proud knight reduced to something [obedient](https://youtu.be/JixD1DhcNgc?t=14064), so eager to please, filled Nerissa with great satisfaction.

Perhaps, becoming a Witch was the [right decision](https://youtu.be/JixD1DhcNgc?t=14082).
