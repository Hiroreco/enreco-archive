Meeting early in the morning, Bijou became quite curious about the "discoveries" that Roa mentioned. Bijou inquired if she would be willing to discuss this in private, though, while Roa was deliberating on this, the two were interrupted by Fuwawa's appearance.

Not wanting to let this go, Bijou later approaches Roa once more, again [inquiring about what she had learned](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uSz1tR2SU28&t=1410). Roa continues to deflect, asking to talk about this later in a quieter place.

The two do agree to party up, taking on many of the day's challenges together. They begin by collecting their pets together, with Roa taking Bijou's pet chicken as a sign that Shakira would have wanted to be friends. The two also take on the Labyrinth of Knowledge and the Secrets of Terminus together, even entering the Labyrinth of Knowledge twice to search for its secrets.

All this while, Bijou had still been curious about what Roa had learned and decided to try one last time before she had to retire for the day. Finally finding a moment of privacy, Roa makes Bijou promise that she is trustworthy and informs her of what she had discovered the previous day: [there was a gap in the world](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uZ0IMLL2Vxc&t=13960).

Suspecting that the world they are in is a simulation, and with Bijou believing Roa's claims, the two vow to investigate this further. However, the two know that it is too early to tell the others yet, so they decide to keep this on the down low, forming a secret society:

[The Illumination](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uSz1tR2SU28&t=13930)

The two decide to leave secret messages around the kingdom in order to leave clues for anyone else who may get curious about the nature of the world. Roa also begins construction on a secret base for the Society beneath her house. The two then [pay their respects](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uSz1tR2SU28&t=14535) at the grave Roa had built for Shakira before parting ways so Bijou could rest.
