For much of the second day Tam Gandr was absent from the kingdom, a fact which only served to deepen Gonathon’s concerns and anguish. For while he and Immerkind had developed [a plan](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=411) to remove the stain from the queen’s head, Gonathon’s thoughts still lingered on what exactly he wished to do with himself and what role the queen should play in his future. In his conversations with Immerkind he expressed [his heartfelt desire](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=3074) to serve the queen for the rest of his days, and wished to escape with her to a new land where they could be free of their past traumas.

Upon getting ready for their expedition into the dungeon, Immerkind and Gonathon realized that Tam had still not shown her face despite her pleas to have them assist her, and the two were forced to tackle the entire challenge on their own.

[Where is the queen?!](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=3805)

Emerging victorious from the dungeon, the two knights were shocked to find Tam had finally emerged from her slumber and was stumbling around the kingdom’s avenues in a delirious haze. While Gonathon began to panic once more, Immerkind forced him to retain his nerve and converse with the queen. Alas, any attempts at enacting their plan were foiled, [as time was not on their side](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=9016). Tam had scant few minutes to complete the riddles for the day and, as a true knight to the queen, Gonathon volunteered to assist her.

[A truly clueless queen](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=9122)

Guiding Tam by the hand to the location of the riddles, Gonathon’s attempts to support her proved less than sufficient, as the amnesiac queen’s mind was so fractured and unstable that she failed to comprehend the enigmas laid out for her. It was only with [the combined assistance](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=9407) of the humble knight, Immerkind, Lady Bloodflame, and Nerissa that Tam was able to overcome the riddle trial.

While Tam would [next delve](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=10489) into the day’s dungeon, Gonathon was unable to accompany her. Tasked with a number of burdensome duties, the queen’s most loyal knight was forced to take his leave. While Immerkind worked in the shadows after Gonathon’s departure to further their plot, it would seem that [yet another day](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=12018) would pass with Tam still unable to touch the precious memories Gonathon yearned her to remember.
