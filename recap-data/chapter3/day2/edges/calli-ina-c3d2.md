The past two days have not been too kind to Mordon Ramsay. Be it the loss of a dear friend or the subsequent smear campaign against her, it was hard for the chef to catch a break. Although there seemed to always be one person whom she could rely on. Was it because she was absent for most, if not all, drama? Perhaps... But Mordon didn't care.

After all, they were comrades. Whether it be [sharing their love of pets](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SbYxNyu5Zn4&t=3117s) or [completing community tasks](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SbYxNyu5Zn4&t=3863s), they seemed inseparable despite being apart for long periods.

And this camaraderie went well beyond simple acts, with Mordon gladly accepting Ina into her party and saying she feels [safe](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VXqngzJKNZU&t=9200s) with her, as well as Ina [defending](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SbYxNyu5Zn4&t=4790s) Mordon when conversation arose about the 'poisoning' of Shakira.

Whatever it was, it was strong, and the two seemed to have each other's backs through it all.
