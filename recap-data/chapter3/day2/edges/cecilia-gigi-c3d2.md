With drama, heartbreak, and murder rife in Libestal, the kingdom had truly become a dangerous place. Through all of this hardship, there was one thing this day proved could never be broken: the bond between two knights.

Following the tragedy that had befallen their queen the previous day, Gonathon and Immerkind were despondent, but still determined to save her. Upon waking, Gonathon read through the letter that Immerkind had left him, discussing her plans to remove the stain hat once and for all. As he met up with Immerkind, the green knight explained [her plan](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=411) to Gonathon: if they could get their hands on a sufficiently cute pet, they may be able to convince the queen to give up the memory sucking headpiece in exchange for the new companion. With their course of action decided upon, the two parted ways for a time.

[2011: The year of allergays](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=938)

Of course nothing could separate these two for long and following some time in the farming fields, Gonathon was reunited with Immerkind. It was here that they indulged in a long heart to heart about their futures.

Immerkind reflected on how some of their companions seemed to change little upon recovering their memories and wondered what she would be like when she remembered her old self. Would she be different? Would she still love Fia? She then asked Gonathon if he wanted to remember who he used to be. Gonathon was quick to reply that [all he wished for was](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=411) to live a peaceful life with his queen far from the pain and hardships of Libestal.

[AWOOOOOOO!](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=3275)

Immerkind was not entirely satisfied with this reply and urged Gonathon to consider his oath to protect the common people and to fight for Justice. Taking her words to heart, Gonathon promised that no matter what he would always help Immerkind, [a sentiment she eagerly returned.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=3193)

When asked the same question, [Immerkind's answer](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=3619) was also simple: she wished nothing more than to serve the princess and grow old with her. While Gonathon at first echoed a scheme by Shiori to be adopted by the princess's family, [he quickly asserted](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=3667) that even without a lofty title that Immerkind should still seek to be together with the princess. With their vows to each other reaffirmed, the two knights departed to meet up with Roa and their queen in order to confront the dungeon.

[We can do it...together!](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=3354)

Upon reaching the meet up spot, the two came to a [stark realization:](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=3843) the queen had never shown up and Roa had left with another party. With all other adventurers off in their own parties, Immerkind and Gonathon were left with only one solution: delve into the depths of the Labryinth of Knowledge on their own.

Such a challenge was already difficult enough with five members, but only two? While lesser heroes would have failed to accomplish this goal, the steadfast knights proved more than up for the task.

It was during their journey through the dungeon that something strange occurred. The personalities of Gonathon and Immerkind seemed to melt away, revealing [a pair that seemed to be in perfect sync with one another](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=8428). Was this the former personalities of the two knights showing through? Whatever the exact circumstances, [the duo emerged victorious](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=7761) over the boss of the dungeon and returned to Libestal together.

[Gonathon the Rapper](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=5299)

It was here that they were confronted by a delirious Queen Tam. Despite wishing to enact their plan to save her, [the two were forced](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=9407) to assist her in solving the Terminus riddles, a task which proved nearly insurmountable due to the queen's damaged mind.

Following this latest trial, the two met up again near Star Speaker Hamal, who gave the the task of trading with other Starborn. While the task seemed geared to exploit one of their companions, Gonathon was able to find a solution that involved [a simple trade](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=11506) of beef for coin, and explained that such a trade benefited both sides.

In one final trial for the day, Immerkind admitted to having lied to accomplish Hamal's task, something which Gonathon [could not stomach](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=11642). Through a slight of hand, Gonathon had Hamal ["forgive"](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=11727) Immerkind, and with one last exchange of pleasantries, the two departed for the day. While the future likely holds numerous hardships for the two of them, one thing was made clear: they could always count on one another.

[I could never have done it without you...](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8CAzdF1qb4&t=11805)
