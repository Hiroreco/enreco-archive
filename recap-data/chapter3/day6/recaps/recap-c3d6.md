## Day 6: For The Bonds They Share

The bonds they forged were fragile at the start,
As strangers met from worlds and miles apart.
Yet through each trial they came to understand
The trust that slowly grew in every heart.

Though fate would pull their woven threads away,
And send them down paths none could change or sway,
The hands of time still drew them back as one,
Bound by ties no distance could betray.

Along the road, so many tales were spun,
Of joy and grief beneath the selfsame sun.
Some dreams took flight, while others met their end,
Yet all became the threads that bound them then.

A phoenix fell before her song was done.
One grieved so deeply that her mind came undone.
A knight gave all to love that bound her fast,
While one wove tales where every soul belonged.

Yet through it all, they never stood alone.
Each sorrow shared became a burden known.
And every triumph, every fleeting smile,
Was brighter still because the group had grown.

Then duty called, and all stood side by side,
Whatever wounds or grudges they might hide.
Together they pressed onward through the dark,
With hope and purpose serving as their guide.

Yet what they found was not an ending near,
But something greater waiting to appear.
Another path unfolded from the last,
A fresh new tale that beckoned bright and clear.

Though all concluded sooner than they'd hoped,
And many threads were left still hanging loose,
Though stories lingered yearning for an end,
And dreams of closure faded out of view,

The journey's worth was never in its close,
Nor measured by the final page it chose.
It lived within the laughter that they shared,
And every memory gathered as it rose.

For none can doubt the smiles that lined the way,
Of those who walked and those who chose to stay.
The joy, the tears, the countless moments shared,
Are treasures no parting can take away.

And when the curtain falls on all they've known,
And every path returns its travelers home,
One truth remains, enduring through the years:

Not the destination,

But the bonds they shared along the way.
