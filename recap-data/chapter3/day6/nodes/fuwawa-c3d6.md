The final day had arrived, and it felt special for more than just being the end of the journey. Fuwawa now had a sister! Not only that, but she had her own group as well: Advent!

Though she was still a little confuzzled about the sister part. Just the day before, her identical counterpart couldn't accept that they were related. She wondered if anything had changed overnight. Perhaps things would be different now? (see [Fuwawa-Mococo](#edge:fuwawa-mococo)).

There was certainly no shortage of things happening today. A trial to uncover Shakira's murderer, a picnic, and for some reason, Shiori was constructing what appeared to be a death trap.

[Be careful up there!](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mIsHnxTVoFM&t=1514s)

It was a day packed with events. The first seemed to be the picnic, as everyone gathered around a beautifully laid red-and-white checkered blanket, preparing for a feast of wonderful foo—

Oh.

This wasn't a picnic at all.

It was the trial.

B-but she wanted food...

[Where's my steak...](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mIsHnxTVoFM&t=4916s)

The trial began with some semblance of order, but before long it completely [derailed,](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mIsHnxTVoFM&t=5461s) leaving everyone unable to reach a conclusion about the culprit. Fuwawa, however, did her best to prove that Mordon was [guilty.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mIsHnxTVoFM&t=6089s) As a fellow chef, she simply couldn't bear the thought of another culinary peer poisoning their customer.

Yet amidst the chaos of the trial, another revelation emerged. It was discovered that she and her fellow Advent members were [_cursed_,](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mIsHnxTVoFM&t=6338s) and that this curse had been the very reason everyone began splitting into opposing sides in the first place.

Naturally, the heroes resolved to free Advent from the curse.

[Through a game of tag.](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mIsHnxTVoFM&t=7248s)

As one does.

In the end, every member of Advent was freed. With everyone united once more, the heroes ventured into the Nullspace, overcame its many challenges, found the Outsider, and uncovered yet another shocking twist (see [Faust](#node:faust)).

The fluffy one's third journey had [come to an end,](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mIsHnxTVoFM&t=11522s) leaving her to await whatever adventure awaited her next.
