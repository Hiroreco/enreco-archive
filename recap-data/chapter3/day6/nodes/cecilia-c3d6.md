The day began like any other.

Knight Immerkind greeted the little princess Bijou, who seemed to have finally gotten over her grudge and [become friends again](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=euqA3GB8Jc0&t=574) with the knight. Proud of her latest accomplishment, Immerkind eagerly showed off the [golden castle](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=euqA3GB8Jc0&t=850) she had painstakingly constructed from every "gold" block she could find. Later, she [excitedly gave Sister Shiori a tour](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=euqA3GB8Jc0&t=2613), proudly displaying both the castle and the Fia hat perched atop her head.

The tour, however, was cut short as the knight was whisked away once more by her ever-changing whims.

["How do you not remember this?"](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=euqA3GB8Jc0&t=3640)

Immerkind had found Roa, who appeared to have lost her memories. Determined to help, she led the pink cat through the elaborate path beyond the castle wall. Together, they boarded a boat and ventured into the world [outside](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=euqA3GB8Jc0&t=3911) the walls.

As they traversed the strange lands beyond, Immerkind recounted the bizarre sight of a possessed Shakira floating through the air. She even tried reminding Roa about [her hips](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=euqA3GB8Jc0&t=4418) in hopes of jogging her memory. After a long journey filled with stories and reminiscing, the pair returned to the castle walls.

Soon after, Immerkind encountered a cheerful Nerissa, who promptly gifted her... mud.

["What a strange thing to do."](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=euqA3GB8Jc0&t=4642)

The knight barely had time to process the gift before Nerissa returned once more, informing her that she had also been the one who gifted the [Fia hat](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=euqA3GB8Jc0&t=4642) resting on her head. Yet in the same breath, Nerissa declared that she [detested Fia](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=euqA3GB8Jc0&t=4690) before abruptly departing.

The contradiction left Immerkind thoroughly confused and quietly sulking in a corner of the castle.

Seeking distraction, the knight visited the church, only to discover a mysterious [hole](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=euqA3GB8Jc0&t=4787) in its floor. Together with Gonathon and Mordon, she discussed the recent [events](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=euqA3GB8Jc0&t=5311) unfolding throughout the kingdom, including Roa's attempts to escape the "simulation" and the growing curiosity surrounding the hole beneath the church.

The trio descended into the deepest reaches of the holy site, where they stumbled upon a surprisingly cozy [abode](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=euqA3GB8Jc0&t=5760). They searched every room, sifted through diaries, and investigated the home for clues. Just as the mystery seemed to deepen, distant [music](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=euqA3GB8Jc0&t=5837) echoed through the air.

The Advent picnic had begun.

With no answers to show for their investigation, the trio returned to find an enormous crowd gathered outside. What awaited them was far more than a picnic.

It was a trial.

Mordon assembled her legal [counsel](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=euqA3GB8Jc0&t=6507): NinoIna, Gonathon, and Immerkind. Chaos quickly followed as accusations flew from every direction and testimonies contradicted one another.

Then, suddenly, the Hot Pink One took center stage.

She boldly [claimed](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=euqA3GB8Jc0&t=7841) that the five members of Advent were the true culprits and that a purification ritual at the castle was necessary to save them.

As the crowd made its way toward the castle, they crossed paths with Princess Bijou, who cheerfully announced that she had killed [Shakira](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=euqA3GB8Jc0&t=8078).

Her confession was met not with outrage but with confusion and indifference.

The united group then launched a frantic chase through the castle halls in pursuit of Advent. After much running, cornering, and confusion, all five were finally captured.

With the ritual complete, their friends in Advent were purified and freed from the curse that had corrupted them.

At last, Fia presented the heroes with a Looking-Glass Shard, opening the way to the mysterious [Nullspace](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=euqA3GB8Jc0&t=11264).

Within this strange realm, the group battled stains and overcame a series of challenges while searching for the truth behind the mysteries plaguing 2011 Libestal. At the end of their journey, they encountered Faust and faced a powerful foe known as ["The Order"](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=euqA3GB8Jc0&t=12167).

After defeating the boss, a greater truth was revealed.

Faust was actually [the Outsider](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=euqA3GB8Jc0&t=12555).

And with that revelation, a new Curse emerged.

Immerkind gasped as the entity's overwhelming power washed across Nullspace itself, sending ripples through reality. Realizing there was no time left, Fia used the last of her strength to teleport the group back to the safety of the library.
