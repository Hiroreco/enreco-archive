<!-- status: Quite Afflicted -->

Bijou awakens once more, yet remains afflicted, but there is still important work to be done with Advent's picnic. She also knows that after this day is done, she will be able to rest and recover if the affliction persists.

She first [meets with Immerkind](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-v3wKaDqyI4&t=365), checking out the castles that her knight had built. She notices Immerkind's new hat, and when Immerkind explains it's a hat based on Fia, Bijou is disappointed with how her knight still trusts in Fia. Still, the two continue to get along, with Immerkind promising to lend Bijou her pickaxe as she gathers materials to make legendary charms.

Soon, Roa calls Bijou, seemingly unable to remember what had happened in the prior days. Bijou decides to stick with her for a while, [assuring her that she is a friend](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-v3wKaDqyI4&t=1445). (See: [Bijou-Roa](#edge:bijou-raora)).

After spending time questing with Roa and then upgrading her charms, Bijou checks in with Shiori about the preparations for the picnic. When Shiori asks how Bijou would want to kill the others at the picnic, Bijou states that she [would like for them to burn](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-v3wKaDqyI4&t=3120). They also discover that they can place Shakira's body over the fire, with Bijou falling into it when trying to retrieve it.

["I burn for my sins."](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-v3wKaDqyI4&t=3240)

Wanting to prepare some music for the picnic, Bijou decides to return to the Labyrinth of Knowledge, as it was the one dungeon she had not found the secret disc in yet. Staying on call with Shiori, the two discussed who else they could try to pin Shakira's death on, [including the Hot Pink One as a possibility](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-v3wKaDqyI4&t=3915).

Later within the dungeon, Bijou is also [called by Immerkind](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-v3wKaDqyI4&t=4800), who hopes to meet to ask about something later. Bijou wonders what this might be and why Immerkind wouldn't just be able to ask over the phone.

As it turns out, it was not Immerkind who wanted to meet and ask something directly, but instead Lady Bloodflame, who wanted to meet with Bijou to have Shakira's body returned. Bijou tries to deny having Shakira's body and delay talking about it, but since Lady Bloodflame does not back down, Bijou decides to...

[Make her escape!](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-v3wKaDqyI4&t=5510)

Escaping to the picnic site, Bijou stashes Shakira's body within the flames. It is ultimately [found not too long later](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-v3wKaDqyI4&t=5700), but it is not of too much consequence, as the body was not found on Bijou herself.

The picnic ultimately ends up becoming a trial over Shakira's murder, where Roa ends up accusing Bijou, now remembering the events of the previous day. Shiori takes Bijou's defense as a lawyer, accusing Roa of insanity, causing the crowd to end up going on a field trip to Roa's house and then the fourth wall after.

With Mordon declared innocent at the trial, the Hot Pink One and the Peasant reveal the curse lingering over the members of Advent, bringing all to the castle for the Rite of Purification. However, tired of the incompetence of the others, Bijou returns to Shakira's grave first, claiming the body once more. Standing before the others, she announces being tired of the charade and that she was Shakira's killer!

["I have had enough of this!"](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-v3wKaDqyI4&t=7890)

Bijou taunts Roa with the body, proclaiming that she will only return Shakira's body if the others manage to catch Advent during the Rite of Purification. During the rite, Bijou manages to evade capture through hiding in a closet for much of the time, once more [noting the incompetence of the others](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-v3wKaDqyI4&t=8600). Knowing that Advent had escaped but believing that they needed to progress anyway, Bijou did return Shakira's body to Roa...only for the rite to begin again!

During the second rite, Bijou is eventually caught by the others. As she is waiting for the rite's completion, she begins to feel the curse lifting, with [her affliction lessening to the point of letting her breathe](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-v3wKaDqyI4&t=10460)! After the rite's completion, Bijou meets once more with Roa, apologizing for her actions and promising to atone for her sins now that the curse has been lifted.

Following the rite, the group of heroes heads off to enter Nullspace and try to reach its heart. However, when the rest of the group enters, [Bijou finds herself getting left behind!](https://youtu.be/-v3wKaDqyI4?t=10977) Fortunately, the gods can help her catch up to the others.

Reaching the Heart of Nullspace, the group runs into Faust, who, somewhat obviously, turns out to be the Outsider! (See: [Faust](#node:faust)) When the Outsider asks Bijou to lend him her power to use the Egg of Creation, [Bijou agrees](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-v3wKaDqyI4&t=12470). After all, "may the lands burn."

Once reunited with the others in the white room once more, Bijou [meets with Shiori](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-v3wKaDqyI4&t=12910) one last time to discuss the curse. It seems it was bold of the others to assume that the curse was the cause of their actions, and it was not of their own volition...
