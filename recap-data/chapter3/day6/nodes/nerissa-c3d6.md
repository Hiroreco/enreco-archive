**BLESSED ARE THE BROKE**

Nerissa clung to her daily routine as though it were a replacement for her missing horn.

Day after day, she worked tirelessly, taking on quest after quest in pursuit of greater wealth.

Yet no matter how much she earned, it was never enough.

_Never enough. Never enough._

Eventually, she began to question the purpose of it all. What was the point of endlessly hoarding coins if they would never satisfy her? Perhaps it would be better to spend them on the things she enjoyed.

Or, on occasion, spend them helping others.

Maybe she had truly changed.

Maybe she had finally become free of her love for money.

["I'm trying to help the poor!"](#embed:https://youtu.be/hjskrraiXp4?t=1963)

When she crossed paths with Mordon Ramsay, Nerissa generously offered the chef a portion of her fortune: ten thousand coins.

A life-changing sum for most.

To Nerissa's shock, Mordon refused.

The proud chef insisted she could earn the money herself. Nerissa attempted to chase after her and force the charity upon her, but Mordon was far too quick.

And so the transaction vanished into the wind.

**DO YOU REMEMBER?**

Nerissa "The Broke" Juliet had decided that helping others was far more rewarding than simply accumulating wealth.

Not just financially.

Emotionally, too.

This time, the recipient of her generosity was Roa Pandora.

The pink cat had somehow survived the ritual performed by the Church of Inkwell the day before. Unfortunately, the experience seemed to have shattered parts of her memory. Faces, places, and events slipped through her grasp no matter how hard she tried to remember.

Determined to help, Nerissa invited Roa on a [little adventure](https://youtu.be/hjskrraiXp4?t=1592).

Together, they traveled beyond the kingdom's green fields and towering mountains, toward the mysterious place known only as The Gap.

The journey did not go as expected.

The moment they arrived, Roa suffered a complete breakdown. She shouted about the Wall, the Gap, the Sheep, and countless fragmented memories that refused to fit together.

Yet through patience and persistence, Nerissa managed to guide her through the confusion.

By the end of the journey, Roa had become enlightened once again.

**SHE'S COOKED!**

The broke princess continued wandering the kingdom, keeping a close eye on the other heroes and the secrets they carried.

Curiosity was one of her defining traits.

Sometimes, however, secrets had a way of finding her first.

While enjoying a bowl of potato salad at the Advent picnic, Nerissa suddenly felt someone whisper in her ear.

It was the Stain Princess, Bijou.

And what she revealed left Nerissa horrified.

[Behold!](#embed:https://youtu.be/hjskrraiXp4?t=2492)

Before her lay the body of the late Shakira.

Burned.

Roasted.

Reduced to a charred corpse resting among the flames.

Nerissa recoiled in horror. Words caught in her throat before finally escaping as a scream.

Little did she know that, before the day was over, she would suffer a fate eerily similar to the fallen Phoenix.

[Goodbye, cruel world!](#embed:https://youtu.be/hjskrraiXp4?t=2642)

---

**WITCH TRIAL**

With Shakira's body finally recovered, attention turned toward the most important question of all:

Who killed her?

Nerissa took charge of organizing the investigation and selected a judge from among those gathered.

Eventually, Lady Bloodflame was chosen to preside over the proceedings.

Order was called.

Testimonies were heard.

Suspects pleaded their innocence.

And then everything immediately descended into chaos.

Arguments erupted from every corner. Accusations flew freely. Evidence appeared, disappeared, and contradicted itself moments later.

At some point, it became impossible to tell whether anyone was solving the mystery or making it worse.

[What even is happening here?](#embed:https://youtu.be/hjskrraiXp4?t=3251)

A question shared by nearly everyone present.

---

**THAT'S A WRAP!**

In the end, the truth was stranger than anyone expected.

Nerissa and the other members of Advent had been afflicted by a curse. The corruption was the very reason so many heroes had found themselves at odds with one another throughout their journey.

Once the truth came to light, the rest of Libestal united to save them.

Together, they performed the [Rite of Purification](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hjskrraiXp4&t=5372), freeing Advent from the curse's influence and restoring them to themselves.

At last, with old conflicts resolved and former enemies reunited, the heroes set their sights on their final destination.

Nullspace.

A place where answers awaited.

A place where their story, at least for now, would reach its conclusion.
