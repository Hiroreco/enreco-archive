A different tone. A different place.
All these white walls wrapped around her like an embrace.

Roa woke to the scent of a rich, toasted elixir.

With a hollow smile, she drank the familiar concoction and began her day as she always did.

_Normal? What is that?_ she wondered.

She spent the morning on routine tasks: helping the citizens of Libestal with their quests, tending the forge, and returning home.

_My home? Why does it look strange?_

Ah. That poster. It shouldn't be there.

With a shrug, she stepped back outside.

Once she was [ready](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mZM0OO3VK9s&t=250), she called Bijou, her beloved friend. Her memories felt hazy and fragmented, but the stained gem princess reassured her that everything was fine. Convinced the pink cat was simply groggy, Bijou handed her a baked potato.

_Edible food? For nourishment?_ she pondered.

As Roa went about her day, each new encounter brought a sudden splitting [headache](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mZM0OO3VK9s&t=1440).

Without thinking, she took a bite of the potato.

The pain vanished instantly.

_Why does it hurt? Am I missing something?_ she wondered.

Later, Roa met Immerkind once more. This time, the green knight guided her back to the castle gap they had explored [yesterday](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mZM0OO3VK9s&t=2520). Taking the potato from her hands, Immerkind helped Roa begin piecing together the memories she had lost (see [Cecilia-Raora](#edge:cecilia-raora)).

Before long, a confrontation erupted in the city's [fields](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mZM0OO3VK9s&t=4425), centered around the body of a dead woman.

Lady Bloodflame stormed away with the corpse slung over her shoulder, dragging Roa along with her. The pink smith resisted at first, but relented when she finally looked upon the object of contention.

_She looks so familiar. Who is she?_

The red knight carefully cleaned the body, restoring its [dignity](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mZM0OO3VK9s&t=4915), before laying it to rest within the gravestone where it belonged.

_Tears...? Why am I crying? Was she my beloved?_

Then Lady Bloodflame spoke softly.

She painted a vision within the flames.

> Roa, as you look into the fire as it grows, you see golden... orange... red.
> And eventually something changes.
> Nobody else necessarily sees this. It's just you.
> Within the flames, a silhouette... wings... a familiar laugh... a familiar smile.
> Who could this be?
> It's not a ghost. It's not a resurrection.
> But it's just enough.
> The shape turns... and it looks directly at you, Roa.
> And at that instant, the memory returns. Not because the Mighty Phoenix tells you.
> But because seeing her **_reignites the fire_** in your memory.
> The **poison**.
> The **betrayal**.
> The **person** responsible.
> The **details** forgotten.
> It all floods back to you at once.
> It comes rushing back.

She remembered.

The chains wrapped around her mind shattered.

The fire of her love severed the oppressive **_bonds_** that had bound her for so long.

And so Roa cried out:

[**_FOR JUSTICE!_**](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mZM0OO3VK9s&t=5070)

With the spirit of her beloved wife blazing beside her, Roa set out to confront the evil lurking within these lands.

A chaotic trial followed as accusations flew and truths surfaced. In the end, every member of the group known as **_Advent_** was hunted down, [captured](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mZM0OO3VK9s&t=9675), and purified. One by one, the curse that had twisted them into villains was stripped away.

At last, the path to Nullspace opened.

As the world faded into a familiar white garden, Roa renewed her vow.

The fire within her burned brighter than ever, tempering her resolve to see the revolution she had begun through to its end.

And this time, she would not forget why she fought.
