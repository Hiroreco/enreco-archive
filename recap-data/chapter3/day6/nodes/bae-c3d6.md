Peasantry.

Humbleness and being content with what one has. To be satisfied with your lot in life.

That is the way of the peasantry.

But the past few days have changed her. The gold that lined her pockets has also lined her heart. Overcharging her fellow heroes, using their goodwill to her advantage, was this truly Peasant The Bae?

The [ancestors have spoken to Bae](https://youtu.be/Qh33RM1yOtI?t=186) in her dreams, rebuked her for turning her back on their way of life.

Perhaps her ancestors were right. To set things right...

EVERYTHING MUST GO!

Too much wealth. Too much stuff. It was time to [sell](https://youtu.be/Qh33RM1yOtI?t=283).

A gold per iron bar. 10 gold per diamond.

Though she was reminded to be humble, Peasant still has her pride. She interrupted a conversation between Ina the Hero and Nerissa Juliet, [defending yesterday's](https://youtu.be/Qh33RM1yOtI?t=708) overpriced sale. Still, Bae must respect game as she and [Ina compared their Lake Kings.](https://youtu.be/Qh33RM1yOtI?t=744)

As Peasant stocked up her shop, she finally heard about the plot. Admittedly, Peasant spent most of her days in Libestal in peace, fishing for the illustrious royal fishes, fighting stains out in the field, and pining for Immerkind. So, when she heard Lady Bloodflame call out the green knight, Bae was quick to overhear.

Bae finally learned of [the stolen body of Shakira](https://youtu.be/Qh33RM1yOtI?t=1277). Of how it was in Bijou's hands. And what would Bae do with this information? To know a beloved friend's corpse was desecrated?

Talk to Immerkind.

[The Peasant walked up](https://youtu.be/Qh33RM1yOtI?t=1368) to Immerkind, the latter responding with a voice that sent the rich rat's heart racing. In an effort to impress her hopeless crush, Peasant showed off her shop...

"A Lake King? for 20,000 gold?!"

No, no, it was going on sale for [5,000](https://youtu.be/Qh33RM1yOtI?t=1442), Bae quickly changed the price. It wouldn't impress the knight if she had known how much Bae used to charge her fellow heroes.

Still, today was the last day, and Bae must try to shoot her shot once again.

A dozen red roses for Immerkind (and a ring, but not for now), which she accepted! And would cherish them.

Bae would then come across the conspiracy again, witnessing the group whisper of what lurks beneath the Church. Bae followed them, silently observing the events that finally fell before her. Including [Ina the Hero](https://youtu.be/Qh33RM1yOtI?t=2012).

And later, [Roa's grief](https://youtu.be/Qh33RM1yOtI?t=2448) as the cat finally remembered her wife.

Peasant also reunited with the Hot Pink One after the latter finally woke from her slumber, and apparently, there was another picnic! Surely this one would be a calm and enjoyable event. Unlike the last time.

It looked like a splendid one, with much food to enjoy and enjoyable company.

And a trial for Mordon Ramsay. Seems like another interesting thing is about to happen.

They listened to Lady Bloodflame as she recapped what had happened over the past few days. To find the trust they all deserve to learn. To find JUSTICE for Shakira (and her hips).

Mordon called her fellow wolves to represent her, while Nerissa reasoned that because it was Mordon's cake that Shakira ate, it was right to cast suspicion on her.

Defendant and accuser talk, Peasant and the Hot Pink one [whispered back and forth](https://youtu.be/Qh33RM1yOtI?t=3196), commenting on the trial. Surprisingly, Shakira was pregnant.

Finally, it was revealed... Bijou was revealed to be the true murderer. But Shiori, Bijou's representative, quickly pointed out the weakness in Roa's statement... **Roa herself**. Roa was insane, has been for the past week.

Her house was certainly [an uncommon design choice](https://youtu.be/Qh33RM1yOtI?t=3402). Determined to prove her point, Roa showed them the Fourth Wall.

Beyond the castle walls, beyond to farther lands was... an invisible glass wall? What was the connection to Shakira's death? Bae was more concerned that she had joined a cult against her will.

Back to the picnic, where they were about to vote on Mordon's innocence. Just then, Bae beckoned the Hot Pink One of the revelation from the gods. And of a missing person's own script to be said. They wondered about a good time to relay the message and decided after the trial.

Bae trusted Immerkind, and since Immerkind believed in Mordon's innocence, so did Peasant. And the Peasant [bribed the Hot Pink One](https://youtu.be/Qh33RM1yOtI?t=4025) to her side as well.

Mordon was finally exonerated. And now, Peasant and the Hot Pink One shone light on the happenings of Advent. A curse lingered in them and caused a divide in the group. They must now all perform the purification rite at the castle to save their souls.

There, they were finally reunited with Tam and now must catch Advent in a game of hide and seek. The rebels were tricky, but ultimately, perseverance and a round 2 had paved the way for our heroes to save their friends.

But first, Peasant must talk to Immerkind. (See: [Bae-Cecilia](#edge:bae-cecilia))

Satisfied with her confession, Peasant and her companions entered the final dungeon.

And of course, they won.

Faust shortly showed up. (See: [Faust](#node:faust))

He asked Bae if she would join him in destroying the world. To start anew.

Of course not! That would mean destroying her gold! Luckily, when all seemed lost as The Curse revealed itself, Fia saved Bae once again.
