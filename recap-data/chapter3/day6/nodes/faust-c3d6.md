After an arduous journey through the depths of Nullspace and the defeat of its guardian, the Order, the heroes finally breached its heart—only for Faust to drop his guise and reveal his true identity as the **Outsider**.

[The Egg of Creation](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/6HUbJshXMAI?t=9048)

The Outsider confessed that the trials, hidden memories, and challenges the heroes faced were all orchestrated by him to cultivate their unique power: **Revelation**. He then presented them with the ultimate secret of Nullspace, the **Egg of Creation**, a primordial crucible capable of remaking the entire multiverse.

However, carrying out this plan demands a terrible cost and an impossible choice:

**A Universe Must Die for Another to Be Born**

To create a new multiverse free from the Origin's cruel design, the existing worlds must first be erased entirely. Every history, every bond, every life and memory would be wiped away so that something new could take their place.

**Someone Must Wield the Artifact**

Though the Outsider discovered a means to reshape reality, he cannot activate the Egg himself. To overcome this limitation, he called upon the heroes and invoked the ominous bargain he had once offered, asking them to lend their infinite potential to awaken the artifact's power.

The heroes gave their answers to his proposal, but before the Outsider could respond, the confrontation was suddenly interrupted by a chaotic twist. The Curse had survived yet again, hijacking the fallen body of the Order to use as its vengeful new vessel.

As the Outsider and the Curse prepared to clash, Fia urgently warned the heroes just as the Looking-Glass Shard activated, whisking them away to parts unknown.
