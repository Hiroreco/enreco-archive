...here it was. The final day had arrived. Everyone would confront The Outsider in Nullspace. Preparations were being made, tensions were high, and yet... the little witch didn't have her mind on any of that. The only thing she could think about was the questions still haunting her from the day before. She had seen visions of her life that jolted buried memories in her heart, she had her... real sister back...

As she tried talking to WOOF about all of this (mostly in an attempt to calm down), her little buddy pointed to the chest by her side. Did it want to show her something? Opening it, she saw the journal that creeped her out a few days ago, and... a letter.

[" 'Dear Moco-chan,' ...that's right, my nickname..."](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/5fdrGFsRtQw?si=SBKHg7BvJylaexB7&t=500)

This letter was from Fuwawa, who had poured so much love and care into it. Mococo contemplated this as her thoughts gradually calmed down... she... she wanted to accept, but... was it okay to be this greedy? Would Fuwawa and Elizabeth both be okay with staying sisters? Is her relationship with her Libestan older sister even real considering she wasn't in her recollections at all...?

Heading out with her mind still cloudy, Mococo's smile slowly returned to her face as she checked her sales. Which were, once again, fairly successful! MONEY!!! Her mood rose a little bit, though it was threatened again once she found that [old message](https://www.youtube.com/live/5fdrGFsRtQw?si=F96dYC-H2zcZuEF7&t=751) addressed to her.

Shopping. Let's do some shopping! That always makes things better! The knight Immerkind was selling enigmatic stones, which sounds nice! Although she clearly still hasn't learned to call her. After all, the little witch's strong passive magic of [ignoring calls](https://www.youtube.com/live/5fdrGFsRtQw?si=kDdMogvIvAey5q3h&t=908) was still active! That's her fault, at this rate.

...

She couldn't avoid her forever (see [Fuwawa-Mococo](#edge:fuwawa-mococo)).

...

After talking through their feelings for a bit, Fuwawa wanted Mococo to talk to Sister Shiori at her church. She didn't really see a reason not to go, so they [did just that](https://www.youtube.com/live/5fdrGFsRtQw?si=MXwO9IDnmimUDRlK&t=1278)! Fuwawa seemed to want to explain their coven in more detail to her sister, but Shiori didn't think it necessary.

Mococo was a little confused, apparently, the fact that she didn't know a lot was a good thing? She still felt cared for by everyone there deep inside, so it was okay to her that she was assigned to be the one to cover for Advent's public image. After all, she was still everyone's landlord! She'll just EVICT them if they become problems, mwehehe!

[A secret!](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/5fdrGFsRtQw?si=w_GtHAGCf3qYBClk&t=1279)

Once she separated from Advent, Mococo had a few final solo endeavours. Her business was suddenly BOOMING!! EVERYONE wanted her charms, and she had to get to work to make more to keep up with demand, but that was okay!! [MOCOPOLY WAS BACK](https://www.youtube.com/live/5fdrGFsRtQw?si=nmtznl32huLhat03&t=3077)!!

Not only that, but Lady Bloodflame approached her and Fuwawa after some time as well. Mococo asked if she was fine with still being sisters despite them not being blood-related... and to her relief, the knight was already intending to keep her promise going strong. In fact, it was now double! As she swore to protect Fuwawa as well.

[patpat](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/5fdrGFsRtQw?si=chSQbiM3Wv6ZL4Qa&t=3389)

Eventually, Nerissa Juliet called her for a picnic... and it turned out to be a trial for Mordon Ramsay all along! It... went off the rails extremely quickly, and it was very overwhelming. Elizabeth was up at the judge's post, but at least she had Fuwawa for comfort. Although that very much didn't go as planned, she was quickly thrust into a new situation as she and the other members of the coven were thrown into a CHASE!

They successfully evaded their pursuers for a long time and were on their way to do it again! Mococo in particular was their sneakiest soldier, [not being caught once](https://www.youtube.com/live/5fdrGFsRtQw?si=ZqrGsQZTyh8q_U7P&t=9175) in almost thirty minutes of pursuit! But alas, the pressure eventually became too much, and she was captured by her old, mortal enemy, NinoIna. How dare she!!!

The curse was supposedly lifted, but truth be told, Mococo didn't really feel any different as she made her way through Nullspace. Oh well! None of this complicated stuff [made any sense](https://www.youtube.com/live/5fdrGFsRtQw?si=sVmK6AFYMiOWJFFX&t=11217) to her, anyway. She was just happy to look toward the future (see: [Faust](#node:faust)). In a new world, she'd have the time to make even more money, as well as treasure the connections that really matter, regardless of whether everyone else treated them as evil for it.
