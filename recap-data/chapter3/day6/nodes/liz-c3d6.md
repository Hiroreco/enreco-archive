With the start of the day and the final dungeon looming over, Lady Bloodflame awoke, taking a brief moment to admire her hard work on her garden. Flowery hedges now lined the edges, keeping it well shaded and protected. The aroma of each flower was pleasant to the senses as she took a deep breath with a smile.

Still, trouble lingered in the back of her mind. Without the body of Shakira, would she be able to revive as a mighty phoenix? Or be trapped in purgatory? Pulling her phone out, she called her fellow knight, [Gonathon](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6HUbJshXMAI&t=658).

A turn to her Princess, Nerissa (see [Liz-Nerissa](#edge:liz-nerissa)), for help led her to the very person she needed to seek.

[Princess Bijou](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6HUbJshXMAI&t=911)

Hope for peace and revival.

Quickly, she called Roa, glad that she could provide relief to the woman who had lost her loved one, her life. Only for the pretty kitty to ask who Lady Bloodflame _was_, assuming she was some [telemarketer](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6HUbJshXMAI&t=1021) trying to sell her something.

That really lit a fire below her to set things right. She could not let Roa forget her wife, forget her, forget everyone!

With pure determination, the Jade Knight searched until she found Bijou, confronting her. Immediately, the smaller woman drank a potion of invisibility and disappeared before her eyes, leaving Lady Bloodflame frustrated.

_"What you seek lies near."_

Within the nearby fire lay the very body she was seeking. As if some cruel joke. Nerissa [jumped](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6HUbJshXMAI&t=2386) in to save the body, dying in the process, before handing it off to Lady Bloodflame.

Adding insult to injury, Bijou dared call her a pet. _As if._

But before any proper burial could be done, she was requested to be the [head judge](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6HUbJshXMAI&t=3043) of the trial of Mordon Ramsay. And how chaotic the trial was, calling people up to testify against one another.

Yet there was no actual proof of anything. No physical evidence to show it was Mordon, or that it was Bijou. There was something about a wall, but she had been directed to stay on the picnic blanket by Nerissa. So she and the suspected killer, Mordon, waited.

When a few returned, Ramsay was suddenly dropped into the fire pit without [judgment](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6HUbJshXMAI&t=3668). Her eyes locked onto Shiori, who feigned innocence in the action. Still, she tried to run a vote of the jury.

Five to five with Nerissa standing in the center, unable to vote due to running the trial. Making her call as the head judge, she made her final call in the case against the defendant.

[Not Guilty.](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6HUbJshXMAI&t=4209)

With the entire case settled, Lady Bloodflame found herself pulled aside by Gonathon, who requested that she join the knights of justice against the cursed Advent group. In fact, her fellow knight implied _she_ was cursed by them too! Shaking it off, they all moved to the castle to progress towards defeating the Outsider.

A rite of purification would need to be done in order to cleanse Advent, it seemed. Without warning, everyone except for the cursed group was in a cage, staring down small versions of Advent. Blinking, Lady Bloodflame watched them scatter off into the castle before they were released.

Immediately, the remaining members took off after them all, trying to capture them all. It took a lot of exhaustion and running, but they soon had them captured.

With the ritual completed, they all made their way back into the portal and approached Faust (See Faust), taking on the task of the final dungeon.

When presented with the option to destroy everything they had known, to restart from scratch, it didn't take much for Lady Bloodflame to make her choice.

["No."](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6HUbJshXMAI&t=9236)

It seemed like all of their efforts were foiled again. They would have to fight another exhausting fight to try to stop this Outsider. Hopefully, the next time would be the last.
