#### TAKE RESPONSIBILITY

---

Sister Shiori, Nyavella—all of them were just Shiori.

Shiori of the Novella, and she was going to have an evil day. The main event had finished yesterday, but there were still loose strings attached. Namely, a trial. And with it, an execution; it wouldn't be exciting without a rather bloody finish.

But, as Shiori noted as she built the platform by the picnic blankets, dying by flame would take far too long. Maybe having a leap of faith to prove their innocence would be far [quicker](https://youtu.be/6r0cA5reqOA?t=1597).

[And who keeps stealing from her Church?!](#embed:https://youtu.be/6r0cA5reqOA?t=1374)

Advent refined the platform and included a place to hide a [certain body with hips that don't lie.](https://youtu.be/6r0cA5reqOA?t=1931) Shiori was truly a Sister of the Cloth to think of a funeral pyre for Shakira, and soon enough the preparations were done.

At the picnic, they finally discovered [Shakira's body](https://youtu.be/6r0cA5reqOA?t=4414) beneath the stage. Lady Bloodflame dived for the body, enduring flames, and began to accost Roa with it as an attempt to bring her memories back. To the cat, a madwoman had started shoving a stranger's dead body at her face, and she fled in horror while the others gave chase.

As Nerissa Juliet, Bijou, and Shiori watched on, they too noted that since Bijou no longer had the body, they couldn't accuse her anymore. Any evidence was eaten by the fire, and Lady Bloodflame's own fingerprints would be fresh. But they must call others to the picnic.

Ah, Shiori might as well be the one to call Gonathon.

[Or Nyavella...](#embed:https://youtu.be/6r0cA5reqOA?t=4521)

Unfortunately, her dramatic monologue was once again cut off by [a rocking song](https://youtu.be/6r0cA5reqOA?t=4540). Can't a girl have some silence for a dramatic moment?

_"Uh, whatever. Come out, Nyavella."_

A sound of shattering glass echoed in Shiori's being as they switched. Nyavella brought out her phone to call Gonathon. To invite him to the picnic, and perhaps a bit more...

> Gonathon is already in a call and cannot answer.

Tragic. Utterly. Tragic.

Would Gonathon be able to talk to Nyavella again before they inevitably part ways? It remained to be seen.

Finally, they all started to gather at the picnic. The trial of Mordon Ramsay began. Lady Bloodflame would be the judge, as Mordon's fellow Wolves tried to prove her innocence. Doubt was still on her until, finally, Roa stepped forward and pointed a claw at Bijou. Roa had finally regained her memories. Of Bijou's trial. Of.... Shakira.

With the spotlight put on her beloved, Shiori also stepped up, casting doubt on Roa's statement. Should they believe a madwoman? She was never... 'right' after Shakira died. Look at the state of [her house!](https://youtu.be/6r0cA5reqOA?t=5553) Is this a truth the jury should believe?

Irked by the judgment, Roa dragged the rest of the jurors to convince them of her sanity, toward the Fourth Wall... Shiori wasn't sure where they went and just decided to stay by the picnic.

[Oh no, Mordon!](#embed:https://youtu.be/6r0cA5reqOA?t=5685)

Dang, death by fire was too long, and Mordon managed to save herself by breaking part of the platform. The jurors came back, dissatisfied with Roa, and finally came to decide Mordon's fate.

Yeah, she's innocent, the majority decided.

But it appears they _must_ get back to the actual plot planned for today, and it was revealed that Advent was... _Cursed!_

And so, it was back to the castle they went for the purification ritual.

One must wonder what a purification ritual would entail. Would it be conducted in a sacred pool of water with chants pleading the gods to save the damned soul? Or perhaps touched by a saint, or a non-hot-pink goddess?

For Libestal, you must be smacked with wooden swords. Again and again until you inevitably pass out.

One by one they were caught until the fuzzy one remained... and perhaps she was too intent on winning the game as she hid. But the alarm bells rang and she was found. A tight chase with only a few seconds to spare, but the Shining Stars did it; they had succeeded.

Advent was purified. And now, the final dungeon.

It contained a series of challenges, some of which were familiar. Each challenge won revealed more of the Outsider's thoughts until the final reveal (see: [Faust](#node:faust)).

The mysterious individual gave Shiori a choice. Would she join in destroying this world? To start again?

[**Yes.**](#embed:https://youtu.be/6r0cA5reqOA?t=11186)

Again. Again. Shiori had lived, and remembered, the Libestals from the past few adventures.

Again and again, nobody but **The Archiver** understood.

What good would it be to live in such an isolated world? Love, friendship? They never seemed to last.

But as the choice was made, the curse still survived, and Shiori was once saved by Fia.

Back to the white room filled with flowers.

---

But what happened to Nyavella?

Underneath the Church of Inkwell, a few of the Wolves broke in, searching for the secret that lay beneath its stony foundations.

What horror would they find?

What Gates of Hell would be revealed?

[A Home.](#embed:https://youtu.be/0erNTlRV7-c?t=1910)

Bright pink flowers decorated the ceiling.

Simple and warm, with a fireplace that brought a gentle heat to the ones that should live in it and lit the cherry blossom walls.

There, the three found a diary.

- Read [Nyavella's Diary](#text:nyavella-diary)

They agreed to not read the diary, to let the person who owned this home have their privacy as they searched for anything suspicious.

Ultimately, it was decided there was nothing here.

It was around this time that Gonathon missed Nyavella's call.

It was perhaps tragic that Gonathon G will never know how Nyavella had truly treasured him in the end.

Her regrets and pain.

And her love.
