Tam was late yet again.

This time, it was even worse. Everyone was essentially stalling, waiting for her to wake up so they could tackle the final challenges together.

[Yeeee hii everyonee](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=b0h8kilUBQk&t=155)

Well, better late than never.

Somehow, Tam already seemed aware of the current situation and objective through [mysterious means](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=b0h8kilUBQk&t=200). The heroes were preparing to undergo the [Rite of Purification,](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=b0h8kilUBQk&t=371) a ritual meant to free Advent from the curse that had driven them apart and placed them on opposing sides. If successful, they would finally stand united once again.

Fortunately, the rite succeeded.

With the curse lifted and Advent reunited, the heroes set off toward their final destination: [Nullspace.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=b0h8kilUBQk&t=2932)

There, they traversed a gauntlet of trials that echoed the many challenges they had overcome throughout their journey. One by one, they pushed forward until they ultimately confronted and defeated [The Order.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=b0h8kilUBQk&t=3978)

At last, it seemed they had reached the end.

But when the heroes finally came face to face with what they believed to be the Outsider, yet another twist awaited them. The journey, it turned out, was not ready to reveal all of its secrets.

And so, this chapter came to a close (see [Faust](#node:faust)).

As for Tam, who knows what adventures await the Azure Queen in the next journey? For now, this one leaves behind plenty of memories—and perhaps none more fitting than her arriving just in time for the finale.
