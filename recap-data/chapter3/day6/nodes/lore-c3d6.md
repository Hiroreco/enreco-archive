#### Main Story

After a relentless chase through the castle, the five cursed heroes are finally purified. Yet one question remains: is the Curse truly gone for good?

The heroes activate the Looking-Glass Shard to enter Nullspace, crossing dimensions into the hidden realm beyond reality.

After overcoming the trials that await them there, they discover that Faust has been waiting for them all along.

Speaking in cryptic riddles, Faust steps aside and allows the heroes to face the guardian of Nullspace's deepest secrets: a towering golem-like behemoth known as **the Order.**

Upon defeating the Order, Faust offers his congratulations before guiding the heroes deeper into the heart of Nullspace.

#### Discoveries

There, he reveals the Egg of Creation, an artifact capable of creating—or recreating—the countless worlds that exist across the myriad realms.

Faust then unveils the truth: **he** is the Outsider. From the very beginning, he has guided the heroes toward this moment so that he may harness their power of infinite potential—the power of Revelation—to awaken the Egg.

He asks whether they will lend him their strength and help him remake all worlds anew. The heroes give their answers, but before the Outsider can respond, a deafening crash echoes through the chamber.

The fallen body of the Order rises once more.

The Curse has survived yet again, possessing the defeated guardian and turning it into its new vessel.

As the Outsider and the Curse prepare to clash, Fia urgently warns the heroes. At that moment, the Looking-Glass Shard activates once more, whisking them away to... somewhere unknown.
