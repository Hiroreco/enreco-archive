Waking up on the final day, NinoIna knew something was going to happen. What was that "something"? Ina didn't know. But she knew she had to be on the lookout for any-

[A sale?](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FRubYpkandA&t=162s)

The day hadn't even started for our hero, and she had already been surprised with the fruits of her labour. Checking her shop, Ina found over _ten thousand_ coins waiting for her to claim from Nerissa, who needed the Lake King for stew. That meant, however, that Ina had to restock, which meant gathering more river princes for the bait.

But as usual, there had to be something to distract her from her fishing, this time being Gonathon, Mordon Ramsay, and later Elizabeth with some rather [troubling](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FRubYpkandA&t=827s) news. Ina was stunned: her own sister, a killer? She would have to contemplate this... Over some fishing, of course!

12 River princes later, Ina saw the sun rising on a new day and headed back to her abode to prepare for more. And just as she was preparing to go to her fishing spot, she heard the familiar tones of... [Her consort](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FRubYpkandA&t=1974s)?

Ina couldn't believe it; The Hot Pink One hadn't been seen since the first day in these lands. She quickly got on her horse and looked, practically turning the kingdom of Libestal upside down, until she spotted her dearest partner in pink.

[Together once more](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FRubYpkandA&t=1974s)

The two of them proclaimed their love (and Ina proclaimed her fishing skills), but unfortunately, their reunion had to be cut short, as a call from Shiori informed Ina of a picnic outside the church. While Ina was hesitant, she trusted Shiori enough to attend, being the first to show up. Lucky too, as it gave her ample time to inform Gonathon of her suspicions of the [ordeal](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FRubYpkandA&t=1974s).

As everyone gathered on the silken flooring prepared for the guests, Shiori began addressing the crowd and revealing her true intention. It was a trial! And not just any trial. It would be the penultimate: the final deciding trial regarding the murder of Shakira that she had only witnessed in passing days prior. The accused? None other than... Mordon Ramsay?

This can't be right, Ina knew the death was at the hands of a cake prepared by Mordon herself, but to accuse her of being the one to _poison_ the cake? Ina couldn't stand for this injustice!

Luckily, it seemed Gonathon, Roa, and Immerkind had been thinking the same thing, springing to action in defense of Mordon. Accusations were thrown, the topic of bail was brought up, which Ina was quick to volunteer to [pay for](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FRubYpkandA&t=2880s); it was utter chaos.

After it all finally came to pass, the Peasant, alongside Tam and the Hot Pink One, addressed the crowd, urging them to meet at the castle. There, they explained that the five troublemakers must be cleansed as they believed _the curse_ still lingered within them. Quickly objecting, it seemed it was up to the heroes to capture them and cure them of their ailments, through the greatest bout the kingdom had seen...

_tag._

Before she knew it, Ina was rushing around the castle walls, the members of Advent dashing away, avoiding the heroes that attempted to [capture](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FRubYpkandA&t=5122s) them. As time went on, things got more and more intense, and the heroes were always just barely dragging behind those they were attempting to subdue.

It took a lot, but after a lengthy battle, the heroes were able to capture the members of Advent, with the Hero NinoIna herself proving her title by managing to gather not just [one](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FRubYpkandA&t=6878s), but two members in the climactic last minutes.

[Gotcha!](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FRubYpkandA&t=7055s)

After cleansing _the curse_ from those affected, the heroes ventured down into Terminus to find their way to the final challenge, _Nullspace_. Inside, the culmination of their abilities was tested. Puzzles, battles, parkour—each and every challenge they had faced thus far had reared its ugly head as a final stand barring their way. But heroes wouldn't ever give up that easily, and so they triumphed.

All that was left, according to Faust, was _The Order_, a beast ensuring the Nullspace was protected at all costs. The battle was fierce, but they were [victorious](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FRubYpkandA&t=8842s).

Now, they could reach Nullspace and find the Outsider... Until Faust had a few words to [say](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FRubYpkandA&t=8895s) (see [Faust](#node:faust)).

Refusing to believe his words, Ina denied the Outsider’s proposal, believing his way was not right. With Fia desperately rushing to get Ina out of Nullspace, the Hero found herself in the white box once [more.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FRubYpkandA&t=9094s)
