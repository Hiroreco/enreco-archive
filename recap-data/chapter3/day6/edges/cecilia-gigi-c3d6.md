This was it, the day of reckoning. With the entire concept of justice hanging in the balance, Immerkind and Gonathon would either be remembered as heroes, or be forgotten as failures for all time.

Immerkind had busied herself for much of the early day with tending to Roa, the amnesiac cat promising to be her ace in the hole against the machinations of Advent. Upon Gonathon's arrival, Immerkind called him immediately, informing him of [the hole](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0erNTlRV7-c&t=973s) she had found near the church and urging him to meet her at the castle.

A brief [distraction](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0erNTlRV7-c&t=1364s) by a fumbling Peasant did nothing to dull the zeal of the knights, and after acquiring some invisibility potions, they readied themselves for their sneaking mission at the church. With most of the adventurers distracted by Shiori's picnic, Immerkind and Gonathon, along with a fugitive Mordan Ramsay, infiltrated Shiori's church. Snooping around the premises, they eventually found a hole in the floor.

But upon descending into what they thought was the depths of the church, they were met with [a bewildering sight](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0erNTlRV7-c&t=1917s): the altar was completely gone! Despite their frenzied attempts to dig through the walls and locate the cursed shrine, there truly seemed to be nothing but a normal bedroom.

[Speak of this to no one.](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0erNTlRV7-c&t=2231s)

Greatly confused and worried they would be discovered, the trio fled the church and returned to the gathering. It was here that Shiori revealed the true purpose of their gathering: she was accusing Mordan Ramsay of the murder of Shakira!

Absolutely certain of their fellow wolf's innocence, Gonathon and Immerkind leapt into action, proclaiming that they would [defend Mordan](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=euqA3GB8Jc0&t=6432) and clear her name. Following a spirited defense, Immerkind brought Roa to the stand. The cat revealed the truth: it was Bijou who had killed Shakira!

While Shiori endeavored to turn suspicion onto Roa's mental fitness, a misunderstanding landed her in the crosshairs of Justice. While the witch made [one final attempt](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=euqA3GB8Jc0&t=7167) to condemn Mordan, she eventually relented, clearing the pink-haired cook's name for good. Celebrating together, Immerkind and Gonathon's joviality was instantly cut short as they were momentarily ripped from the world.

[The curse of Advent!](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0erNTlRV7-c&t=3828s)

Realizing that something had to be done, the duo decided that they would need a new approach. Together with Lady Bloodflame, a new order was founded: [the Knights of Justice](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0erNTlRV7-c&t=4027s)! Gathering together with the other heroes, the knights learned of what had to be done to save Libestal. First and foremost, they would need to cleanse Advent of their curse and bring them back to the side of good. Alas, this would prove to be a far more arduous task than they realized.

[The Knights spring into action](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=euqA3GB8Jc0&t=9008)

After an aggravating set of trials, including a strenuous game of cat and mouse, the heroes cleansed Advent of their curse. But it was here their final challenge appeared: the malignant golem, Order. But the knights had come too far to back down! With a rallying cry of FOR JUSTICE, they charged into battle. With Order laid low, Immerkind and Gonathon were able to [celebrate](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=euqA3GB8Jc0&t=13386) the end of their journey with their companions.

Conversing in the white void, the knights decided to take [a vow of celibacy](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0erNTlRV7-c&t=9658s). This vow proved to be more enticing than they realized, and they soon were able to add Tam and Mordan to their ranks. It was here, though, that Immerkind and Gonathon's journey would end. Through thick and thin, the former knights of Tam Gandr, now the Knights of Justice, had defeated all that had stood against them. While the future remained murky, one thing remained ever apparent: Justice would always prevail!
