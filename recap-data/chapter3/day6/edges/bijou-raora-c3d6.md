Bijou and Roa were friends... right? Roa's memory was fuzzy, but she seemed to remember that much. And it very much helped that when Roa's head began to act up, [Bijou was able to reassure her that all that mattered was that she was a friend](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-v3wKaDqyI4&t=1445).

The two then go out to fight some stains together. It seems some memories still linger in Roa's mind, leading to her [asking some quite existential questions](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mZM0OO3VK9s&t=1025) of Bijou.

After the two split, they meet again after Roa's memory is returned. [Managing to find the invisible Bijou](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mZM0OO3VK9s&t=5090), Roa accuses Bijou of having killed her wife, but Bijou claims no one will ever believe Roa, since she has no evidence now that Bijou no longer has the body.

The issue is brought up again during the trial about Shakira's murder, where Roa once more accuses Bijou, but this time, Shiori takes Bijou's defense. Ultimately, no one is able to prove that Bijou is the one who killed Shakira.

However, tired of the charade, Bijou eventually announces the truth of her killing Shakira before the Rite of Purification. She [taunts Roa](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-v3wKaDqyI4&t=8010), proclaiming that she will only be able to get her wife's body back if she manages to succeed at the trial to come (not Rock Paper Scissors though, through which Roa was trying to take it).

Once the Rite of Purification is properly complete, Bijou apologizes to Roa, promising to atone for her sins by helping to bring Shakira back.
