Roa sought out Immerkind, who remained skeptical of the pink cat's fractured state of mind. Together, they journeyed to the [Terminus](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mZM0OO3VK9s&t=2330), where Roa repeatedly insisted that she didn't remember any of it.

Immerkind was unconvinced.

She suspected Roa was merely pretending to have amnesia, citing the fact that Roa had never once joined her in exploring a Labyrinth.

Their argument was cut short when another agonizing headache struck. Watching Roa writhe in pain, Immerkind began to realize that her condition might not be an act after all.

The pink cat followed the green knight to the "castle gap," where the pair climbed through trees and over walls before reaching the open sea and the mysterious lands [beyond](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mZM0OO3VK9s&t=2820).

There, they boarded a pair of wooden boats and drifted through the waters surrounding Libestal. As they surveyed the landscape, they noticed several strange inconsistencies that seemed impossible to explain. Yet through discussion and observation, they somehow managed to reconcile those anomalies with their own [experiences](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mZM0OO3VK9s&t=2950).

Questions led to answers, answers led to more questions, and eventually Immerkind revealed a devastating truth.

Shakira was Roa's wife.

And she had been murdered.

The revelation was too much.

Overwhelmed by the truth, Roa instinctively bit into the enchanted [potato](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mZM0OO3VK9s&t=3120). Moments later, everything they had discussed vanished from her memory.

Again and again, the cycle repeated.

Roa would learn the truth, suffer unbearable pain, eat the potato, and forget.

Eventually, Immerkind resorted to a small lie in order to break the pattern. It did not restore Roa's memories, but it gave her fractured mind something stable to hold onto—a temporary bandage over a much deeper wound.

The two eventually discovered a more reliable solution.

Whenever the headaches threatened to return [again](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mZM0OO3VK9s&t=3370), Roa could grasp an enigmatic stone. Though it offered no cure, it dulled the pain and prevented her memories from unraveling completely.

For the first time that day, they had found a way to fight back against whatever force was tearing Roa's mind apart.
