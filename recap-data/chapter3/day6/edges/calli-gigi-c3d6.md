Wolf found wolf at the break of dawn. Outside the library, [Mordon crossed paths with Gonathon](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0erNTlRV7-c&t=396s), which, surprisingly enough, led to one of the first real conversations between the two in all of Chapter 3. Both had many things to share.

Wolf and wolf think of Shakira. The missing body of Shakira weighed on both their minds. Mordon shared that she had spotted Sister Shiori with the body, while Gonathon shared that he had been haunted by it just the day prior. Even stranger, both wolves noted similar lapses in memory surrounding the Church. Mordon added that even her pet Shakira (the chicken) had reacted repulsively to the place. Unfortunately, neither wolf spoke chicken, so what Shakira (the chicken) truly said was up for interpretation. Together, both made haste for Shakira's grave to meet with Lady Bloodflame.

[A rap fight for a good knight that is not right](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0erNTlRV7-c&t=537s)

Wolf trusts wolf always. Mid-conversation, Fuwawa entered the trio's fray. When the conversation reached the topic of Bijou's whereabouts, both Gonathon and Mordon quietly removed themselves from earshot. Gonathon shared what he knew—that Advent could not be trusted, Fuwawa included. Cursed as they were, their plans to kill Justice could not go unanswered. Mordon had her own reasons for keeping her distance from Fuwawa. With a timely call from Immerkind, the pack had a plan: [Investigate the Church](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0erNTlRV7-c&t=1883s) and uncover its secrets.

Wolf follows wolf into the unknown. Descending into the Church basement, the trio found not the dark secrets they had braced for, but something far more ordinary: a small bedroom, fitted with twin beds, a diary, and a fireplace. Whatever lay within these holy grounds was keeping itself well hidden. Maybe too well hidden.

[But if she were to break the fireplace...](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0erNTlRV7-c&t=1933s)

Wolf stands with wolf. Outside, however, trouble had found Mordon. A trial was underway at the newly built picnic area just outside the Church, with Mordon being accused of killing Shakira. Despite this, Gonathon and the others in the Wolf Pack stood firmly by her side. Mordon, thankfully, was found not guilty.

[One final AWOOOOOOOOOO](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0erNTlRV7-c&t=9247s)

Wolf and wolf need not romance. Overhearing Gonathon's calls for the formation of the "Knights of Justice" and their [oath of celibacy](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0erNTlRV7-c&t=9520s), Mordon's ears perked up immediately. Romance? Never. She was in. One oath. One pack. Wolf and wolf. Together? Strong.
