**EYES ON ME**

Jealousy—a complex emotion that typically arises when a valued relationship is perceived to be threatened by a rival.

Lady Bloodflame complimented the Fia hat Immerkind was wearing. It was [cute](https://www.youtube.com/live/6HUbJshXMAI).

Nerissa Juliet was not so inclined to believe so. Never before had she sounded so sharp in her tone.

Low tones marked the voice of jealousy as the maiden expected Lady Bloodflame to explain herself, watching her simply explain away that it was a hat.

A hat that the Princess had given her.

["Don't call other people cute."](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/6HUbJshXMAI)

It was a demanding order, accepting no room for any other answer than to say 'yes.'

Falling to one knee, the knight promptly responded with 'okay.'

A command was given, and it was a command to be followed.

---

While still standing outside the castle walls, Nerissa pulled her to the side for a private discussion. Without hesitation, Lady Bloodflame [followed](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6HUbJshXMAI&t=4976).

How could the fair maiden think she was cursed? She was beautiful. She could do no wrong.

The raven-haired woman was a _hero_ for god's sake!

Then the oddest command was provided. Lady Bloodflame was not to fight Nerissa under any circumstances.

Tilting her head, the knight agreed she would not do so. For what reason would she need to harm her to begin with?

---

She had to kneel. Just to make sure she was seeing things correctly in the little corner, under the shelf.

There stood Nerissa Juliet. Small. Fast. Cute.

However, instead of attacking as she should have, the knight turned her back and stood guard.

Giving a small sigh, Nerissa made a request of her.

[Take her out.](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6HUbJshXMAI&t=6678)

Romeo would have to kill Juliet in a cruel twist of irony and fate—to free her from this supposed curse that Lady Bloodflame was sure did not exist.

Raising her sword with a grimace, she struck once.

Twice.

Until the other fell.

Rushing back to the front of the castle to the cage, relief flooded her at the sight of the other trapped inside said cage.

With big eyes and a sad voice, Nerissa asked her to [free her](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6HUbJshXMAI&t=7194). And as much as Lady Bloodflame wished to, there was no way.

Trying to ease the tension, with a lopsided smile, the knight instead asked a question.

"Do you like tall women?"

Smiling back, the Princess responded, though scared.

"I'm seeing you in a whole new light."

**IF THE WORLD WAS ENDING**

> Two souls both alike in dignity,
> In fair Libestal where we lay our scene...

Nerissa Juliet and Lady Bloodflame.

Two souls destined to find one another. Lifetime after lifetime.

If their lives were a game, they had probably played through all the routes imaginable. In one, they were enemies. In another, one was obsessed while the other ran. There were lives where they became friends, and lives where the maiden fell deeply in love with the knight, assuming the version of herself she thought the knight wanted.

Where were they at this point? Honestly, it was still complicated. But one thing was for certain: these two depended on one another, as allies, as friends, as ~~lovers~~ roommates.

And today, as they confronted The Outsider, it was no different.

Nerissa walked a few steps ahead of Elizabeth, when suddenly the knight stopped to grab her hand and called:

["Wait!"](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6HUbJshXMAI&t=7519)

As if declaring her love for the last time before this world came to an end, she handed her a ring and a bouquet of flowers, confessing that Nerissa Juliet was the light to her darkest of days.
