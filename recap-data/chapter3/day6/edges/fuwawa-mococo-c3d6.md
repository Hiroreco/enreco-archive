"...I read it."
"You read it...?"
"And your feelings... [they reached me](https://www.youtube.com/live/5fdrGFsRtQw?t=1095)."

Fuwawa's eyes beamed with light as Mococo spoke to her. She was overjoyed on the inside that they were able to reconcile. But, before they were both ready to move on and continue living as the sisters they always were, she recognized that there were still a couple of issues to be addressed. Most importantly, the fact that Mococo was still worried about Lady Bloodflame and what she was thinking right now.

She couldn't answer that question truthfully. But she encouraged her little sister to go speak to the knight herself, promising she'd be by her side the entire time. She told Mococo that, even though they weren't blood sisters, the time she spent with her still mattered!

["Your memories make you who you are, Moco-chan!"](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/5fdrGFsRtQw?t=1132)

Mococo's emotional defenses gradually lowered as they exchanged bau baus. The warm feeling of doing so was as familiar as could be.

The two carried on with their final day's preparations, and from that point onward, you would never see them separated from each other. Everyone they talked to was in at least a 3-person conversation; every quest they had, they tackled as a duo. They had [their disagreements](https://www.youtube.com/live/5fdrGFsRtQw?t=3766), but what kind of siblings don't? The more time passed, the more comfortable they became with each other, and truthfully, not a lot of time had passed at all.

[Together they are... FUWAMOCO!](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mIsHnxTVoFM&t=2224)

When it came to the final day's big events, they were together as well. They had each other for company during the chaos of Mordon's trial and the chase they had to participate in, and ESPECIALLY for the final push into Nullspace. When Fuwawa fell into lava at some point, [Mococo jumped down in an attempt to save her sister](https://www.youtube.com/live/5fdrGFsRtQw?t=10095) with zero hesitation—even if it resulted in her _also_ getting herself seriously hurt.

They were also in sync when it was time for them to make their final decision, making sure whatever it was that awaited them in the future... it didn't matter to them. As long as there were the two of them, they knew they could push through it!

Or... more accurately, to one fluffy one...

As long as there's... [only](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mIsHnxTVoFM&t=11736s) the two of them.
