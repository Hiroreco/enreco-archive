They say opposites attract. But does that mean they always have perfect synergy?

Fuwawa and Mococo had been at odds for a good while now. Sure, they're members of the same coven; Sure, they may have helped Shiori and the others pull some theatrics off right before they embarked on this next step of their journey; And sure, they LOOK really similar; one could easily mistake them for twins. Even their pets ended up being named the same by coincidence. But did any of that really matter when, to themselves, the other was always so gosh darn INFURIATING to be around?! 

["What's with your wolf...?" "What's with YOUR wolf?!"](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/T5wi-wnloHQ?si=Vjj_UD5AY4HRtO95&t=4328)

To Fuwawa, Mococo was always so obnoxious. She would push people away every time, 'oooh, charms this, monopoly that, nowhere else! 'Urgh! She was thankful when she saw other people stepping up to become jewelers as well, because good riddance. It was impossible to make a good trade with her, because she'd always demand more from others and provide less in return. Plus, she was starting to ignore her directly too! She's just trying to do business, [the NERVE!](https://www.youtube.com/live/T5wi-wnloHQ?si=v_5rlCQFBBMCsWML&t=4030)

To Mococo, Fuwawa was always so stuffy. She would never share the stuff she made without asking for WAYYY too much! These charms are hard to make, y'know!! She always undermined her business, going as far as to dissuade others from getting stuff at her shop for NO reason. She wasn't a jeweler, what gave her the right to speak on this, HUH?! Plus, she had been bothering her with a bunch of calls recently! SHE'S A BUSY DOGGO! Fuwawa, to her, [wasn't worth her time.](https://www.youtube.com/live/teJSRYWdIXc?si=leIE9vhnHdXZL6Bx&t=4020)

This rising tension over the last couple of days culminated right before the dungeon. There was a small dispute about party size while Fuwawa wasn't in the room, and Mococo's immediate thought was to boot her for not being there, no questions asked. When Fuwawa was kicked from Nerissa's group without warning, she obviously had to find out what was wrong. And when she did, she wasn't exactly happy to find out it was Mococo's idea.

[First come, first serve...?](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/T5wi-wnloHQ?si=aTcjwxw_W4WDJSTt&t=4150)

Poor Gonathon was the one to end up getting the short end of the stick, but even afterward, as they focused on the labyrinth at hand, frustrations between the fluffy one and fuzzy one were at an all-time high. Anytime they'd get remotely close to one another, they'd be telling each other to go away, [even when, realistically, they couldn't](https://www.youtube.com/live/teJSRYWdIXc?si=x92AjCn_yfBQcyD-&t=5009). They were both raring to go their own ways again as soon as they were done with their business in Terminus. Unluckily for them, their tasks together were far from over.

> "Do something that proves you are free. Throw away a precious possession. Ignore the rules of polite society. Antagonize a close friend, or antagonize a bitter enemy."

These were Prisoner Zero's words to both of them. They needed to prove to her that they were truly free. And while neither would use the terms "bitter enemy" (...or at least Fuwawa wouldn't), they both knew what they had to do; even if neither of them really wanted to do so, it was worth it for the sake of what they truly cared about.

[Mutual Situation - for the sake of the Woofs.](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/teJSRYWdIXc?si=DZEMGTYJ5_CVzOnf&t=7542)

Though this wouldn't be the last of their begrudging alliance. Prisoner Zero stated to them that the next labyrinth they had to conquer was to be taken on together with a "mirror image" of themselves, and it was clear what that meant they'd have to do tomorrow, but they'd also cross paths a couple more times during this day, too. And their new agreement didn't really change their dynamic. Like, [at all](https://www.youtube.com/live/T5wi-wnloHQ?si=X_yx3CZE0nnc9XTO&t=15098).

And yet, despite all this... there was a weird sense of familiarity hanging around both of them as they headed to rest for the night. Perhaps they were even more linked than they realized... the answer to that was something only time would reveal.
