Initially both Tam and Gonathon planned to only tackle the day's dungeons before retiring. But already there was a complication: Tam had yet to complete the previous day's Labyrinth of Emotion and would need to catch up before the current challenge became available. Ever eager to assist his queen, Gonathon leapt to her aid and assembled a party including himself, Tam, Roa, and Bae.

Almost immediately tensions flared as Roa and Tam began to bicker. Some quick mediation by Gonathon and Bae tempered these hostilities and the party descended into the dungeon. Not wishing to simply solve the entire dungeon for Tam, the other party members did their best to [guide](https://www.youtube.com/live/mss5t4szFko?si=wLsX0PuNnje_E2xi&t=1527) her. It was during this first dungeon that the topic of the members' favorite colors came up. Without thinking Tam said her lucky color was orange, a fact not lost on the loyal knight.

[Blue and orange](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/mss5t4szFko?si=oiMHCNJhKLdbTdgf&t=2176)

After dispatching the boss of the dungeon, the party quickly began to prepare for the next. During this downtime Gonathon filled Tam in on the goings-on of the kingdom the previous day. Realizing Tam's apprehension to being called queen, the knight explained he meant "queen" as a form of [compliment,](https://www.youtube.com/live/mss5t4szFko?si=tTflYzfQBfTm5qXw&t=9971) although Tam did not seem entirely convinced. Despite this, Tam seemed to slowly be coming around, and Gonathon reaffirmed his admiration for her.

Alas, entering the next dungeon would not be easy, as Tam and Gonathon quickly discovered the steep requirements required for the prerequisite quests. While Tam was quick to fly into a [rage](https://www.youtube.com/live/mss5t4szFko?si=rJOeop9uqlQbrOm-&t=4956) and threaten Star Speaker Maaz with bodily harm, Gonathon reassured the queen that if they moved swiftly they could make it to the dungeon.

The first quest proved simple enough, only requiring some haggling with Little Witch Mococo. The second proved much more daunting, as the gluttonous Star Speaker demanded 5 bowls of [high-quality soup](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mss5t4szFko&t=4953s). With their faction coffers drained and their resident chef bedridden, it seemed that their day's journey might end then and there.

[I am going to reach into the screen and...!](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JZ2gO4qDJ2w&t=4950)

In a shocking turn of events, Nerissa and Lady Bloodflame, both members of the rival Rebellion faction, offered their [aid](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mss5t4szFko&t=5695s) in procuring the soup, showing that despite their differences, a bond still existed between supposed enemies. With soup in hand and renewed determination in their hearts, the queen and her knight ventured into the Labyrinth of Song. Here as well no challenge was enough to fell the heroes, and soon enough they emerged victorious once more. Following the revelation of Nerissa's true nature, Tam and Gonathon parted ways momentarily as they went about other business.

Some time later, Gonathon took Tam to see Shiori's church and lay eyes on where Shakira's body had been located. It was here they discovered [a hole](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mss5t4szFko&t=9533s) in the ground, whereupon a descent downwards brought them face to face with an indignant Shiori. After having their memories wiped, the two engaged in a friendly sparring match with Nerissa and Lady Bloodflame, emerging victorious over the vile rebels.

[Victory for the blue team!](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mss5t4szFko&t=9841s)

A short time later, the two were [accosted](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mss5t4szFko&t=10558s) by Bloodflame and Nerissa, the rebels accusing them of peeking on an undressed Shiori. When Gonathon protested he had never seen a naked woman, Lady Bloodflame reacted in shock that he had never seen his "Pearl's pearls." Having the memory of his lost love dredged up drove Gonathon over the edge. Realizing he had lost two of his treasured loved ones to memory loss and determined not to lose a third, he pleaded to Tam with renewed vigor to take off the stain that was devouring her memories.

[A queen's twisted resolve](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mss5t4szFko&t=11183s)

Tam bluntly refused, and even with Nerissa and Lady Bloodflame supporting him, Gonathon once more failed to get through to his queen. At the same time, the sight of the rebels supporting Gonathon snapped Tam out of her stupor. Seeking to distract Gonathon, the queen brought him [back to the church](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mss5t4szFko&t=12111s) to spy on Shiori and Nerissa. Alas, the two were quickly spotted and, following a conversation about Shiori's intentions, the two retreated.

Returning to the kingdom, Gonathon showed Tam the site of his and Immerkind's [planned castle](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mss5t4szFko&t=12530s) for her. Touched by the sentiment, Tam began asking Gonathon questions about his knighthood. If Immerkind served Fia, then Gonathon must also serve some kind of royal too.

[Who do you wish to serve?](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mss5t4szFko&t=13067s)

With a wistful melancholy, Gonathon described his [monarch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mss5t4szFko&t=13083s): a queen whom he served with his entire being. While his queen remained locked in the grips of amnesia, Gonathon was determined to not give up. He would find a way to save his queen!

[Tam's meat is SOOOO good!](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mss5t4szFko&t=9989s)
