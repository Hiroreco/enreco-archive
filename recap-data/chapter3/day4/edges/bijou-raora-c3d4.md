Bijou was still suffering from immense pain, though thankfully she seemed to be recovering little by little. At the very least, Roa could only [hope](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P3uwaftxSG0&t=3030) things were improving.

Wanting to stay informed, Roa asked if there was any important news she should know about, but Bijou admitted she had nothing new to share. Roa instead brought up the grotesque sheep that had begun appearing throughout the town, mentioning the posters she had distributed across the city warning others about them.

Despite the whispers and accusations surrounding Roa, Bijou once again reaffirmed that she believed in her, even if everyone else called her [crazy](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P3uwaftxSG0&t=3520).

The jeweler then spoke about her recent trip into the dungeon alongside Shiori. Hearing this immediately caught Roa's attention. She began to think there might be a way for them to explore the dungeon together next time, allowing her to observe the woman of the cloth more closely from within the shadows of the expedition itself.
