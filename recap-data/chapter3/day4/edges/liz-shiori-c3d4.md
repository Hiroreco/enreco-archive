**Fighting without mercy...against the weak**

After completing Nerissa's dungeon, Lady Bloodflame spoke to Faust, still trying to figure out how to stop the Outsider. Amidst her conversation, Sister Shiori approached her, surprising the knight. More so when requested to complete a duel of all things.

Not as [rivals](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HEWSty8elxo&t=7159), apparently. But as...dog versus cat. Stumbling over her words, she opted to stay by Nerissa's side, the words stuck in her head.

After completing a bit more questing, she found herself also needing the duel, so picking her phone up, she called her opposing force: [Shiori](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HEWSty8elxo&t=8450).

When the other declared she was weak, she fought to explain that it would be a fair fight! She had not sparred in some time, the swords would both be wood, they would have shields. It would be completely even. No Thorn, no revelations — just basic combat.

---

**I'll help- What the!?**

After the battle was accepted, swiftly she received a call from her Princess before inviting her over to watch.

Immediately she was called [codependent](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HEWSty8elxo&t=8695) by Sister Shiori when she explained she was waiting for the other woman. Then the despair set in that the work on the church was being delayed by this action.

The guilt hit Lady Bloodflame like a truck. She was ruining something that needed to be done with the time they had left. After all, it seemed like each time all the dungeons were completed, something happened. It would be horrible to try to put effort into something only for it to be incomplete.

[Sighing](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HEWSty8elxo&t=9028), she resigned herself to help the dual-haired woman with this building task.

Apparently she was helping build the gates to hell. What had she just signed up for?

---

**Happy Tone**

Sometime later, Sister Shiori would give her a call for a request to get items for the church. Exasperated, she agreed to get these campfires. Immediately the other requested she speak in a [happier tone](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HEWSty8elxo&t=10205).

Quickly she fought to defend that it _was_ her happy tone! And then the other woman had the audacity to call her abusive!?

As soon as Nerissa suggested it, Lady Bloodflame hung the call up, right as the barking began.

It was a wonder the relationship between herself and Shiori was strained at best. Not hateful, but boy did Sister Shiori make it difficult to want to talk to her.
