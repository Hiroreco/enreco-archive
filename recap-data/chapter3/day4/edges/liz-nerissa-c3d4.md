**Our unity was so strong**

The moment to conquer yet another dungeon arrived. Nerissa Juliet came to inform her that they'd have to be split into two teams. For a change, Lady Bloodflame decided to team up with lilwitch Mococo this time.

However, the Witch was not convinced in the slightest, and [pressured](https://youtu.be/HEWSty8elxo?t=3813) her into joining her again. Lady Bloodflame tried to reason out, arguing that Gonathon could protect her. But Nerissa insisted on her decision, and so the poor knight yielded.

Could she ever resist her?

---

**Living our lives heroically**

Upon seeing the memory of Nerissa, Lady Bloodflame felt ecstatic. She had been right from the beginning. Lady Juliet was a _[hero](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HEWSty8elxo&t=9964)_! She had saved the people of that town from the horrid monster.

Smiling, she gently patted the fair maiden on her head with a bright smile, all too unaware of the mental struggles this had put on her Princess. Providing her with praise and assuring the other so easily.

With each uneasy response Nerissa gave, Lady Bloodflame pointed out how big her heart is. Even with mistakes, the other had grown. After all, you couldn't learn if you didn't fumble things along the way or at least try.

Lady Bloodflame knew with full confidence, now that everyone had seen the memory, that others would look at the maiden with confidence in her abilities to help save Libestal.

---

**Roses whirling in freedom**

After the team spar with Gonathon, the couple continued on their own.

It was just like the Old Days. Back in the Ancient Libestal where they were free to clash swords against one another in a friendly duel.

_How nostalgic._

Their blades danced in a lively [rondo](https://youtu.be/HEWSty8elxo?t=13751); laughter drowning the loud clashing of steel. Each strike spoke of experience, each parry of trust. Thus, the duel ended with a draw.

The maiden demanded another round, and this time with a wager. The winner shall grant the other their wish. A kiss for a maiden, a Libestal figurine for the knight.

Still, neither could overcome the other.

So, instead of raising their blades for the third time, Lady Bloodflame offered a different solution.

Before Nerissa could respond, the cheeky knight reeled her straight into her arms and [kissed](https://youtu.be/HEWSty8elxo?t=14132) her.

Nerissa let out a startled yelp against her lips, eyes widened as she watched Lady Bloodflame retreating.

---

**If the two of us should be separated**

Instead of clarity, the fragments of memories only cast doubts within Nerissa's heart. Not only about her own identity but also her relationships. Especially, with Lady Bloodflame.

It became more apparent when she talked with Prisoner Zero and dared her to let go of the most precious thing to her.

Of course, the [Jade Knight](https://youtu.be/60_Pduc0ODY?t=18018) was the first to come to mind.

Could she truly allow her the freedom to choose her own path? To loosen her grasp and let Lady Bloodflame decide whether to love or hate her?

Setting her free? The idea alone felt unbearable.

---

**Take my revolution**

After a quick dash to help Shiori with...whatever it was that she was working on, Lady Bloodflame [stared](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HEWSty8elxo&t=9964) up at the tunnel that led to the exit.

Minus one ladder.

Looking over, she watched as Nerissa removed some dirt carefully and told her to step into the spot and summon her magical horse. Confused, she stepped forth into the area and did so, instantly feeling the crush of being crammed into the spot, making it nearly impossible to breathe.

Painfully, the knight declared she was dying. Upon the realization, she looked beyond the head of the horse into those red eyes watching her.

"You want me to die?"

The confirmation of yes was given, even if only for this one moment.

Instantly there was that fog across her head, her eyes glazing over as she accepted to do what she was told. As her knight, she had to die for the maiden. It would protect her if she did so.

---

**Heroically stripping her down until she's bare**

To offer oneself unabashedly to another without cause or reason was improper. Yet without hesitation, Lady Juliet [requested](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HEWSty8elxo&t=14798) such of Lady Bloodflame. A request to see her without clothing.

Calmly, the knight explained to her that Gonathon was a brother in arms. A fellow knight. To see each other nude was normal. Even as the Princess's knight, it was _absolutely_ inappropriate to shed her clothing for the other to see!

More than enough had been exposed in the onsen in the Libestal of old. A memory not forgotten of how close the two had been, skin-to-skin contact as she avoided eye contact awkwardly.

Request: denied.
