As Roa returned to her house, she found Sister Shiori and Mococo [leaving through the door](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P3uwaftxSG0&t=475). The enlightened woman immediately confronted the nun and the jeweler for trespassing.

Mococo kept insisting that it was her house to begin with, but Roa refused to acknowledge the claim, coldly telling her to do something about it if [she really wanted to](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P3uwaftxSG0&t=520).

Shiori attempted to deescalate the situation, though Roa only grew more hostile, lashing out with venomous words. No matter how many ideas or explanations Roa tried to present, Shiori brushed them aside, reducing her secret organization to nothing more than a movie club.

In the middle of the argument, Roa randomly invited the two of them to join the [Illumination](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P3uwaftxSG0&t=595), though the response was far from what she had hoped for.

Shiori's indifference felt like cold ash thrown in Roa's face. She mocked the entire idea, throwing around words like minions and yellow guys. And speaking of minions, that was exactly what Shiori believed Roa's secret organization was trying to create in the first place.
