#### Main Story

**Objective**

Thanks to the Amulet of Levitation, the heroes are finally able to cross the bottomless pit and reach the **Labyrinth of Song** within Cell 1121.

There, they defeat the Prisoner of Song and obtain the Music Box of Memories.

By playing the music box at the entrance to Nullspace, then strengthening its melody with their own voices, the heroes uncover a memory revealing how the Outsider first entered Nullspace: through the use of a shard from something mirror-like.

**Discoveries**

Faust explains that the object in question must be a _Looking-Glass Shard,_ a fragment of a mirror capable of allowing travel between dimensions in a far subtler way than conventional methods.

The shard can supposedly be obtained from the prisoners of Cells 0201-0202, though a massive floor of molten lava blocks the path forward.

To overcome this obstacle, some heroes prove their warrior spirit to Star Speaker Vega and are rewarded with a Fireproof Shield.

Others demonstrate to Prisoner Zero that they are truly free, learning of a hidden shortcut in return.

In addition, the heroes discover that they must find someone who serves as their "mirror image" in order to confront the prisoners together.
