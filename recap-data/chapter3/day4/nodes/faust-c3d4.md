After learning how the Outsider used a mirror fragment to reach Nullspace, the heroes consulted Faust to uncover its origin.

[The Mirror of Worlds](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=60_Pduc0ODY&t=7381)

Faust explained that a legendary mirror once allowed travel between worlds. Though it was shattered, its pieces—the **Looking Glass Shards**—still hold that power. These shards are currently held by the prisoners of **Cells 0201-0202**.

However, reaching them requires overcoming two strict conditions:

**The Mirror Requirement:** The prisoners will only speak to people who are "mirrored." The heroes must form a group where everyone has a counterpart, rival, or opposite.

**The Molten Hallway:** The path to the cells is blocked by a floor of pure fire and magma.

To bypass the deadly heat, the heroes must choose a path forward:

**Talk to Prisoner Zero:** A legendary escape artist who likely knows a secret route around the hallway.

**Find Commander Vega:** The leader of the Star Speakers, who can provide protective defensive gear.

Before they departed, Faust reminded the heroes of an ominous deal he had previously proposed, warning them that the time for their decision was coming soon.
