Something was wrong. Again. And it wasn't about money, for once.

It was another lovely morning in Mococo's house, sure, but what's up with everything here?! The floors are all stained in a weird red thing, and all her doors are open!! Again!! She DID make sure to close them this time!! She found comfort in her little buddy WOOF, who seemed just as iffy on the situation as she was, as she started to clean up and- WHAT is THAT?? A [_sheep??_](https://www.youtube.com/live/teJSRYWdIXc?si=BmdDkZKRZBoxmzGM&t=3830)

The note being left with the sudden statue only added to her unease as she scrambled to get her materials and head out to explore, but what she didn't expect was that the entire STREET was vandalized. Posters everywhere about "the Illumination"... she didn't like this. She had to think about better things! Like... money! MONEY!!! [SHE GOT SALES!!!](https://www.youtube.com/live/teJSRYWdIXc?si=zPD85BN7UQnuajoU&t=598) This was enough to lift her spirits for a little bit, and she united with Nerissa and Sister Shiori to discuss what was going on. The latter suggested they check on Roa's place, as she thinks the catto is the one behind all this.

That's weird. Mococo never gave her a house.

["This should be EMPTY!!! WHY?!"](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/teJSRYWdIXc?si=vePwggB4MwpBLFCN&t=954)

The little witch was NOT happy. Sure, this was her secondary house and not the one she was using, but it was still HER property!! Roa herself was only making this all more confusing; at one moment, she'd be screaming at Sister Shiori, at the other, she was [hyperventilating in panic](https://www.youtube.com/live/teJSRYWdIXc?si=WWhhWE3QW5fvTS9f&t=1265) next to Lady Bloodflame. And apparently, there was something bad Mococo missed yesterday after she went to bed about booms and shaka lakas?? And now Roa wants her and her sister to ["know the truth"](https://www.youtube.com/live/teJSRYWdIXc?si=AN5kMQGWRt3HCC2w&t=1670)??? She had a really bad feeling about this; there was something in the back of her mind telling her not to listen to this at all, and then Roa started accusing her of being possessed?! It ended up being too overwhelming for her, and Mococo fled to the wilds, needing to get away from it all.

...at least there was still the sunset. That was something to look forward to.

[The two certainties in life: sunlight and money](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/teJSRYWdIXc?si=wXVS6O26ZFJUGxbp&t=1750)

With her feelings more in order, Mococo embarked on the daily resource-gathering she had become used to by now. During this time, she discovered a new location, alongside a game called [Color Cannon](https://www.youtube.com/live/teJSRYWdIXc?si=UYIqMaA2LvXiXDcU&t=2063)? Sounded fun! And really familiar... she'd remember this place and come back here later! The little witch also spent a lot of time with [her beloved sister](https://www.youtube.com/live/teJSRYWdIXc?si=y-yFbtarj3g_7Dq8&t=3976), just the way that made them both feel safer (see Elizabeth-Mococo back in day one). After that, it was time to form a dungeon party again! And, yet again, it seemed like Mococo was going to have to split off from her sister. But she wasn't having it. Not today.

[can we kick somebody out](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/teJSRYWdIXc?si=KAa1CpgVghh0SJOE&t=4081)

First, it was Fuwawa. Then, it was Gonathon. Mococo would not back down until she was able to be in a party with Lady Bloodflame, no matter who she had to boot off to do so. After a lot of back and forth, plus a good 15 minutes of random tangents and side tracks, the group that headed off consisted of Mococo, Lady Bloodflame, Nerissa, Sister Shiori, and Fuwawa. The shenanigans began before they even entered the dungeon. It turns out [wolves aren't too used to levitation](https://www.youtube.com/live/teJSRYWdIXc?si=iKlTOEt4OP4dL8Di&t=4700).

Once they actually entered the dungeon, Mococo found herself amazed at the atmosphere of the Labyrinth of Song. Progressing through melodies, hymns, and the [cutest thing you'll see all week](https://www.youtube.com/live/teJSRYWdIXc?si=YlkRVtFpLIAGQKXI&t=6298), they'd recover the memories of the Demon of Sound, [Nerissa Ravencroft](https://www.youtube.com/live/teJSRYWdIXc?si=0xRmpr6L_yx3pJg6&t=6666).

With the day's adventure complete, Mococo decided to speak to Prisoner Zero after seeing Faust-- and she was finally put into the uncomfortable dilemma she'd been avoiding for the past couple of days (see [Fuwawa-Mococo](#edge:fuwawa-mococo)).

What a nightmare... it was time to cool off by getting more and more materials! The most eventful things that happened in the coming hours were a run-in with a shopkeeper with somehow even more [questionable business tactics](https://www.youtube.com/live/teJSRYWdIXc?si=UEQXlWKcO3tyUgwS&t=13034) than her own, as well as learning from her sister how to actually feed her best friend WOOF. So THAT'S why he was crying... oops.

But this calm now was okay to her! It didn't matter how scared she was earlier. She knew she'd make it through. She had her sister, her pet, and of course... SHE WAS STILL THE BEST JEWELER IN ALL OF LIBESTAL!! BAU BAUUUUU!!!!
