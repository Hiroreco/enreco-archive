After a troublesome night, the morning arrived with Roa sitting alone by the [pier](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P3uwaftxSG0&t=300).

For a brief moment, she allowed herself to breathe. The warm glow of the sunrise reflected gently across the ocean, waves softly brushing against the wooden docks as the endless sea stretched toward the horizon. A rare moment of calm.

Then, without hesitation, Roa leapt into the water once again.

As the day began, she emerged fresh from death.

Not long after, Roa noticed certain people sticking their noses where they did not belong. Sister Shiori and Mococo were casually walking out of her house as though nothing was wrong.

[How dare you steal my house!](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/P3uwaftxSG0?t=537)

The founder of the Illumination stood her ground, daring the supposed owner, Mococo, to take the house back by force if she truly believed it belonged to her. With her black sheep wandering beside her, Sister Shiori exchanged insults and verbal abuse with Roa as tensions rapidly escalated. (See [Roa-Shiori](#edge:raora-shiori))

In return, Roa casually suggested they join the Illumination instead, hoping to save them from their blindness.

But then she noticed the blood.

A trail of crimson stains led directly inside her safe haven, whose walls were now covered corner to corner with black posters bearing the symbol of the Illumination.

The poster depicted a sealed emblem etched into a square: an unblinking eye suspended above a gap of light. Rays stretched outward like forbidden thoughts leaking through solid walls, while a staircase ascended toward a distant quadrupedal figure standing at the horizon.

[This is the Illumination.](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/P3uwaftxSG0?t=647)

Following the blood trail deeper into the basement, Roa descended carefully into the darkness. Before she could reach the bottom, however, she encountered a geometry of horror.

A grotesque construct of pale cuboids stood before her, its edges unnaturally sharp, too precise to belong in this reality. Its silhouette vaguely resembled some kind of beast, yet remained impossible to properly comprehend. A creature of pure [ambiguity](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P3uwaftxSG0&t=660).

Terrified, Roa screamed and fled into the streets.

Lady Bloodflame soon arrived to help deal with the situation. (See [Liz-Roa](#edge:liz-raora))

Mococo attempted to sneak in an offer to purchase the house for a handful of coins, but the red knight immediately shut the deal down after revealing that similar monstrosities had begun appearing throughout Libestal itself.

With countless issues spreading across the kingdom, Roa dedicated herself to destroying every grotesque statue she could find, sealing the remains inside boxes and burying them deep underground. Along the way, she also assisted Nix with several puzzles.

But the horrors were far from over.

After some time, an ominous figure suddenly revealed itself before Roa. A drifting [entity](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P3uwaftxSG0&t=2710) appeared from nowhere, floating through the air as though carried by laws of physics that did not belong to this reality.

For a single moment, Roa froze.

Her voice caught in her throat while her legs instinctively backed away from danger.

Then she saw it clearly.

Why had the universe shown her such a cruel image of her beloved?

The beautiful memory she held of Shakira had been twisted into something profane. Her limbs had collapsed inward into her ribcage to fit a doll-sized frame. Her arms bent unnaturally through broken joints. Her legs compressed vertically beneath shattered ligaments. Organs bulged and pulsed grotesquely through an exposed chest cavity.

Roa ran.

She sprinted desperately until she reached the altar holding her wife's sword and shield, pleading to her departed love to reveal the truth in a kinder way. She was ready to listen now.

Even through the terror and uncertainty, Roa still held firmly onto the same goal.

She wanted to [make Shakira proud](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P3uwaftxSG0&t=2890).

Later, after finally regaining some composure, Roa encountered Gonathon and Nerissa along the road. There, the corpse of her wife appeared once more, attempting to communicate again.

This time, Roa steadied herself and listened carefully to the ghastly voice emerging from the rotting flesh.

[REVENGE.](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P3uwaftxSG0&t=3775)

Again and again, the same word repeated endlessly.

Revenge.

And Roa promised that she would uncover the truth needed to exact it.

Despite everything, the day continued. Roa, Tam, and Gonathon eventually conquered both the Labyrinth of [Emotion](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P3uwaftxSG0&t=6930) and the Labyrinth of [Song](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P3uwaftxSG0&t=12080). Peasant had joined them for the first dungeon, though she later decided to do the second alone.

Earlier tensions between Roa and Tam had caused some [conflict](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P3uwaftxSG0&t=4390), but the two ultimately put aside their differences in order to finish the dungeons together.

Later on, Tam attempted to visit Roa's house to collect the present Roa had prepared for her. Roa welcomed her warmly, only for Tam to suddenly [walk away at the last second](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P3uwaftxSG0&t=12280).

Roa later spotted Nerissa wandering through the streets and immediately approached the blue witch.

She explained everything to her in full detail—the truth behind the Gap, the strange revelations she had witnessed, and the existence of the massive glass tower she called the _"Fourth Wall."_

To Roa's surprise, Nerissa accepted the story wholeheartedly. Rather than mocking her like the others had, the demon singer listened with genuine interest and even offered to accompany Roa the next time she ventured into the Gap.

[Next time](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P3uwaftxSG0&t=12845)

With the possibility of a grand expedition into the Gap awaiting tomorrow, Roa raised her voice proudly for all to hear:

[VIVA LA RAOVOLUTION!](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P3uwaftxSG0&t=12885)
