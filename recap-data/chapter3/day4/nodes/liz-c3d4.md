I want you, Gonathan, to keep [Justice](https://www.youtube.com/live/rg_rZ8reBVA?si=SiiDaSZ1ONq3Bc33&t=13227) in your heart and continue doing good. Even if I fall...

Lady Bloodflame awakens from her slumber to a confusing sight within her home. [Blood](https://www.youtube.com/live/HEWSty8elxo?si=LB_6Da_k7xFZN1LZ&t=631) is scattered across the floor. Panicked, she investigates and discovers a giant sheep statue standing just outside her house. Beneath the strange sheep lies an ominous [message](https://www.youtube.com/live/HEWSty8elxo?si=cDSFmHi2UDhHXw9a&t=670).

While contemplating what to do with the sheep, Lady Bloodflame [finds](https://www.youtube.com/live/HEWSty8elxo?si=2552X2R5Jk6rK56W&t=743) herself wandering toward the water and staring at her reflection. Both of her eyes have turned blue.

She laughs it off, telling herself that it is simply the way things are now. Despite the change, she still believes she is the same Lady Bloodflame as before.

Yet, when she tries to recall her master, she can barely remember his name.

While Lady Bloodflame is lost in thought, Nerissa [arrives](https://www.youtube.com/live/HEWSty8elxo?si=lr3U-bB1dx7MYzY8&t=824), and they discuss the possibility that Roa is behind the incident due to her current fixation on sheep.

Strange [Illumination posters](https://www.youtube.com/live/HEWSty8elxo?si=i7WV4VsJBStuCdX7&t=937) have also appeared throughout various places, but the situation is far worse at Roa’s house, where the walls are completely covered in them. The sight causes Roa to scream and run away. She [requested](https://www.youtube.com/live/HEWSty8elxo?si=LtANOgHvawEJIRnj&t=1028) for Lady Bloodflame to investigate further, and there Lady Bloodflame found under the house another giant sheep and another strange message which was also found in Mococo's house (see [Lady Bloodflame-Roa](#edge:liz-raora)).

After conquering the Labyrinth of Song, Lady Bloodflame [learns](https://www.youtube.com/live/HEWSty8elxo?si=p3S98XoHA9vFzS7h&t=6399) that Nerissa was never evil and is, in fact, a hero. Before she can speak with Nerissa, Nyavella approaches her sheepishly and [invites](https://www.youtube.com/live/HEWSty8elxo?si=iyKJMevfpAAr_QBj&t=6896) her to be her counterpart for the next dungeon. Lady Bloodflame accepts Nyavella’s request.

After her talk with Nyavella, Lady Bloodflame [checks](https://www.youtube.com/live/HEWSty8elxo?si=iyKJMevfpAAr_QBj&t=6896) on Nerissa, and they discuss the memory they had just seen and who would be Nerissa’s counterpart, since Lady Bloodflame already has Nyavella. Nerissa mentions Gonathan and their spar, which surprises Lady Bloodflame and leaves her disappointed that she wasn’t there.

Nerissa then invites her to spar, and they agree to do it after completing their quests.

As if the timing were right, after recovering her memories, Lady Bloodflame and Nerissa, for the first time, do not [choose](https://www.youtube.com/live/HEWSty8elxo?si=iyKJMevfpAAr_QBj&t=6896) the same path for Faust’s task. A sign that things are changing once again?

On her way to speak with Star Speaker Vega, Lady Bloodflame [meets](https://www.youtube.com/live/HEWSty8elxo?si=VMJklk7j0o-0U9fp&t=7673) Gonathan, who notices the change in her eyes. Lady Bloodflame continues to shrug it off, even though she is the only one affected, which worries Gonathan.

Lady Bloodflame reassures him that it is nothing to worry about.

During their conversation, Lady Bloodflame [encounters](https://www.youtube.com/live/HEWSty8elxo?si=kZnPw7rprhCXA9zx&t=7838) Tam, who appears to be acting strangely, especially with a Stain on her head. Tam is visibly erratic, even raging at Maaz. Gonathan mentions that she had wiped her memories and has not been the same since.

The two decide to work together to find a way to remove the Stain from her head. After Gonathan leaves, Lady Bloodflame is left alone and [witnesses](https://www.youtube.com/live/HEWSty8elxo?si=S6LFkAMW8tE9J12W&t=8065) Tam completely losing control, leaving her filled with fear.

In need of a sparring partner for Vega’s request, Lady Bloodflame first tries to [call](https://www.youtube.com/live/HEWSty8elxo?si=OBrmXNb4JkuuPm14&t=8424) Nerissa, but there is no response. She ends up asking Nyavella instead, who accepts.

Nerissa still joins them to witness the spar, and in the end, Nyavella wins.

After completing Vega’s task, Lady Bloodflame heads toward the [church](https://www.youtube.com/live/HEWSty8elxo?si=nwVvPsKnQB2t26h2&t=9381) but gets distracted when she sees Nerissa, Gonathan, and Tam fishing at the fountain for their task.

A series of fishing-rod shenanigans follows, ultimately ending with Lady Bloodflame giving Nerissa a [kiss](https://www.youtube.com/live/HEWSty8elxo?si=rRzJsPaZcEdXqpHI&t=9599).

Shortly after, Nerissa invites her to spar at the arena, where they have two draws and one round won by Nerissa. The final round ends in a draw, with their bets being a kiss and a figurine box. They agree it still counts as a win, so Lady Bloodflame kisses Nerissa, and Nerissa promises to give her a gift.

After their spar, things take a drastic turn when Lady Bloodflame is [informed](https://www.youtube.com/live/HEWSty8elxo?si=ZHO-WJCMI6LnoY4-&t=14265) that Gonathan and Tam were caught peeping at Nyavella while she was naked. This leads her to scold Gonathan, and painful memories are unearthed in the process.

It takes Lady Bloodflame, Nerissa, and Tam to comfort a crying Gonathan, who claims the only way to calm him down is for Tam to remove her hat. After more chaotic conversation, Lady Bloodflame decides to return to her tasks instead, but is suddenly taken by the ping ghost...

So... does this mean Lady Bloodflame may be freed from whatever has been binding her? Will the changes happening to her continue?

One can only hope she remains true to herself...
