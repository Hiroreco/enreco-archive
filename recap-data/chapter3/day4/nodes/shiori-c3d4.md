It was going to be a peaceful day in Libestal, thought Sister Shiori, until she found the [blood](https://youtu.be/o-aseqW0iiU?t=335) on her path. Red and dried, almost like dust, it led to multiple houses, staining each door, and the Illumination posters that hung nearby. 

Sister Shiori, now joined by her coven, entered Gonathan's house only to be greeted by dark, beady eyes. A sheep stood in the middle of the room, a chest by its feet.

A chest held a [mysterious poem.](#embed:https://youtu.be/o-aseqW0iiU?t=368)

An occurrence at several other homes.

Cryptic. It could have only been done by a madwoman. Touched by an otherworldly force and having seen things no mortal should have ever seen. Veiled by an innocent air that belies insanity. 

[It could only be Roa](#embed:https://youtu.be/o-aseqW0iiU?t=713)

The evidence was there. The trail of blood led to a large sheep under her basement. The group saw the grief-stricken widow, shouting nonsense and a 'truth.'

Roa claimed ignorance of the blood and the flock of sheep.

She did admit responsibility for the Illumination posters.

The madwoman left, screaming "Viva la revolución" as Sister Shiori and the rest watched.

Well, Sister Shiori has much to do, namely a secret project known only to herself. Underneath the Church of Inkwell, the Sister dug and toiled. So much so that each dirt started to look like poop. There was much to do, since [the gods denied](https://youtu.be/o-aseqW0iiU?t=3371) the kind and benevolent Sister Shiori's request. Sister Shiori was nothing if not resourceful. Though a bucket of lava would be nice.

Nerissa Juliet came by the Church, looking for Sister Shiori. Amused, hearing the Maven's voice above her, Sister Shiori enlisted her help with her project but kept the secret untold.

Shortly after, the two would join Lady Bloodflame, Mococo, and Fuwawa for the Labyrinth of Song. It would be a musical-themed parkour theme park, [Sister Shiori said](https://youtu.be/o-aseqW0iiU?t=4544), and it was so.

They danced as they jumped from platform to platform with the music stringing along with them as Sister Shiori ragebaited Lady Bloodflame in tune with the beat. 

'Twas truly a trial of balance, musical knowledge, and, of course, memory.

With the power of the Coven and Lady Bloodflame, the boss fell, and another memory was recovered.

["Wow, that was a great AU,"](https://youtu.be/o-aseqW0iiU?t=6574) Sister Shiori exclaimed upon seeing Nerissa Juliet the Hero save a village.

And a final bit of song to truly complete the challenge:

[da da ra dan dara da ra doo...](#embed:https://youtu.be/o-aseqW0iiU?t=6670)

What memories did Sister Shiori associate with this song? We may never know.

To further prepare for the future, Shiori approached Prisoner Zero's cell for help. But it seems the prisoner was more interested in the philosophy of freedom. 

To truly prove her freedom, [Sister Shiori antagonized Lady Bloodflame](https://youtu.be/o-aseqW0iiU?t=7192) once again.

With one storyline out of the way, Sister Shiori headed back to her church to finish the setup for her own plot, Nerissa Juliet joining her soon after. Sister Shiori noticed Nerissa Juliet's bemusement over her memory and inquired about her well-being. (See: Shiori-Nerissa c3d4)

As the two talked, Shiori finally revealed the secret:

[It was a gate to another world](#embed:https://youtu.be/o-aseqW0iiU?t=8380)

Shiori received a call from Lady Bloodflame. The Crimson Knight beckoned the Sister to the Arena for a spar. Sister Shiri obliged, calling out the knight for choosing a weak opponent. The battle ensued, and as the Sister predicted, Lady Bloodflame won handily. Though, as a recompense, the knight paid the Sister with gold and agreed to help supply her project, the nature of it, the knight was doubtful about its actual nature.

And the actual nature of it? Sister Shiori unveiled it to Nerissa Juliet:

[To summon the King of Hell.](#embed:https://youtu.be/o-aseqW0iiU?t=12398)

It will need the rest of the Coven to achieve such a thing. And no, it's not Nerissa Juliet's father.

They decided to use Nerissa Juliet's newly found membership with Illumination to gather information and further encourage her madness. To bring Roa closer to the plot [and the gates of hell…](https://youtu.be/o-aseqW0iiU?t=13292) As Nerissa Juliet turned to leave, it seemed there were a few who were rather disrespectful of a sister's private place, [dropping into her hole](https://youtu.be/o-aseqW0iiU?t=13306) without even a courtesy call. Nerissa Juliet's voice forced them to disappear, taking their memories as well.

However, with their memories erased, they wandered back to the Church again.

Irked and wanting the Gate to be kept secret until the promised day, Shiori called for Nerissa Juliet's help, and what happened can only be described as a pathetic madness.
