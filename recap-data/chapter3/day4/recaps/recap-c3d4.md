## Day 4: From Chords to Naked Women

It was supposed to be a calm day. Compared to the previous three days, far fewer people were around, and for a while, it _did_ seem like things would stay peaceful. Aside from Roa continuing to spiral over the reality of the world—made even worse now that strange sheep structures were mysteriously spawning inside people's homes and throughout the kingdom—most people began the day with a single shared goal: finishing the day's dungeon.

And they truly committed themselves to it, very diligently in fact.

The **Labyrinth of Song** fully embraced its musical theme, introducing unique puzzles and challenges centered around melodies and sound. Every button pressed rang out with a different note, while each completed section rewarded challengers with another soft lullaby, almost as if the dungeon itself was trying to soothe them while they slowly lost their minds venturing through. In the end, their efforts allowed them to finally recover the memories of Nerissa Ravencroft.

![Labirynth of Song](/images-opt/labyrinth-of-song-opt.webp)

But as the day went on, it became increasingly clear that while one side of the kingdom still maintained that initial calm atmosphere, the other was descending deeper and deeper into complete absurdity.

Lady Bloodflame and Nerissa Juliet's relationship somehow continued growing even more complicated by the hour, while elsewhere chaos erupted as the queen (or not-queen) and knight duo became tangled in a whirlwind of accusations, arguments, denials, and increasingly confused explanations over claims that they had been peeping on a woman changing clothes.

Meanwhile, the very same woman they were accused of spying on was apparently in the middle of constructing some sort of secret demonic ritual underground.

Even the people directly involved could barely comprehend what was actually happening anymore.

![Caught in 4k](/images-opt/kronii-caught-opt.webp)

Still, with only two days left before the end, who could really blame these dorks for whatever shenanigans they managed to stumble into next?
