**Knights Log 0001**

My name is Elizabeth Bloodflame. A Jade Sword Knight. Trained by Viridis. L̴̞̬̾̽ẽ̶̪a̵̩̼̒-̴̛̝̯̊ Knight of Honor. I know it's odd to write—but I do not wish to keep on forgetting or at least I feel this is best.

We are in new lands. New past lands. A strange journey through time. It seems we need to prepare again, ordered by a King and now a Princess. 

Seems some hearts have forgotten bonds, even small. Seeing loyalty disappear in front of my eyes is strange, literally swallowed by time.

What is Fia? 
Why have most forgotten?

My little sister Mococo has forgotten. Part of myself feels it's a good thing as it means she has forgotten any h̷u̷r̶t̴, or s̸a̶d̴n̶e̷s̴s̴, or n̷e̴g̷a̵t̸i̸v̷i̵t̴y̵—but also I feel greedy in ways still wanting to be her sister.

I was quick to remind her of what we were—what ~~is~~ was—but her eyes did not know mine. I guess some threads are too sacred to leave frayed.

---

In the previous land we ventured I was called "Romeo". I dismissed it, of course—believing the weight of duty left no room for such nicknames.
Now I wonder. Perhaps moments stolen so suddenly like sword strokes I begin to understand it was something to cherish, even a little bit. I guess I've learnt to appreciate the little things ~~and~~ as  sometimes the little things can vanish.

My comrades—I will call them that even if they are lost—remain by my side. I'm happy at least the Mighty Phoenix remembers. I feel there is a warmth between us shared. A fire which burns, literally. The "Shining Stars" is what we were referred to as. It must mean something. I'm guessing the shine is our revalation? (I mean that would make sense.) I hope throughout this journey I learn truly what they mean by Shining Stars.

Until more answers come, I have Thorn by my side and my vow as a Knight.

For memories untold.

For what was forgotten.

For honor!
