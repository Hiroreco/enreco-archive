**Knights Log 0001**

My name is Elizabeth Bloodflame. A Jade Sword Knight. Trained by Viridis. ~~Lea~~ Knight of Honor. I know it's odd to write - but I do not wish to keep on forgetting or at least I feel this is best.

We are in new lands. New past lands. A strange journey through time. It seems we need to prepare again, ordered by a King and now by a Princess. 

Seems some hearts have forgotten bonds, even small. Seeing loyalty disappear in front of my eyes is strange, literally swallowed by time.

What is Fia? Why have most forgotten?...

My little sister Mococo has forgotten. Part of myself feels it's a good thing as it means she has forgotten any hurt [She  says "hurt, or sadness, or negativity" but the journal in the background only says "hurt"] - but also I feel greedy in ways still wanting to be her sister.

I was quick to remind her of what we were - what is [She says "what is– was", but the book just says "what is"] - but her eyes did not know mine. I guess some threads are too sacred to leave frayed.
