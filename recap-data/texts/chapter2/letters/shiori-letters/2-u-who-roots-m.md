To Dearest Gone of Thons,

I bring thee gift.
The moment I saw its vibrant colors, it reminded me of how passionate your soul is.

You spread kindness and good to all around.

My vegetable beets for you when you are near. I am unsure why, but I present it to thee.

- From a humble witch living under a roofless shack.
