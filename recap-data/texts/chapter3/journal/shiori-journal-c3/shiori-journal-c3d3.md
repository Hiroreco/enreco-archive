**Day 3**

Today was a splendid day where things actually progressed.

The church has been finished.

It looked majestic in the light, and the funeral prep was made.

The chaos was expected after that.

What's amusing to me is that no one expected this.

Everyone (except my favorite gem) was flabbergasted.

They looked down on my methods, yet no one has the courage nor the pixelated balls to do anything about Shakira's memory.

They whine. They mourn. They talk about how she was an amazing person.

But when someone dies, the grief is so clearly temporary for most.

What exactly are they doing to honor her death?

They simply forget the way she died?

That she ate a suspicious cake, choked, then drowned?

And no one even made the effort to host a funeral.

No one made any events surrounding this except a eulogy, crocodile tears, and just 'remembering the good times'.

Shakira deserved better than just a passing thought.
