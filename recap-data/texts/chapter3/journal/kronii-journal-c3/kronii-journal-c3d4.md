**Day 4**

Star Speaker Maaz... that sick old woman... corrupted by greed... extortion... how corrupt the Star Speakers must be to let someone in like that and let it slide, turning a blind eye to such crime...

Are the Star Speakers truly benevolent?

It's getting difficult to define good and evil. Vile actions are seen as shortcuts and honesty goes unrewarded. If that is the case, then what is the point being on the side of good?

Nerissa Juliet is suspicious and plants the seeds of doubt onto others, but she has also helped Gonathon and I with our tasks. An evil person can make kind gestures and a supposedly heroic individual, like Lady Bloodflame, can commit heinous crimes.

If this is the case, those boundaries are so difficult to navigate through...

I don't know if being on the good side is beneficial... I have no money and I always feel like I need to work harder than others. It's getting harder to resist everyday.

On a side note, everyone seems to want my pet. Why is that?

It fills me with joy, helps me see certain things that I normally wouldn't be able to see, and my worries never last too long.
