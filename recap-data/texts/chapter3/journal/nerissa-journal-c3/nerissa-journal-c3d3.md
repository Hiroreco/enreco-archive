**Day 3 Journal:**

Today was filled with many satisfactions. I had the pleasure of having Lady Bloodflame follow me into the dungeon. Strange, tomorrow's dungeon feels... familiar somehow. With the way it's being described. It seems Lady Bloodflame is incredibly malleable. I plan to continue to use her for our mutual advantage – clearly she is unwell and not in her right mind, so she will continue to require my gentle guidance and opinion. I hope she will do the right thing and continue to hold it in highest regard.

I have also advanced my cooking career! I am now able to make milkshakes, among other things. I have been requested to make more pet food, but I have no cod, so it is currently off the table. I have also regained my memories of Bijou. I really did consider her a princess, but it seems like she is some sort of gem girl. A gem girl able to control the emotions of those around her – but curious, I feel no such effect. Mayhaps it is because I am a witch? Has becoming so caused me to abandon my humanity? Or maybe I was never human to begin with.

Signing off,
Nerissa Juliet
