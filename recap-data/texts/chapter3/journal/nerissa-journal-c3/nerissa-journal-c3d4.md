**Day 4 Journal:**

Today was filled to the brim with surprises and confusion. The dungeon today felt familiar, and I was awarded back my memories upon completion. However, they weren't what I was expecting. In the past, I had let others tell me who I was, based on their memories. But now that I have the full picture back, I feel as though I do not recognize myself. I thought I was a normal girl, only to find out I was a witch, only to find out that, in actuality, I was neither. I was a demon. And... somehow, it seems I was also a hero? But there are memories that returned that were not seen by all. Memories of the cell, as Shiori had described it. Memories of a punishment by the gods, for actions of my own. So though they call me a "hero", I think maybe what we saw was, in a way, my repentance. For something I knew I would do eventually. Afterall, as a demon, it is in my nature to destroy. I was asked to give up something, to prove that I was free, and in that moment, I thought allowing Lady Bloodflame to be free of our pact would be appropriate. However, as the moment came, I could not... Hm.

Signed,
Nerissa "Juliet" Ravencroft
