Dear Diary,

I realise today again that all I need to is to stay positive and hopeful.

I should not listen to the quiet words in my non-beating heart. To the doubts that are creeping in while I slowly but surely keep goin.

How do we know which side to fight for. How do we know what Ipanina truly wants. And how do we know how to fix the mistakes we make.

I am still learning all of this. But I hope the Princess, my friends and the voices in my head keep staying with me until I have figured it all out. Until I have found true meaning to this existence.

Yours truly
Knight Immerkind
