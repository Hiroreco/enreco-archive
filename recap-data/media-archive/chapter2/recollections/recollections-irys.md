### Description

IRyS’ memories were recovered after the conquest of the Star Site: Elpis, which took place on the second day of the second chapter.

A Nephilim born from the war between Angels and Demons, her purpose was to bring an end to their conflict. With her beautiful voice and mesmerizing songs, she united both factions—a being many came to call “a goddess,” albeit an alcoholic one.
