### Description

Shown on May 3, 2025, the day before the start of the second chapter, this stream, featuring Kronii, Bijou, Nerissa, Ina and Calli, was to introduce and announce official info and what to expect of the second chapter.

The stream held many fun activities, such as guessing songs, dancing, and answering questions as a balloon gets larger and larger, so large it traumatized Bijou and Calli.
