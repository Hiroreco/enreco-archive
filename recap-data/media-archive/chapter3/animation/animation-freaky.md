### Description

Released on May 24, 2026, _Freaky Deaky Love_ is the official theme song for the third chapter of ENigmatic Recollection. It is performed by Takanashi Kiara, Hakos Baelz, Koseki Bijou, and Raora Panthera.

Stepping away from the heavy, dark tone of Chapter 2's theme, this track blends an upbeat, danceable tech-house rhythm with jazzy brass melodies, creating a catchy yet deeply ironic tonal contrast. Lyrically, the song tackles the chapter's overarching themes of **betrayal, separation, and fractured relationships**—masking the bitter pain of "broken bonds" behind an energetic, deceptively playful facade.
