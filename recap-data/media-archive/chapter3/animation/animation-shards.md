### Description

This loading screen was used by most of the talents as they prepared to begin each day of Chapter 3. It features a looping animation of shattered shards drifting downward against a red-and-black background, with each fragment reflecting the profile of a different member.

The background music is the instrumental version of **Freaky Deaky Love**, the official theme song of Chapter 3.
