### General

Originally serving as the demon dogs tasked with guarding the gates of hell, the duo spent an eternity keeping the dead in and the living out. Despite the monotony of their post, they remained dedicated to their duty until they encountered a lost puppy who didn't belong in the underworld.

Deciding to escort the lost soul back to the mortal realm, the sisters embarked on a journey that led them away from their grim post. After navigating through trial and error, and learning to rely on one another, they finally stepped out of hell and into a world filled with anime, manga, and games. Embracing this new life, they decided never to turn back, choosing to pursue their passion for entertainment and visual novels together.
