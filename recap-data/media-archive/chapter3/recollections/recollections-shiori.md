### Description

As someone who possesses the unique ability to turn stories that resonate with her into bookmarks, Shiori previously lived by blurring the lines between fiction and reality through her collection. After losing her shelves and her memories of those gathered tales, as she was captured for possesing "forbidden knowledge", she initially felt hollow and trapped.

However, in her darkest moment, she realized that she was not truly empty. She understood that the stories she once collected were not external objects, but rather foundational parts of her own identity that had been woven into who she is. Ultimately, Shiori finds clarity in the knowledge that she carries those experiences within her, regardless of whether she can explicitly recall them, allowing her to escape her imprisonment with glee.
