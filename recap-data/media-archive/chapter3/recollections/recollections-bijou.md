### General

In the past, her radiant power was a burden, as her beauty mesmerized those around her and stirred intense, often negative emotions that sparked wars and civil strife. For ages, civilizations rose and fell while people desperately sought to possess her, leading her to feel exhausted and jaded by her own nature.

Ultimately, Bijou decided to move past the weight of those historical conflicts. By shifting her focus away from her origins as a source of discord, she now embraces a more lighthearted daily life, finding joy in simple pleasures like tea with rock friends, reciting poetry, and resting under the moon, choosing to define herself by her own terms rather than the desires of others.
