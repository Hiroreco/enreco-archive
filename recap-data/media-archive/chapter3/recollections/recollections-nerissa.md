### Description

Guided not by maps, but by the melodies of beautiful souls, Nerissa views music as a "universal magic" that serves as a bridge between herself and others. Throughout her journey, she has moved past internal conflicts and doubts about her own nature—symbolized by her unique appearance—to embrace a life dedicated to harmony and peace. Even going as far as to put herself upfront to protect others.

Ultimately, Nerissa has chosen to define her existence through this passion, crafting her own "ode to humanity" as a way to connect, sustain her spirit, and ensure the safety of those around her.
