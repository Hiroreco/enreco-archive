On this day... truth be told, Jyon didn’t really know what she wanted to do. All around her, things were rising at a ridiculous pace—one person putting up a shop, another building a gallows and immediately asking her to "test" it. With the world shifting so quickly around her, maybe today wasn’t meant for planning at all. Maybe today, she’d just drift with the current and see where it carried her.

[Ah yes, a gallows. Let me test it](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/aP7OWMQ18ao?t=911)

And drift she did, all the way to the [Eldritch Horror Dungeon,](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aP7OWMQ18ao&t=3087s) alongside two other members of the Scarlet Wand. Jyon hadn’t been there the day before, so she had some catching up to do before stepping into the newly opened depths.

The moment that conquest was over, she was already moving on to the next. This one, though, had a different flavor—interesting, yes, but more than anything, mischievous. Because Jyon, our dear blonde, seemed particularly intent on stirring trouble both in word and in action with the two accompanying her: Gonathon, her husband, and Gumbo Jumbo (if that’s what she’s called).

Why mischievous, you ask? Well, Jyon seized every chance she got to mess with her husband. She would casually toss out [lines](https://www.youtube.com/live/aP7OWMQ18ao?t=1082) like "he doesn’t let me speak unless permitted" in front of everyone, watching as panic crept in almost instantly. And if that wasn’t enough, she went a step further: declaring a sort of "date" with Gumbo Jumbo to the dungeon, leaving the poor man sweating buckets and abandoning everything just to chase after them and "monitor" the situation.

It really does make you wonder if Jyon actually liked being with this guy or not (see [Gonathon-Gura](#edge:gura-gigi)).

[Poor guy](#embed:https://www.youtube.com/live/aP7OWMQ18ao?t=9345)

Still, chaos and teasing aside, the three of them managed to conquer the fiery depths of the Volcano Dungeon, though not without plenty of screaming, burning, and [general suffering.](https://www.youtube.com/live/aP7OWMQ18ao?t=12197s) And in doing so, recovered the memories of [Takanashi Kiara.](https://www.youtube.com/live/aP7OWMQ18ao?t=12605)

Only then did things finally begin to settle. According to the King, there was just one dungeon left. And somehow, someway, Jyon couldn’t shake the feeling that the last one would have something to do with [her.](https://www.youtube.com/live/aP7OWMQ18ao?t=13407)

Even so, after a full day of drifting wherever the wind took her, she finally landed on something she wanted to do.

She would build a bathhouse for the masses to use.

Because apparently... they all stink.
