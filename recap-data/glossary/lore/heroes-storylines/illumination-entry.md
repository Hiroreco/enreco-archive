## Overview
**Chapter:** 3
**Duration:** Day 1 to 8

After witnessing the passing of her wife on the first day in Chapter 3, Roa started spiraling down into madness as she uncovered more and more secrets of the kingdom they were all dwevlling in, founding a cult called the Illumination and dragging others into her beliefs.

## Details
### How it started
On day 1 of the thirdr chapter, everything was going fine just as usual, birds were singing, flowers were blooming, and Roa was enjoying her new life with her beloved, Shakira, in present Libestal. But in a morbid twist, after eating what was presumed the "Cake of Fertility", Shakira got poisoned and ultimately passed, right in front of Roa's eyes. Grieving for the lost of her wife, Roa swore she would seek justice and find the culprit behind the murder.

Though in the pursuit of such, while exploring arorund the kingdom, Roa accidentally stubmled upon lands and sights that she weren't supposed to see, which sow the seed of the idea that the kingdom was a simulation and Roa had broken out of it. This revelation broke Roa's view of the world and she needed others to know as well.

### How it went
**The Spiral Into Madness**
As Roa explored more and more, she found more and more "exits" and "disfigured lands", fueling more proof to what she believed. Anytime she would go too deep into these lands, she would suddenly be transported back into the kingdom, and Roa believed it was the gods stopping her from finding the truth. To further fuel it, throughout all of this, she noticed how there was always a sheep in these lands, as if it's watching her, judging her, to the point where paranoia started to kick in, and the meer sight of the creature was enough to drive Roa into fear and break down.

Day by day, Roa's mental state became more and more unhigned, others could tell she wasn't acting like her usual self, but despite what would usually be burshed off as ridiculous claims, Roa's had proofs behind her babbling. The flat lands, the out of place wall of glass, the giant gap between lands, Roa showed them all, and theo ones that agree with what Roa believed, she deemed them as "enlightened". 

On day 4, Roa founded the Illumination, the group (or cult) wherre she recruited those who had been enlightned to lead them all into finding the truth about the world. She made a custom poster herself to represent the group, and started plasting the poster everywhere, in houses, around the kingdom, the Illumination was all that she could think about. On day 5 things became more haunting as there were structures representing a sheep randomly popped up around the kingdom as well.

**The Haunting of Her Loved One**
In adjacent to this, Roa was still suffering from the loss of her wife, and although there was a funeral held by Shiori on day 3 to help Roa finally get a peace of mind, Roa heself could not attend it.

It wasn't until around day 4 where this grief would come back to haunt her, literally. While wondering around the kingdom, Roa suddenly saw Shakira's lifeless corpse following her, whispering to her in a demonic tone, causing the pink cat to always dash away in fright and tears. This would happen frequently, until it reached its climax where on day 5, the corpse, for some reason along with Shiori and Nerissa, corneered her and pushed her down a hole in the kingdom's church, where a ritual would take place.

It was here where the corpse, after manipulating Roa into believing it was actually her wife coming back for Roa, was actually Bijou pupetteering the corpse, and Bijou herself was the one that killed Shakira, despite Roa having warmed up and placed so much trust in the gem princess.

Roa was then forced to read cryptic scriptures, and made a sacrifice for the twisted sister's ritual.

### How it ended
At the beginning of the last day, Roa for some reason had forgotten about everything up till now. The Illumination, her love and grief for Shakira, and she acted like her madness had gone away. It wasn't until the otherrs snapped her out of it did she regain her memories.

In the end, Roa never found out due to the limited time this world allowed them, Roa never found out the truth she wanted of this realm, though at the very least, she found closure for wife, as a trial was held and with it, Shakira's corpse was cremated, leaving the widowed finally finding peace.
