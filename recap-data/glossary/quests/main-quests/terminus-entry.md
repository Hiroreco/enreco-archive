## Overview

The Prison at the End of Infinity, also known as **Terminus**, was a realm introduced in Chapter 3 where [Faust](#entry:outsider-entry) resided. It served as the setting for many of the chapter's major quests, as its countless prison cells provided access to important locations, including the labyrinths that held Advent's lost memories.

## Details

### Appearance

Terminus was an immense and foreboding prison suspended beyond the boundaries of ordinary reality. At its center lay a vast, empty courtyard surrounded by towering walls of blackstone, while rows upon rows of prison cells stretched endlessly along its perimeter.

The atmosphere was cold, grim, and unsettling. Most of the prison was dimly lit, with many cells stood abandoned, though others were far from empty. Some had been uniquely [decorated](#entry:prison-design-entry) to reflect the identities of their occupants, while others housed mysterious individuals whose knowledge or actions were vital to advancing the story.

Despite its oppressive appearance, Terminus acted as a hub for the chapter's progression. Through a dimensional rift located within the realm, heroes could freely travel between the prison and the Kingdom of Libestal.

### Labyrinths

"Labyrinths" were the dungeons of this chapter. Each one could only be accessed through a designated prison cell and was themed around a member—or members—of Advent. There were four in total:

- [Labyrinth of Knowledge](#entry:labyrinth-of-knowledge-entry) – Unlocked on Day 2; accessible through Cell 0502; contained Shiori Novella's memories.
- [Labyrinth of Emotion](#entry:labyrinth-of-emotion-entry) – Unlocked on Day 3; accessible through Cell 0414; contained Koseki Bijou's memories.
- [Labyrinth of Song](#entry:labyrinth-of-knowledge-entry) – Unlocked on Day 4; accessible through Cell 1121; contained Nerissa Ravencroft's memories.
- [Labyrinth of Duality](#entry:labyrinth-of-duality-entry) – Unlocked on Day 5; accessible through Cells 0201–0202; contained Fuwawa Abyssguard and Mococo Abyssguard's memories.
