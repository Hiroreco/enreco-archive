## Overview

**Day Unlocked:** 6

The **Rite of Purification** was a special trial that took place before the heroes could begin their final mission into Nullspace. Its purpose was to purify the members of Advent and break the Curse that had driven a wedge between them, allowing them to reunite before the chapter's conclusion.

## Details

The Rite of Purification took place within the Kingdom of Libestal's castle and functioned as a large-scale game of tag between the heroes and Advent. With Advent consisting of five members and the remaining heroes numbering ten, the trial effectively became a 10-versus-5 chase across the castle grounds.

The objective was simple: capture all members of Advent before time ran out. If even a single member remained uncaptured by the end of the round, the ritual would fail and the heroes would have to attempt it again.

The rules of the rite were as follows:

- Each round lasted 15 minutes.
- To complete the ritual, all five members of Advent had to be captured within the time limit.
- Advent members were considered captured after being struck enough times with the wooden swords carried by the pursuing heroes.
- Advent members were slightly smaller in size, possessed increased movement speed, and had access to various special abilities that aided in evasion.
- Advent members had to periodically use fireworks and bells that gave away their position, in order to reduce their alarm meter. If their alarm meter filled up, they would emit fireworks continuously for a while.
- There were teleportation pads located at certain spots throughout the castle.

The rite led to numerous pursuits as Advent used their enhanced mobility and abilities to evade capture while the heroes coordinated their efforts to corner them. After two rounds of chasing, all five members of Advent were eventually purified, allowing them to reunite and opening the path to the final journey into Nullspace.
