## Overview

**Day Unlocked:** 3

The Labyrinth of Emotion was the second dungeon unlocked in Chapter 3. The labyrinth was themed around Koseki Bijou and contained the memories of the Gem of Emotion herself.

## Details

### Appearance

The Labyrinth of Emotion took the form of a magnificent gemstone castle hidden within a vast and tranquil expanse of darkness. White crystal walls rose high into the air, adorned with colorful gem fragments that shimmered with reflected light. Surrounding the castle were towering crystal spires that pierced the void like enormous gemstones emerging from the ground.

Despite its beauty, the labyrinth carried an almost dreamlike stillness. The glittering crystals illuminated the darkness around them, causing the entire structure to sparkle as though it were a precious jewel suspended in space.

### Challenges

**Crystal Puzzles**

Most of the labyrinth's challenges took place within the gemstone castle itself. The puzzles revolved around manipulating crystals, light, and patterns to unlock the path forward.

The first challenge required players to rearrange a series of crystals so that their order matched the subtle patterns hidden on the surrounding walls. This was followed by the Circle of Lights puzzle, where lights embedded in the floor had to be aligned with those on the ceiling and adjusted to match the correct configuration.

Next came the Hanging Lights puzzle, which required players to alter the heights of suspended lights so that various blocks aligned properly. A second crystal-ordering puzzle awaited afterward, functioning similarly to the first but with a randomized arrangement.

Eventually, players reached a chamber where a wall of multicolored crystals blocked the way forward. To clear the obstacle, they had to enter four different Rooms of Emotion and destroy the crystals hidden within each one.

- **Room of Melancholy** – Players helped solve the problems of several Stains, cheering them up and gaining access to the blue crystal.
- **Room of Joy** – Players collected food, jewels, and other goodies scattered throughout the room. As their joy meter increased, so did their jump height, eventually allowing them to reach and destroy the yellow crystal.
- **Room of Wrath** – Striking the red crystal caused waves of Stains to appear. After defeating them, the crystal became vulnerable and could be broken.
- **Room of Fun** – Players recreated paintings using paintballs. Completing the challenge twice revealed the green crystal.

Once all four emotion crystals had been destroyed, the multicolored barrier disappeared and the path to the final boss was opened.

### Final Boss – Prisoner of Emotion

With the castle's challenges completed, players could finally confront the Prisoner of Emotion.

Upon defeat, the boss transformed into a white crystal. Destroying this crystal removed the white crystal barriers blocking a secret room located in an earlier section of the labyrinth. The secret room contained many Moai statues and Bijou's music vinyl.
