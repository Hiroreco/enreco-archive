## Overview

**Day Unlocked:** 6

Nullspace was the final destination of Chapter 3's journey and the place where the heroes ultimately found the Outsider. What they expected to be the conclusion of their quest instead became the beginning of an even greater dilemma, as the mysterious figure presented them with a choice that would determine the fate of countless worlds.

## Details

### Appearance

Unlike the major locations encountered throughout the chapter, Nullspace did not possess a conventional exterior. True to its name, it existed as a vast, isolated space detached from the worlds beyond it.

Rather than following a single theme, Nullspace was divided into multiple sections, each recreating locations and challenges the heroes had previously conquered. As they progressed deeper into the realm, they found themselves revisiting familiar trials from earlier in their journey.

At the very center of Nullspace lay its core, where the Egg of Creation awaited. This area bore a striking resemblance to [the Sanctuary](#entry:sanctuary-entry), the place where the heroes first arrived at the beginning of Chapter 1. The familiar sight created a sense of coming full circle, as though their entire journey had led them back to where it all began.

### Challenges

**Challenges of the Past**

After entering Nullspace through the Looking-Glass Shard, the heroes' objective was to reach its core. To do so, they had to overcome a series of challenges drawn from their past adventures.

Many of these trials were recreations of obstacles first encountered during Chapter 2, including challenges from locations such as Star Site: Elpis and Star Site: Chronos. By conquering these familiar tests once more, the heroes gradually advanced deeper into the realm.

**Battle Against the Order**

Before they could proceed any further, the heroes were forced to confront the guardian of Nullspace: the Order.

The battle proved to be an arduous one, demanding the combined efforts of everyone present. After a hard-fought struggle, the heroes emerged victorious and secured passage to the core of the realm.

**The Egg of Creation**

At the center of Nullspace, the heroes once again met Faust. There, the mysterious figure finally revealed his true identity as the Outsider.

The Outsider unveiled the Egg of Creation, an artifact capable of creating, or recreating, countless worlds across the myriad realms. He explained that he had guided the heroes toward this moment so that he could harness the power of their Revelations and awaken the Egg.

With its power, he intended to erase the existing worlds and remake reality anew, free from the suffering, cycles, and fate that had governed existence until now. He then asked the heroes to join him and lend him their strength to make this vision a reality.

For more details regarding the Outsider's plan and the events that followed, see the [Outsider](#entry:outsider-entry) and [Kingdom of Libestal (Present)](#entry:libestal-present-entry) entries.
