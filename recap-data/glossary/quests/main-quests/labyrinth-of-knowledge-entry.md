## Overview

**Day Unlocked:** 2

The Labyrinth of Knowledge was the first dungeon unlocked in Chapter 3. The labyrinth was themed around Shiori Novella and contained fragments of her lost memories.

## Details

### Appearance

The labyrinth was divided into several distinct sections, each with its own unique theme, atmosphere, and challenges. Progressing through the dungeon felt like flipping through the pages of a book, with every area resembling a different chapter in a larger story.

One moment, adventurers would find themselves traversing a lush jungle filled with ancient ruins and towering structures. The next, they would emerge into a frozen landscape dominated by a massive ice castle. Despite belonging to the same labyrinth, each section felt like an entirely different world connected together through an elaborate tale.

### Challenges

**Jungle Area**

The first section of the labyrinth took place within a towering structure hidden deep in the jungle. The area focused heavily on platforming challenges, requiring players to make use of several different types of jump pads.

Some jump pads launched players high into the air, while others allowed them to float slowly back down. Certain pads even cause players to float against the ceiling, creating unique traversal challenges.

A secret bookmark could be obtained by discovering a hidden opening in one of the walls. By jumping through the gap and pressing a concealed button, players could unlock a chamber in the ceiling and enhance a nearby jump pad, allowing them to reach the secret reward.

**Snow Area**

The second major section led players into a frozen castle filled with a variety of puzzles.

The first challenge required players to trace beams of light between colored blocks and use the resulting sequence to unlock the next puzzle. Afterward came a rotating bridge puzzle, where the bridges had to be repositioned to create a path forward.

The next room featured a pattern-matching challenge. Players were shown a design and then directed toward a blank wall, where they had to recreate the pattern correctly.

Following this was the marching statue puzzle, which required players to set movement directions for a statue and guide it safely to its destination. Once all challenges were completed, the Icebound Core could be destroyed, melting the ice that blocked access to the boss arena.

A second secret bookmark could be obtained after melting the ice. Players had to guide the marching statue out of its original room, across the rotating bridges, and into a separate goal area.

**Final Boss – Prisoner of Knowledge**

At the end of the labyrinth awaited the Prisoner of Knowledge. The battle took place upon a dark and gloomy platform suspended above a vast sea of lava.

The encounter consisted of two phases. Defeating the first phase rewarded players with the final bookmark. If all three bookmarks had been collected throughout the dungeon, a chest containing Shiori's music disc would appear inside the church-like structure found at the end of the labyrinth.

After overcoming the second phase, players were transported to a peaceful area containing the church and a mysterious book. Reading the book revealed a final twist: the heroes had been inside a book all along, unknowingly acting out the events of a story.
