## Overview

The NPC and Prison Cell Design Ideas event was a community contest that ran from `March 15, 2026, to April 15, 2026 (PDT).` During the event, fans were invited to design a prison cell and create a prisoner NPC complete with custom dialogue. Selected submissions would have the chance to be incorporated into the world featured in Chapter 3.

## Details

To participate, fans were provided with a prison cell template (Minecraft: Java Edition version 1.21.1) and were tasked with decorating it within the given boundaries. All builds had to be created using approved building materials and follow the rules outlined.

In addition to designing the cell itself, participants could create a unique prisoner NPC to inhabit it, complete with their own dialogue.

Many of the submitted designs were later incorporated into Chapter 3 and could be found throughout the prison realm [Terminus](#entry:terminus-entry), where heroes encountered the custom-built cells and their inhabitants while exploring the facility.
